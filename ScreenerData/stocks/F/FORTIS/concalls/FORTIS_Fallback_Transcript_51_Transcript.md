### 👤 About Fortis Healthcare Limited
Fortis Healthcare Limited is a leading integrated healthcare delivery service provider in India. The healthcare verticals of the company primarily comprise hospitals, diagnostics, and day care specialty facilities. Currently, the company operates 29 healthcare facilities (including JVs and O&M facilities). The Company’s network comprises approximately 4,500 operational beds (including O&M) and ~410 diagnostics centres.
