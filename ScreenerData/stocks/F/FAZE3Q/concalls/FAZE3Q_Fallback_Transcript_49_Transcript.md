### 👤 Cotton
Floor coverings (Bathmats, Rugs, Outdoor, performance textiles) being predominantly polyester based and technical in nature were largely exported out of China until 2018/2019 & estimated exports are said to be at least 20 times of India Post 2019, Tariffs imposed on China textiles exports, rising labour & power costs, pollution crackdowns, diminishing incentives, etc made the Chinese exports uncompetitive. Chinese Yuan has appreciated over 12% to 6.37 from 7+ in 2020 affecting Chinese exports In 2020/21 owing to COVID Pandemic, supply chain disruptions & strong momentum towards “China Plus One” has led to demand shift from Top Organised Retailers across the Globe towards India, being a natural ally & having reliably delivered over the years Owing to Domestic challenges & demand scenario, many known contemporary suppliers out of China in range of USD 150 Mln - 250 Mln annual revenue have downsized / diversified out of business Customer preference across USA, UK & EUR has tangibly shifted to “other than Made in China” as demonstrated from surveys / trends Incumbent suppliers in India have a huge demand tailwind from above factors. Effective expeditious execution by brownfield / green field expansion is the key to tap demand momentum Other Supplier countries likes Turkey, Egypt, Portugal have also faced challenges leading to customer preference towards India
