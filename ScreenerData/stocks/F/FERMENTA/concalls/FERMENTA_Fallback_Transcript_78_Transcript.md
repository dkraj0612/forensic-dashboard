### 👤 Vitamin D3
Enjoying Sunshine Vitamin D3 API demand for human is fairly stable and prices as well. However, it’s demand for animal feed is volatile causing its prices to go up substantially in the past couple of years Fermenta Biotech is among the lowest cost producers of D3 in the world implying longer staying power and better margin Boost to Vitamin D3 human nutrition demand is driven by awareness and research on its great preventive and curative benefits beyond fortifying bones; it’s a versatile vitamin! Spike in feed prices due to the following trifecta 1. Rising demand 2. No major new capacity additions 3. Plant shutdowns in China due to stricter environment policies Vitamin D3 is enjoying great sunshine for the past few years With Chinese cost of production and compliance rising across the sectors including Vitamin D3, its prices for animal feed are unlikely to revert to earlier bottom

### 👤 Nutraceuticals
