### 👤 Q4 FY19 RESULTS
Q4FY19 vs Q4FY18 - Business Performance Highlights Revenue - Revenue registered a y-o-y growth of 19.2% to Rs. 9,642 mn

### 👤 Volume
PVC Resin volume registered a y-o-y increase of 6.1% to 76,549 MT. Pipe and Fittings volume registered a y-o-y growth of 12.6% to 80,771 MT.

### 👤 Operating Performance (EBIT)
EBIT margins at 13.4% as compared to 21.3% (7.9% decrease). PVC Resin EBIT margin at 14.5% as compared to 24.1% (9.6% decrease). PVC Pipes and Fittings EBIT margin at 7.7% as compared to 9.2% (1.5% decrease).

### 👤 PAT
PAT was lower by 24.4% to Rs. 914 mn as against Rs 1,209 mn in Q4FY18. High EDC price and sliding PVC price has resulted in lower PVC/EDC delta which has reduced the margin in PVC segment on y-o-y basis. The PVC price volatility and delayed demand pick up has also affected the Pipes and Fittings margin.
