### 👤 Q3 FY20 RESULTS
Q3FY20 vs Q3FY19 - Business Performance Highlights Revenue Revenue registered a y-o-y decline of 7.6% to Rs. 6,994 mn

### 👤 Volume
PVC Resin volume registered a y-o-y decline of 11.5% to 59,154 MT. Pipe and Fittings volume registered a y-o-y decline of 10.8% to 52,815 MT.

### 👤 Operating Performance (EBIT)
EBIT margins at 17.1% as compared to 15.8%. PVC Resin EBIT margin at 20.1% as compared to 16.7%. PVC Pipes and Fittings EBIT margin at 7.9% as compared to 7.4%.

### 👤 PAT
PAT higher by 18.5% at Rs.933 mn as against Rs 787 mn in Q3FY19. Extended monsoon season with cyclones thereafter has impacted the volumes in both the segments. In spite of lower volumes, operating margins are better on account of better realizations and higher PVC EDC Delta. Highest ever PBT (Rs 1,246 Mn) and PAT (Rs 933 Mn) realized in third Quarter of any year.
