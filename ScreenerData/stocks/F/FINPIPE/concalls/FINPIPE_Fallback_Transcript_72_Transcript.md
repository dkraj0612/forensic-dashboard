### 👤 ABOUT FINOLEX INDUSTRIES LIMITED
Finolex Industries Limited (FIL) is India’s leading manufacturer of PVC Pipes & Fittings and the only PVC pipes and fittings company with backward integration. FIL is headquartered in Pune and operates through its state of the art manufacturing plants located in Pune & Ratnagiri in Maharashtra and Masar in Gujarat. FIL is the first Indian PVC Pipes manufacturer to be awarded the IS/ISO 9001:2008 certification. With its network of over 17,000 direct and indirect retail outlets spread across India, FIL’s Pipes and Fittings are easily accessible throughout the country. Carrying water from the source to the destination, FIL has been helping millions across the country to create wealth. Today, with water being a limited resource, the company's vision is to help every consumer find a more effective way of managing water by creating products across all touch points to manage water better.
