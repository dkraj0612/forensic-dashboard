### 👤 Finolex Industries Limited (NSE
FINPIPE | BSE:500940), India’s leading manufacturer of PVC Pipes & Fittings, at its Board Meeting held today announced unaudited financial results for the second quarter ended September 30, 2017. The sales volume for PVC Pipes & Fittings was at 47,426 MT in Q2FY18 up by 23.9% against 38,266 MT in Q2FY17. Total income from operations excluding excise duty and taxes was at INR 4,753 mn for Q2FY18 up by 4.0% against INR 4,568 mn in Q1FY17. EBITDA stood at INR 496 mn for Q2FY18 down 43.0% against INR 870 mn for Q2FY17. Profit after tax was at INR 283 mn for Q2FY18 down 44.8% against INR 513 mn for Q2FY17.
