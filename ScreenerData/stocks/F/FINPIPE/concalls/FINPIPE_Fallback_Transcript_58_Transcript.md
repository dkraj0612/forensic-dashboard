### 👤  Trends in Quarterly EBIT Margin – Overall and Key Segments
Total income from operations was at INR 9,438 mn for Q1FY20 up 14.0% against INR 8,279 mn in Q1FY19. EBITDA stood at INR 1,236 mn for Q1FY20 lower by 36.3 % against INR 1,939 mn for Q1FY19. Profit after tax was at INR 725mn for Q1FY20 lower by 29.8% against INR 1,033 mn for Q1FY19.
