Revenue of INR 3963 million was mainly driven by the growth in domestic market. However, it was impacted by slowdown in US and European markets, reducing the export share to 9% of the total revenue but growth in domestic market has more than compensated the reduction in exports. EBITDA was INR 454 million versus INR 352 million of corresponding quarter of previous year. As a percent of Revenue, it was 11.3% Vs 11.0% in corresponding quarter of previous year. The current quarter was highly impacted by steep rise in commodity and global supply chain disruption resulting in significant increase in premium freight.
