Revenue EBITDA • Founded in 1998 by the Meghe group, Ceinsys has over the years focused on offering technology solutions across various domains and established itself as a premier Geospatial & Engineering solution provider in the ITES space. • The Geospatial engineering services and Enterprise solutions offerings encompass various aspects of geospatial intelligence, including Data Creation, Data Analytics, Decision Support Systems (DSS), Enterprise Web Solutions, and Dashboards. • Looking at the opportunity of convergence of GIS and autonomous driving technologies in the mobility industry, Ceinsys took a strategic decision to foray into the mobility services space via its acquisition of a specialized engineering service provider -
