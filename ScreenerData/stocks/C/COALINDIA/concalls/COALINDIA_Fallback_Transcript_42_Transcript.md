### 👤 Environmental Highlights
CIL undertook extensive plantation along avenues, on OB dumps, around mines & residential colonies to maintain the ecological balance in & around its operations. MCL & NCL have developed Miyawaki forest in their command area covering 4.5 Ha and 2.5 Ha land respectively in FY 2022-23. This technique involves planting dozens of native species in the same area which results in 30 times denser than usual, 10-time quicker growth. During FY2022-23 , three (03) no. of Eco Parks have already been developed. During H1 of 2023- 24, 02 no. of eco parks have been developed. CIL has always endeavored to create an ecological balance in and around its operations. SECL transformed its abandoned quarry no. 6 of its Bishrampur open cast mine into a pisciculture and water sports centre. It has not only created livelihood for local people from pisciculture, boating and running of the restaurant but also enriched the ecology and surrounding biodiversity.
