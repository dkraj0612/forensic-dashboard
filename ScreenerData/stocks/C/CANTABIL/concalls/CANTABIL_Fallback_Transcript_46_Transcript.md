“I am happy to share that we have registered revenue of Rs. 163 crore in Q3 FY23 with EBITDA and PAT of Rs. 56 crore & Rs. 27 crore respectively. For the nine month period, revenues stood at Rs. 380 crore with EBITDA and PAT of Rs. 123 crore and Rs. 50 crore. Our revenues from same stores has played an instrumental role in delivering the desired growth in financial performance. Apart from this, addition of new stores has also significantly contributed towards the superior performance and provides an expanding platform to support our strategic growth objectives.
