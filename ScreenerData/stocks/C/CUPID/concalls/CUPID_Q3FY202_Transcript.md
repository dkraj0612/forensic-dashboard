### 👤 Cupid Limited @ Glance
Cupid Limited is a diversified healthcare and consumer products company with a strong global B2B export presence and a rapidly expanding FMCG business. The company is one of the leading supplier of male and female condoms, personal lubricants, and IVD kits, exporting to over 125 countries and working closely with global institutions such as WHO/UNFPA, global funds, MSI, PSI & IDA Foundation. With disciplined execution and a clear growth strategy, Cupid Limited is evolving into a balanced, globally relevant healthcare and FMCG platform. The export business remains the company’s core strength, supported by multi-year international programs, global certifications, and a proven execution track record. WHO/UNFPA prequalification for both male and female condoms, along with key regulatory approvals, positions Cupid among a select group of globally trusted suppliers. In parallel, Cupid is scaling a branded FMCG platform across personal care and wellness categories, building domestic distribution while gradually expanding its presence in international markets. This dual B2B-B2C model provides stability, margin upside, and long-term growth visibility.
