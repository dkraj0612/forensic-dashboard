Net profit for Q3FY19 up by 152% Y.o.Y to Rs.318 Cr. (Q3FY18-Rs.126 Cr). Gross profit stood at Rs.2357 Cr in Q3 FY19 (Q3 FY18 -2831 Cr.) Net Interest Income increased by 3.7% to Rs.3814 Cr (Q3FY18 -Rs.3679 Cr) Aided by healthy growth in interest on advances (25.18% Y.o.Y) and Interest on investments (9.84% Y.o.Y) Net Int. Income excl. Int. on income tax up by 14.45% y.o.y at Rs. 3366 cr. Non-Int. Income during Q3 FY19 was at Rs.1325 Cr; (Q3 FY18 - Rs.1566 Cr.) Non-Int. Income excluding trading profit increased by 6.29% Y.o.Y.
