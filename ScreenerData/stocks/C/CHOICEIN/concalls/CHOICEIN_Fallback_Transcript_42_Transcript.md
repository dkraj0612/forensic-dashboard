We are proud to end another year. delivering exceptional performance. We focus on getting more orders from the government to execute projects on the ground level. This quarter was all about expansion. We have successfully opened 14 new branches of our NBFC arm. To strengthen our presence in North India, we plan to launch more branches in North India to expand our operations. Choice is moving ahead with full vigor and we aim to be the top financial services firm in the country in the coming years. Our focus will always remain on client satisfaction and we will continue to innovate that will simplify the financial journey of every customer. All our efforts are aligned towards value creation for our shareholders and we will continue to do the same every year.
