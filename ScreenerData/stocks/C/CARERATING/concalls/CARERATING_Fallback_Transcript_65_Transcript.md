### 👤 Rating Movement
There has been a moderation in credit quality of entities rated by CARE Ratings in 2018‐19. The credit quality as measured by CARE Ratings ‘modified credit ratio’ (MCR), for 2018‐19 came in at 0.95 compared with the ratio of 1.08 in 2017‐18. The softening in overall credit quality in 2018‐19 can partly be ascribed to the small enterprises (total revenues less than Rs.100 crs), which have seen a decline in credit quality, while the large and medium enterprises have been steering the overall stability in credit quality. The stability in rating was noted across sectors with the majority of sectors having a higher proportion of reaffirmation of credit ratings in 2018‐19.

### 👤 CARE Ratings Debt Quality Index (CDQI)
