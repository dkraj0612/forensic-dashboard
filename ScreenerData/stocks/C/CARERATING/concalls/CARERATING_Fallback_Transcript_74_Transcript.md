### 👤 CARE Ratings Debt Quality Index
The CDQI had shown a continuous decline from May’15 (when it was first announced by CARE) to Dec’15. After improving marginally in Jan’16 and Feb’16, the index declined considerably in Mar’16, and further marginally in Apr’16. The index has shown stability, though with an upward bias in the subsequent months up to Aug’16. It further declined in Sept’16 and Oct’16. The decline continued in the month of Dec’16.
