As a result, publicity spending in Q4FY24 accounts for INR 15.4 crore versus INR 24 crore in Q4FY23. This has positively impacted EBITDA margins by 1.7%. In FY24, the Company continued its strategy of investing more in publicity with total spending reaching to INR 63 crore as compared to INR 57 crore in FY23.
