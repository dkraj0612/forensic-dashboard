Another delicate aspect is the ratio of in-house manufacturing and outsourcing, as neither full outsourcing nor fully in-house manufacturing has worked for any player striving to be competitive. At Cera we have been able to balance the products made in-house, and those that need to be outsourced along capabilities. We believe our facilities are best used for complex products such as wall-hung WCs, table top basins and one piece floor-standing WC’s.
