### 👤 About the Company
• In 2006, Camlin Fine Sciences Limited (CFS) was formed after de-merging (mirror shareholding) the fine chemical business from Camlin Ltd. • The Company has over 30 years of experience providing innovative solutions with a competitive advantage in fine sciences. • CFS is a vertically integrated company, engaged in research, development, manufacturing, commercializing and marketing of specialty chemicals and blends, which are used in a wide array of sectors. Food, feed, animal and pet nutrition

### 👤 Bio Diesel
