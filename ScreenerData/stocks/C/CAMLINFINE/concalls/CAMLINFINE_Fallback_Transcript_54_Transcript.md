### 👤 Structural & Other Impacts
Unprecedented increase in logistic cost during the quarter which could not be passed on immediately - recovered with lag of a quarter - mitigation by way of changing terms to FOB from October 2021 wherever possible Inflationary trend continues in crude impacting the raw material costs Increase in employee costs of INR 1.80 Cr - yearly increment & induction of managing director & sales personnel and new business heads in the current quarter - benefits of new inductees will start fructifying in subsequent quarters Gross Margins remained stable QoQ, however shy of expected 50%+ margin as increase in selling prices were undone by inflationary conditions and a lag of quarter to pass to the customer
