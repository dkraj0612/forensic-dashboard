### 👤 NSE
CYBERTECH), a CMMI Level 3 next-generation Enterprise Cloud Transformations provider, announced today its financial results for the first quarter ended on 30th June 2024. Commenting on the results, Mr. Vish Tadimety, CEO and Founder, CyberTech, stated: “I am delighted to share that we have had a strong start to the new financial year, FY25. Our revenue grew by 7% year-over-year to approximately ₹622 million, and our net profit (PAT) surged by 38% to ₹73.6 million. This quarter's performance, amidst a global atmosphere of caution, underscores the strength of our pipeline and our ability to sustain growth while maintaining robust margins. A key driver of our success has been our strategic partnership with Esri. Our Managed ArcGIS Cloud Services (MACS) continue to stand out. Known for technical excellence and a dedicated approach to fulfilling our clients' cloud requirements, we take great pride in our services. I'm delighted to report that we've welcomed several Utility customers to our growing MACS clientele. Our SAP strategic alliance has also significantly contributed to our business, particularly with SAP S/4HANA Cloud migrations. As more enterprises accelerate their move to the cloud, SAP remains at the forefront of our focus. I am pleased to share that several large enterprises have chosen us as their trusted partner for their SAP S/4HANA Cloud migration journeys. GeoShield product remains a major focus. It has successfully achieved a strong product-market fit with its features, and we are in advanced discussions with several US Law Enforcement Agencies to further expand our presence. Our associates are the backbone of the Company. We remain committed to attracting and nurturing new-age technical talent. Operationally, our current focus is on building a robust demand generation and sales mechanism in both India and the US. To this end, we have strategically decided to strengthen our sales teams. In conclusion, I am proud of our achievements and the positive trajectory we are on. We remain dedicated to driving innovation and delivering value for our stakeholders. I extend my heartfelt thanks to all our clients, fellow shareholders, and CyberTech colleagues for their unwavering support
