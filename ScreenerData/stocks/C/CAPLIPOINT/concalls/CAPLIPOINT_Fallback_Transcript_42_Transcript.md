Caplin Point Laboratories Limited is a fast-growing pharmaceutical company with a unique business model catering predominantly to emerging markets of Latin America and Africa. Caplin Point is one of the few companies to show consistent high-quality growth in Revenues, Profits and Cash flow over the last 15 years. The Company has state of the art manufacturing facilities that cater to a complete range of finished dosage forms.
