I am pleased to announce that Acrysil UK Limited which is a wholly owned subsidiary of Acrysil limited has agreed to acquire 100% shares of Tickford Orange Limited, UK along with its wholly owned operating subsidiary Sylmar Technology Limited., UK is one of the major export markets of our company. I am talking about the Tickford Orange Limited it is a parent company of its wholly owned subsidiary Sylmar Technology Limited (STL) which is a manufacturer and distributor of customizer of high-quality solid surface products for kitchen and bathroom segment. These products are used for both domestic and commercial purposes catering to the markets across United Kingdom.
