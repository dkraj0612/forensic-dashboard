Sales volume for quartz kitchen sinks stood at 170,035 units, stainless steel sinks at 28,608 units, kitchen appliances and other stood at 12,192 units in Q1 FY2023. EBITDA margins for the Q1 FY23 stood at 20.1% as compared to 23.7% in corresponding Q1 FY2022.
