### 👤 New Product Launches / Important Developments
Ddev Plastiks Industries has launched their first locally produced WTR XLPE (Water Tree Retardant XLPE) for the insulation of cables up to 72 KV This insulation material is highly effective in reducing the growth of electrical treeing caused by water, making it a superior choice for insulation purposes Reduced treeing result in better service life of the cable resulting into better returns for electrical distribution companies This product was primarily imported, however, DPIL has introduced an exceptional version that has successfully passed a long-term test at a third-party laboratory It has successfully passed a two-year test on cable at a prestigious VDE laboratory • A global leader in XLPE compound manufacturing, producing EHV compounds since 1980 • Commercially launched for major applications in Solar / Photovoltaic
