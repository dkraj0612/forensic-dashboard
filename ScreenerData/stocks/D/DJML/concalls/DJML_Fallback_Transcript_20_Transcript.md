### 👤 OVERVIEW
Since its incorporation in 2009, DJ Mediaprint & Logistics Limited (DJML) has evolved into a pioneering force in the printing and logistics, delivering a fully integrated suite of services designed to meet the diverse operational needs of clients across India and overseas. Building on its core expertise in commercial and security printing - including MICR cheque books, identity cards, OMR sheets, and high-volume variable data printing - DJML also provides comprehensive courier and postal services, covering Speed Post, Registered Post, Bill Mail, Direct Post, and specialized bulk mailing solutions. Beyond its core logistics capabilities, the company offers advanced Records Management and Document Management services, supported by over 4,00,000 sq. ft. of secure, ISO-certified facilities equipped with state-of-the-art RMS/DMS software, 24/7 security, fire safety systems, and restricted access protocols. Its portfolio extends to scanning and digitization, graphic design, bulk email/SMS/WhatsApp campaigns, newspaper advertising, software solutions, and manpower supply for corporate, hospitality, valet, and security staffing needs. DJML’s end- to-end service model ensures clients benefit from a one-stop solution that streamlines workflows, reduces operational complexity, and enhances customer engagement. Serving a prestigious clientele that spans corporates, government and semi-government bodies, banking, financial services, insurance, healthcare, airlines, shipping, logistics, education, manufacturing, and retail sectors, DJML has built long-standing partnerships by adapting to evolving business needs, investing in cutting-edge technology, and maintaining an unwavering focus on quality, compliance, and timely delivery. With its integrated infrastructure, strong governance, and proven track record of operational excellence, the company is uniquely positioned to continue driving value for stakeholders and leading the industry in service innovation.
