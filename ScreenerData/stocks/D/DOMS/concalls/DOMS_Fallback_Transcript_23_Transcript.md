The EBITDA for Q4 FY '24 grew by 22.6% to INR 759.3 million as compared to INR 619.3 million in Q4 FY '23 with a margin expansion of 40 basis points over the same period last year and stood at 18.8% as compared to 18.4% in Q4 FY '23.
