### 👤 
The Company has put a team in place to evaluate the financial statement and any corollary impact of adopting Ind As 115, and is in process of developing an adoption plan. Management is assessing major revenue streams , and has progressed on accumulating, identifying and inventorying detailed information on major contracts that may be impacted by the changes at the transition date.
