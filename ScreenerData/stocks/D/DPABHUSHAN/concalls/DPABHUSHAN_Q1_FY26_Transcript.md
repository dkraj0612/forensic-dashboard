Thanks to our efficient inventory management practices, our dead inventory is nearly negligible around 1-2%, and even that is not truly dead. High-value items may take slightly longer to move, but regular products are expected to sell within 30 to 45 days. If they don’t, we will proactively shift them to another store where they may perform better.
