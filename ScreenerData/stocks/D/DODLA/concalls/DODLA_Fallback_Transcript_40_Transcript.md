### 👤 PROCUREMENT STAGE

### 👤 PROCESSINGS STAGE
At chilling centers and processing plants, the raw milk undergoes adulteration tests and neutralizer tests to detect contaminants FT-1 Milko-scanner at Hyderabad - enables conduct of 26 adulterant tests automatically Well-defined and documented quality system, monitored at various stages
