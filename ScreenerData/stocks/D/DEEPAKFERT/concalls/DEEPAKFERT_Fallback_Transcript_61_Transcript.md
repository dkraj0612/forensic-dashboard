### 👤 Explosive
▪Anhydrous ammonia is reacted with nitric acid (can be produced from ammonia) to produce ammonium nitrate ▪High density prill is made from 95-97% AN solution. The migration of moisture causes porosity and fragmentation which allow fuel oil to be absorbed up to a level of 5-6% to produce ANFO
