### 👤 Performance
FY26 has been a year of steady growth and disciplined execution for Denta Water and Infra Solutions. Our revenue grew by 23.17% YoY, while EBITDA and net profit increased by 15.32% YoY and 15.04% YoY respectively. The performance reflects our continued focus on operational efficiency, prudent project execution, and strong financial discipline, while reinforcing our commitment towards developing sustainable water infrastructure across India. During the year, we witnessed healthy momentum across irrigation, drinking water, and wastewater management projects, supported by the Government of India’s continued emphasis on water security, rural connectivity, and infrastructure development. The sector continues to present significant opportunities driven by rising investments and increased policy support. In line with our focus on timely project execution and uninterrupted delivery schedules, we increased inventory holdings during the year. While this led to higher inventory holding days and increased working capital requirements, it strengthens our ability to efficiently execute projects and maintain operational continuity. Further reinforcing the strength of our operational and financial profile, CARE Ratings reaffirmed the Company’s credit ratings at CARE BBB; Stable / CARE A3+, reflecting confidence in our execution capabilities and financial stability.”

We remain optimistic about the future and committed to contributing meaningfully towards the nation’s water security and sustainable development goals, while creating long-term value for all stakeholders.”
