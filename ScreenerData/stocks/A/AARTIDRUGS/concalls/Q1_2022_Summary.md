## Executive Overview

Aarti Drugs Limited (ADL) reported a resilient performance for Q1 FY2022 amidst prevailing headwinds, with consolidated revenue growing 6.6% year-on-year to ₹581.6 Crores. The revenue mix saw a slight shift towards domestic markets (69% vs. 31% exports). The API segment, contributing approximately 85% of consolidated revenue, experienced a 6.4% YoY growth, driven by a 13.2% volume increase, although acute therapy product demand remained subdued due to the COVID-19 second wave. Formulation revenue grew by 6.7% YoY to ₹86.5 Crores. The company successfully navigated increased raw material prices and supply chain disruptions by passing on partial cost increases and focusing on backward integration. While gross margins contracted due to input cost hikes outpacing price adjustments, management expressed confidence in a return to normal EBITDA margins (around 18%) in the coming quarters, with the worst of raw material price hikes behind them. The company incurred ₹48 Crores in capex during the quarter and reiterated its commitment to a significant ₹550 Crores capex plan over three years. A ₹50 Crores share buyback was successfully concluded.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **API Dominance & Diversification:** Continued focus on APIs, aiming to increase the share of lifestyle and chronic therapeutic areas while reducing reliance on acute therapies.
*   **Backward Integration:** Remains a cornerstone strategy to mitigate supply chain risks and improve cost efficiencies.
*   **Capacity Expansion:** Reiterated ₹550 Crores capex over three years, primarily for Greenfield and backward integration projects.
*   **Zero-Liquid Discharge (ZLD):** Four additional manufacturing locations converted to ZLD in Tarapur, with more planned, incurring marginal overheads expected to be offset by byproduct utilization and waste heat recovery.
*   **Cost Leadership & R&D:** Emphasis on process R&D, manufacturing efficiency, engineering skills, and economies of scale to maintain cost leadership.
*   **New Product Development:** Focus on anti-fungal, anti-diabetic, and antibiotic therapies for both API and formulations. Recent launch of a new antidiabetic product.
*   **Regulated Markets:** Continued focus on enhanced data integrity and automation for opportunities in regulated markets.
*   **Specialty Chemicals:** Identified as an attractive segment with wider end-use applications, with plans for significant capacity expansion, including a brownfield expansion to enter the market early.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

*   **Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance:**
    *   **Revenue Growth:** Achieved 6.6% consolidated revenue growth in Q1 FY22, demonstrating resilience. While below the historical robust growth, it was achieved amidst significant headwinds, including raw material price hikes and subdued acute therapy demand.
    *   **EBITDA Margins:** The company's target of "around 18% going forward" for EBITDA margins was not met in Q1 FY22 due to a significant squeeze in gross margins (attributed to input cost hikes outpacing price increases). The previous FY21 EBITDA margin of 20.5% was impacted by raw material price increases, which management believes are peaking. They anticipate margins to improve to normal levels (18%+) in the coming quarters.
    *   **Capacity Utilization & Expansion:** The Tarapur plant, which faced a shutdown in Q4 FY21, is now fully functional. The commitment to ZLD conversion continues. The overall capex plan of ₹550 Crores over three years remains intact.
    *   **Backward Integration:** Continues to be highlighted as a key strategy, contributing to resilience and cost management.
    *   **Zero-Liquid Discharge:** The proactive conversion of facilities to ZLD is proceeding as planned, with an acknowledgment of incremental operating costs.

*   **New Forward-Looking Promises, Guidance, or Timelines:**
    *   **EBITDA Margins (Forward):** Expected to return to normal levels, around 18% or more, within the next couple of quarters.
    *   **Gross Margin Improvement:** Expecting an improvement of around 4% in gross contribution over the next couple of quarters.
    *   **Raw Material Price Stabilization:** Expecting stabilization or a slight decrease in raw material prices by mid-Q2 FY22.
    *   **Metformin Phase 1 Facility Commissioning:** Expected in approximately 10-12 months, pushing its contribution towards FY2023.
    *   **API Volume Growth Target:** Targeting 10% to 15% volume growth for the API segment.
    *   **Overall Revenue Growth Target:** Targeting 10% to 15% growth for the overall topline in FY22 and FY23.
    *   **API + Specialty Chemicals Contribution:** Currently around 85% of consolidated business, expected to remain similar for overall growth projections.
    *   **Formulation Revenue Growth Target:** Targeting 15% to 20% growth, driven by registrations in new geographies and an asset-light model.
    *   **Capex Plan (Next 3 Years):** ₹550 Crores for Greenfield capex (including backward integration).
    *   **Brownfield/Maintenance Capex:** Budget of ₹50 Crores to ₹60 Crores per annum remains applicable.
    *   **Greenfield Project On-Stream:** Most Greenfield projects expected by FY2023. Mini-projects (antidiabetic expansion, chlorosulfonation product) to come on stream in FY2022.
    *   **ZLD Conversion:** Two more Tarapur facilities to be converted by the end of FY2022.
    *   **Incremental Opex for ZLD:** Approximately ₹1 Crore per quarter, with plans to offset through waste heat recovery and byproduct revenue.
    *   **FDA Update:** Planning a mock audit by an ex-USFDA inspector in August/early September, with updates expected by next quarter.

    *   **Formulation Capacity Utilization:** Currently operating at 80%.
    *   **Formulation Capex:** ₹55 Crores budget for Oncology product expansion and development, expected to commercialize in two years.
    *   **Formulation Business Model:** Asset-light, IP-specific model allows for manufacturing outside, not restricted by internal capacities.
