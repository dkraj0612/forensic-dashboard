## Executive Overview

Aarti Drugs Limited (ADL) reported Q1 FY26 results with a 6% year-on-year revenue growth to INR 591 crores. Gross profit margins improved by 130 basis points to 36.8%, driven by the normalization of input costs. EBITDA saw a 12% YoY increase to INR 74 crores, with EBITDA margins improving to 12.6%. The Sayakha greenfield project, focused on backward integration for anti-diabetic products, has commenced trial production. The Tarapur greenfield site for salicylic acid production is undergoing a calibrated ramp-up, with contributions expected from Q3 FY26. The Formulation business grew by 14% YoY to INR 80 crores, with USFDA and UK MHRA approvals obtained for oncology and oral solid dosage facilities, respectively. Management anticipates further EBITDA margin improvement driven by increasing global API demand, higher regulated market penetration, and enhanced capacity utilization, with a strategic focus on deepening backward integration and cost competitiveness.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **API Segment:**
    *   **Volume Growth:** Recovered and grew by 9% YoY in Q1 FY26. Anticipates double-digit volume growth for FY26.
    *   **Pricing:** Negative price variance of ~4.5% in Q1 FY26. Expects 3-4% negative price variance in H1 FY26, stabilizing in H2 FY26.
    *   **Capacity Expansion:** Metronidazole capacity increased. Salicylic acid production at Tarapur greenfield is ramping up, aiming for 800 tonnes/month soon and 1,600 tonnes/month installed capacity by end of FY26. Expects contribution from Q3 FY26.
    *   **Regulated Markets:** USFDA approved Tarapur API facility is undergoing audits. Expects commercial sales to the US market in 9-12 months. Europe market also opening up with potential for increased regulated sales.
    *   **Metformin:** Company is a leading manufacturer, aiming for higher utilization and planning 350 tonnes/month brownfield expansion.
*   **Specialty Chemicals & Intermediates:**
    *   **Sayakha Greenfield Project:** Trial production commenced; expected to be utilized in Q2 FY26, with potential to shift to Q3 FY26. Primarily for captive consumption of anti-diabetic products and intermediates, also for choline chloride.
    *   **Salicylic Acid:** Targeting 800 tonnes/month for break-even at current pricing, with a long-term aim of 1,600 tonnes/month. Exploring antidumping duty on Chinese imports.
*   **Formulation Segment (Pinnacle Life Science):**
    *   **Growth:** Grew 14% YoY in Q1 FY26. Targeting revenue of INR 550-600 crores in 3 years, driven by oncology and other OSD registrations.
    *   **Product Launches:** Oncology dossier development ongoing, targeting completion by December 2026 for filing. UK MHRA approval for OSD facility received. Targeting commercial sales for oncology products from FY27 onwards. Exploring toll manufacturing opportunities.
    *   **Market Expansion:** Commercial operations commenced in Latin America and parts of Africa.
*   **Sustainability:**
    *   **Solar Power Plant:** 24.4 MW solar project through JV expected to lower power costs from FY27 onwards.
*   **Backward Integration:** Sayakha plant aims to reduce reliance on external raw materials for anti-diabetic products and choline chloride.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance:

*   **Q1 FY26 Performance:** Management reported revenue growth of 6% and EBITDA margin of 12.6%, indicating a recovery from previous quarters. Gross margins improved as expected due to input cost normalization.
*   **Sayakha Greenfield Project:** Trial production has commenced as per previous announcements, with commercialization expected in Q2 FY26, subject to resolving initial operational issues. This timeline is consistent with earlier expectations for trial runs.
*   **Tarapur Greenfield (Salicylic Acid):** While trial runs and sales started, the ramp-up is slower than initially implied, with break-even expected at 800 tonnes/month and full contribution from Q3 FY26. The competitive pressure from Chinese dumping has introduced a new challenge, with the company seeking antidumping duties.
*   **Formulation Segment Approvals:** Achieved USFDA and UK MHRA approvals for relevant facilities, aligning with strategic goals for regulated market entry.
*   **API Business Recovery:** The 9% volume uptick in Q1 FY26 confirms the anticipated demand recovery. The negative price variance of ~4.5% is in line with management's prior expectations for H1 FY26.
*   **Capex Guidance:** Q1 FY26 capex of INR 48.5 crores is within the full-year guidance range. The allocation towards R&D (50%) and brownfield expansion indicates a continued focus on growth initiatives.
