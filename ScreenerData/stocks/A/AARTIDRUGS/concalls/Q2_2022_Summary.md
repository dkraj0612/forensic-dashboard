## Executive Overview

Aarti Drugs Limited (ADL) reported a resilient performance for Q2 FY2022 amidst ongoing input cost pressures. Consolidated revenue stood at ₹579.7 Crores, with EBITDA at ₹73.8 Crores and PAT at ₹42.6 Crores. The company navigated supply chain disruptions, power outages in China, and rising freight costs, which impacted EBITDA margins to 12.7%. For H1 FY2022, consolidated revenue was ₹1,161.3 Crores, with EBITDA at ₹155.1 Crores and PAT at ₹91.4 Crores, resulting in a blended EBITDA margin of 13.4%. The standalone business contributed 87% to consolidated revenue, with domestic revenue declining 4.5% YoY and exports growing 7% YoY, primarily due to a high volume base in Q2 FY2021. API volume growth was flat, with a subdued contribution from acute therapy products. The formulation segment saw revenue of ₹75.7 Crores, a 6.4% YoY growth. Management expects raw material prices and freight costs to taper off by the end of FY2022 and anticipates EBITDA margins to return to normal levels (18%+) within the next couple of quarters. The company continues to focus on leadership for its top revenue-contributing products and maintain a strong balance sheet with a net debt-to-equity ratio of 0.5x.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Product Leadership & Pricing:** Continued focus on maintaining leadership for top revenue-contributing products, with proactive price hikes to sustain margins.
*   **Mitigation of Input Costs:** Strategy to pass on price increases, acknowledging a lag due to unforeseen events. Expectation of raw material price stabilization by end of FY2022.
*   **Shift to Chronic & Lifestyle Therapies:** Ongoing transition towards high-value lifestyle and chronic therapies to drive long-term EBITDA margins.
*   **Specialty Chemicals & Intermediates:** Expected robust growth driven by niche value proposition and geopolitical tailwinds.
*   **Formulation Pipeline:** Robust pipeline of products under development across multiple therapeutic areas, with expanded market reach.
*   **Capacity Utilization:** API segment operating at around 70% utilization, with potential to scale back to 75-80%. Formulations segment at 70-75% utilization.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

*   **Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance:**
    *   **EBITDA Margins:** The guidance of returning to "normal levels" in the coming quarters was not achieved in Q2 FY2022, with margins at 12.7% due to significantly higher input costs (coal, freight, wage arrears) and a lag in passing these on. The previous Q1 FY22 call anticipated the worst of raw material price hikes to be behind them, but Q2 experienced continued upward pressure. The expectation of EBITDA margins around 18% in the "coming quarters" has now been revised to a target of 17-18% within the next two to three years, with Q4 FY2022 being the target for commencing this recovery.
    *   **Raw Material Price Stabilization:** The expectation from Q1 FY22 that raw material price hikes were peaking has not materialized, with Q2 FY22 seeing continued volatility and sharp increases, particularly in coal prices and domestic basic chemicals. Management now expects stabilization by the end of FY2022.
    *   **API Volume Growth:** The Q1 FY22 expectation of 10-15% API volume growth was not met, with Q2 FY22 reporting flat growth due to challenges in taking aggressive orders amidst price volatility and a temporary dip in demand for certain antibiotic applications.
    *   **Revenue Growth:** While not explicitly targeted at the start of Q2, the H1 FY22 growth of 3.3% is significantly lower than the 10-15% targeted for FY22 and FY23 mentioned in the Q1 call.
    *   **Capacity Utilization:** API utilization remains at approximately 70%, with plans to scale back to 75-80%. Formulation utilization is 70-75%. This is consistent with previous indications.
    *   **Formulation Business:** Export formulation sales, impacted by logistical issues and lower tender order execution in Q2, are expected to recover, with Q3 showing improvement over Q2.

*   **New Forward-Looking Promises, Guidance, or Timelines:**
    *   **EBITDA Margins:** Targeting 17% to 18% for the next one to two years, with recovery expected to commence in Q4 FY2022 if market conditions stabilize.
    *   **Raw Material Price Stabilization:** Expected by the end of FY2022.
    *   **Gross Margin Improvement:** Expecting improvement in Q4 FY2022 and Q1 FY2023, contingent on stabilization of raw material prices.
    *   **Formulation Exports:** Expected to contribute a higher percentage of overall sales in the next couple of quarters as logistical issues resolve and registrations/market expansion continues. Q3 expected to be better than Q2.
    *   **API Volume Growth:** Implicitly expected to improve from flat, but no specific new target provided for Q3.
    *   **Product Price Realization:** Improved realization expected for finished products. Export contracts (2.5-3 months lag) will see price increases reflected with a lag.
    *   **USFDA Update:** In an advanced stage with mock audits planned; positive Australian audit for the same plant provides confidence. Updates expected next quarter.
    *   **Capex:** Production at couple of Greenfield sites planned by end of calendar year 2022.
    *   **Gliptins Plant:** Recently commissioned, with two commercial batches completed and validation batches for new gliptins taken.
    *   **Freight Costs:** From China, freight costs have corrected 20-30% from peak levels. From India, normalization is ongoing and expected to streamline in the next two quarters.
    *   **Power and Fuel Costs:** Expected to decrease as coal prices taper off.
