To be a global leader in building the future of energy, contributing to the power, transmission & energy industries through cutting-edge solutions - whilst enhancing the quality of life for generations to come.

### 👤 Mission

### 👤 Transforming Lives
▪Incorporated in 2010, Advait Energy Transitions is emerging company mainly focused into Power Transmission Solution & energy Transitions Sector. ▪Possesses a niche product portfolio and a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, providing EPC solutions and services across geographies in both business verticals. ▪Operates manufacturing facilities for ACS and OPGW wires, Emergency Restoration Systems (ERS), and manufacturing over 140+ stringing tools for its power transmission business.
