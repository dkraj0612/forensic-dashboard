## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, a seasoned Indian technical staffing company founded in 1987, continues to demonstrate its robust presence across crucial sectors such as Oil & Gas, Engineering, Power, and Infrastructure. With over three decades of experience and a workforce exceeding 4,000 engineers and technical professionals, Aarvi reinforces its position as a comprehensive end-to-end solutions provider. The company highlights its ability to deliver substantial cost savings to a prestigious clientele, including Larsen & Toubro, Cairn, Reliance Industries, and Indian Oil. Its service portfolio encompasses Technical Staffing, Project Management, Construction Supervision, Inspection Services, Pre-Commissioning & Commissioning Assistance, and Operations & Maintenance (O&M). Aarvi is actively pursuing international expansion, with a strategic focus on the UAE and Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, leveraging a comprehensive in-house resume database of over 700,000 professionals and a strong referral program for talent acquisition. The Q2 2021 earnings presentation reiterates the company's foundational strengths and strategic outlook, emphasizing resilience and growth.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's strategy continues to focus on leveraging its core competencies and expanding its market reach:

*   **Client Wallet Share & Acquisition:** Increase business from existing clients and secure new clients within current and emerging sectors.
*   **International Market Expansion:** Strengthen global presence, particularly in the Middle East, with a new JV established in Oman for manpower deputation and O&M services.
*   **Talent Acquisition and Retention:** Utilize an extensive in-house resume database (over 700,000) and a robust referral program to attract and retain talent.
*   **End-to-End Service Delivery:** Provide a comprehensive suite of services from project conception through to Operations & Maintenance (O&M), including large-scale manpower mobilization for project shutdowns.
*   **Sectoral Diversification:** Target a broad spectrum of industries including oil & gas, power, infrastructure, telecom, automotive, and metals & minerals.
*   **Value Proposition:** Offer highly effective and affordable services that deliver significant cost savings to clients.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Enhance domain expertise and achieve operational efficiencies.
*   **Digitalization:** Implement digital processes for contactless operations management.
*   **New Vertical Ventures:** Explore opportunities in emerging sectors such as Airports, Ports, Defence, and Healthcare.
