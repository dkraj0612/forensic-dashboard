## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, an Indian technical staffing and project management solutions provider established in 1987, continues its strategy of market expansion and service diversification across key sectors including Oil & Gas, Power, and Infrastructure. The company maintains a significant operational footprint with over 4,500 deployed engineers and technical professionals, leveraging a substantial resume database and referral network. Aarvi is actively pursuing international growth, with recent acquisitions and new business development managers in the UAE, UK, and Indonesia. The company is also focusing on digital transformation and exploring new industry verticals such as Ports, Defence, and Healthcare, while expanding its service offerings across the engineering value chain.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's strategic priorities continue to revolve around growth and diversification:

*   **Client Deepening & Expansion:** Securing new orders from marquee clients like TECHNIP Energies India Ltd, IOCL, Mahanagar Gas Ltd, and Reliance (for a shutdown project at Jamnagar Refinery).
*   **International Growth:** Successfully acquired MNR Technical Services LLC in the UAE. Appointed Business Development Managers in Indonesia and the UK, with minor orders received in the UK.
*   **Vertical Diversification:** Venturing into new industry verticals such as Ports, Defence, and Healthcare.
*   **Service Diversification:** Expanding service offerings across the engineering value chain, including Social Media Marketing and SEO. Started a permanent placement division. Reinvigorating IT staffing efforts.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Aggressively working towards O&M of Tank Terminals. Focus on improving efficiencies by enhancing domain knowledge.
*   **Digitalization:** Implementing digitalization for contactless operations management.
*   **Talent Acquisition:** Continuous efforts to attract and retain professionals, supported by a large in-house database and referral programs.
