## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, a pioneer in technical staffing services since 1987, is a leading Indian company specializing in engineering staffing solutions across Oil & Gas, Power, LNG, Refinery, Petrochemical, and Infrastructure sectors. With over 4,000 engineers and technical personnel on its payroll, the company provides end-to-end services from project conceptualization to Operations & Maintenance (O&M), including large-scale manpower mobilization for shutdowns. Aarvi utilizes an extensive in-house resume database of over 700,000 entries and a strong referral program. Key clients include Larsen & Toubro, Reliance Industries, Indian Oil, Cairn, and Shell LNG. The company has expanded its international presence to the UAE and Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, with ongoing procedures for establishing an office in Indonesia and an advisor appointed in the UK. Aarvi aims to provide cost-effective solutions and is exploring new verticals such as IT staffing.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's strategy continues to focus on leveraging its core competencies and expanding its market reach:

*   **End-to-End Service Delivery:** Covering the entire project lifecycle from conceptualization to O&M, including shutdown mobilization.
*   **Sector Diversification:** Targeting a wide range of sectors including oil & gas, power, LNG, infrastructure, manufacturing, and IT.
*   **Talent Acquisition & Mobilization:** Utilizing a large resume database (over 700,000) and a strong referral network to identify and deploy skilled professionals.
*   **International Expansion:** Actively establishing operations in the UAE, Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Oman (subsidiary nearing completion), Indonesia (procedures begun), and building strategy in the UK. Agency agreements in Kuwait and Saudi Arabia are also being discussed.
*   **Client Acquisition & Retention:** Added over 15 new clients during the financial year and secured long-term contracts with major companies like Nayara, BORL, IOCL, and Shell LNG.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Enhancing domain knowledge and efficiency.
*   **New Vertical Exploration:** Identifying opportunities in the IT staffing sector for potential inorganic expansion.
*   **Cost Savings for Clients:** Delivering effective and affordable staffing solutions.
*   **Geographical Expansion:** Opened a branch in Kolkata to expand network in eastern India.
*   **Cash Utilization:** Surplus cash is intended for inorganic expansions.
