## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, a prominent Indian technical staffing company founded in 1987, continues to be a significant player in providing specialized engineering staffing solutions. The company currently deploys over 4,800 engineers and technical personnel across a wide spectrum of core sectors including Oil & Gas, Power, LNG, Refinery, Petrochemical, Infrastructure, and Renewables. Aarvi offers comprehensive end-to-end services, encompassing project conceptualization, design, construction, pre-commissioning, commissioning, and Operations & Maintenance (O&M), as well as large-scale manpower mobilization for shutdowns. The company leverages an extensive in-house resume database of over 700,000 resumes and a robust referral program for talent acquisition. Key clients include industry leaders such as Larsen & Toubro, Reliance Industries, and Indian Oil. While international business faced COVID-19-related travel restrictions in prior periods, Aarvi has continued its international expansion by establishing joint ventures in Oman and Indonesia to capitalize on growing demand for manpower deputation and O&M services in Asia. The company has also highlighted positive domestic momentum, securing new contracts.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's business strategy remains centered on leveraging its extensive experience and expanding its market footprint:

*   **End-to-End Service Delivery:** Providing comprehensive technical staffing solutions across the entire project lifecycle, from initial concept to operations and maintenance, including specialized shutdown services.
*   **Sector Diversification:** Targeting a broad range of industries such as oil & gas, power, renewables, infrastructure, and manufacturing, aiming for resilience against economic fluctuations.
*   **Talent Management:** Utilizing a vast in-house resume database (over 700,000) and a strong referral network to ensure a consistent supply of skilled technical professionals.
*   **International Market Growth:** Building on its Indian success to expand internationally, with a strategic focus on high-growth Asian markets, as evidenced by previous joint ventures.
*   **Client Relationship Enhancement:** Increasing business from existing clients through additional manpower supply and cross-selling opportunities, alongside acquiring new clients in established verticals.
*   **Operational Excellence:** Continuously improving domain knowledge and operational efficiency, coupled with the digitalization of processes for contactless operations.
*   **New Vertical Expansion:** Exploring opportunities in emerging sectors such as Airports, Ports, Defence, and Healthcare, while diversifying service offerings across the engineering value chain.
