## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, an Indian technical staffing pioneer established in 1987, continues to be a prominent provider of expert engineering staffing solutions, with over 4,000 engineers and technical personnel currently deployed. The company specializes in comprehensive end-to-end solutions, from project conceptualization to design, construction, pre-commissioning, commissioning, and operations & maintenance (O&M) services, including large manpower mobilization for shutdown assignments. Aarvi maintains an extensive in-house database of over 700,000 resumes and a robust referral program to attract talent. It serves a diverse array of core sectors including Oil & Gas, Power, LNG, Refinery, Petrochemical, Infrastructure, and Renewables. The company boasts a distinguished client base, including major players like Larsen & Toubro, Cairn, Reliance Industries, and Indian Oil. While international business faced headwinds due to COVID-19 travel restrictions, Aarvi has continued its strategic international expansion by forming new joint ventures in Oman and Indonesia to tap into high-growth markets for manpower deputation and O&M services in Asia. The company saw positive domestic momentum during the quarter, securing over 10 new contracts with private companies and new orders totaling INR 50 Cr.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's strategy remains focused on leveraging its established expertise and expanding its market reach:

*   **Comprehensive Service Offering:** Providing end-to-end technical staffing solutions across the entire project lifecycle, from conceptualization to O&M, including specialized shutdown assignments.
*   **Diversified Sector Focus:** Targeting a broad range of sectors including oil & gas, power, renewables, infrastructure, and various heavy industries, ensuring resilience across economic cycles.
*   **Robust Talent Acquisition:** Utilizing a large in-house resume database (700,000+) and a strong referral program to ensure a continuous supply of qualified technical professionals.
*   **International Market Expansion:** Building on its track record in India to expand globally, with a specific focus on high-growth markets in Asia, evidenced by new joint ventures in Oman and Indonesia for manpower deputation and O&M services.
*   **Client Relationship Management:** Serving an esteemed client list of marquee names and securing new contracts, as highlighted by 10+ new contract wins and INR 50 Cr in new orders during the quarter.
*   **Cost-Effective Solutions:** Emphasizing its value proposition of providing highly effective and affordable services that deliver significant cost savings to clients.
