## Executive Overview

Aarvi Encon, established in 1987, is a leading Indian technical staffing solutions provider focusing on engineering talent for the Oil & Gas, Power, LNG, Petrochemical, and Infrastructure sectors. The company currently has over 6,000 engineers and technical personnel on its payroll, offering end-to-end services from project conceptualization to Operations & Maintenance (O&M), including large-scale manpower mobilization for shutdowns. Aarvi leverages an extensive in-house resume database of over 800,000 entries and a strong referral program for talent sourcing. Its client base includes prominent companies such as Larsen & Toubro, Reliance Industries, Indian Oil, and TechnipFMC. Aarvi has expanded its international presence to the UAE and Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. The company emphasizes providing cost-effective solutions and is exploring new verticals, including IT staffing, potentially through inorganic expansion.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarvi Encon's strategy focuses on leveraging its established strengths and pursuing growth opportunities:

*   **End-to-End Service Delivery:** Providing comprehensive engineering services from project inception through O&M, including critical shutdown manpower needs.
*   **Sector Diversification:** Serving a wide array of industries including Oil & Gas, Power, LNG, Infrastructure, IT, and manufacturing.
*   **Talent Acquisition & Mobilization:** Utilizing a large resume database (over 800,000) and robust referral networks to deploy over 6,000 professionals.
*   **International Expansion:** Expanding global operations, with established presence in UAE and KSA.
*   **Client Acquisition & Retention:** Aiming to increase wallet share from existing clients and acquire new ones. Key clients like Reliance, Indian Oil, Cairn, TechnipFMC, Larsen & Toubro, and HMEL are highlighted.
*   **Geographical Expansion:** Opening new branches in key Indian locations to enhance reach.
*   **Cost Savings for Clients:** Delivering efficient and affordable staffing solutions.
*   **New Vertical Exploration:** Venturing into new industry verticals such as Airports, Ports, Defence, and Healthcare.
*   **Digitalization:** Enhancing operational efficiency through the digitalization of processes for contactless operations.
