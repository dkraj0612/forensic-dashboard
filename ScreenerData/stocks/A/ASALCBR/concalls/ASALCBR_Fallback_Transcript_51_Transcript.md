### 👤 Q4-FY22 Operational Highlights and Outlook
The capacity utilisation was optimum during the quarter. Margin impact in Q4-FY22 can be attributed to the sudden and steep increased in raw material prices and power & fuel costs. The raw material prices continue to show increasing trends throughout FY22 and have increased the material consumed cost by 5% and the power & fuel costs by 33% on a YOY basis. The sales during the FY 2021-22 returned to normalcy in spite of 50+ days Covid-19 2nd wave impact during Q1- FY22, the outlook for sales in FY23 continues to be positive. The BOD has recommended a dividend of INR 1/- per equity share for FY22, subject to shareholders approval.
