## Aarti Pharmalabs (AARTIPHARM) - Q3 Calendar Year 2025 Investor Update - One-Page Executive Summary

**Date:** September 22, 2025 (6th Annual General Meeting Investor Presentation)

---

### Executive Overview

Aarti Pharmalabs (APL) presented a comprehensive Investor Presentation at its 6th Annual General Meeting on September 22, 2025, summarizing its full financial year 2025 (FY25) performance and strategic progress. The company reported a robust FY25 with EBITDA reaching INR 16,507 Mn, reflecting an inferred year-on-year growth of 12.6%, and an EBITDA margin of 20.3%. APL solidified its position as a leading small molecule CDMO/CMO company in India, a globally recognized manufacturer of generic APIs, and a dominant player in Xanthine derivatives. Key operational successes included numerous successful USFDA, EUGMP, EDQM, KFDA, and COFEPRIS audits across its facilities (Tarapur Unit 4, Vapi Intermediate facility, Dombivli Unit). Strategic expansion projects progressed well, with the commissioning of Caffeine production at Unit 5 (100 MT/month capacity), enhancement of Xanthine capacity to 5,000 TPA, expansion of the semi-commercial block at Vapi, and the commencement of a 21 MW solar power plant at Akola. The first phase of the greenfield Atali site, a significant CDMO/CMO and Intermediates project, is also expected to be commissioned.

### Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Segment Focus:** APL maintains its strategic focus on Generic API, Xanthine derivatives, and CDMO/CMO services, emphasizing regulatory adherence, novel chemistries, and cost-effective process development for rapid scale-up.
*   **Regulatory & Quality Excellence:** Continued successful completion of global regulatory inspections and accreditations (USFDA, EUGMP, EDQM, KFDA, COFEPRIS) across various units reinforces confidence in its compliance and quality standards. Specific EUGMP approval for Bicalutamide in the Oncology block at Unit 4 underscores specialized capabilities.
*   **Capacity Expansion & Infrastructure:**
    *   **Atali Project:** Phase 1 commissioning expected, signifying a major capacity addition (450+ kL) for CDMO/CMO and intermediates, supporting future growth.
    *   **Xanthine Expansion:** Capacity enhanced to 5,000 TPA, reflecting continuous progress towards higher utilization and market share.
    *   **Vapi & Tarapur Units:** Ongoing expansion and successful audits of semi-commercial production and API blocks indicate readiness to meet increasing demand.
    *   **New Product Lines:** Commissioning of Caffeine production at Unit 5 diversifies the Xanthine portfolio and taps into new market opportunities.
*   **R&D and Innovation:** Operationalization of a third R&D center underscores the company's commitment to process innovation, cost optimization, and developing robust manufacturing routes.
*   **Sustainability Initiatives:** The commencement of the 21 MW solar power project at Akola contributes to operational efficiency by reducing energy costs and aligns with environmental sustainability goals.
