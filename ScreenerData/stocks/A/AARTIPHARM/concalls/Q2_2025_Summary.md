## Aarti Pharmalabs (AARTIPHARM) - Q2 FY25 Earnings Call - One-Page Executive Summary

**Date:** November 4, 2024

---

### Executive Overview

Aarti Pharmalabs reported a solid H1 FY25, with consolidated revenue increasing by 13% year-on-year to ₹1014 crore. Consolidated EBITDA saw a 13% year-on-year increase to ₹197 crore, and Consolidated PAT grew 11% year-on-year to ₹110 crore. The company operates across three segments: Xanthine derivatives (45% of Q2 turnover), API & Intermediates (51% of Q2 turnover), and CDMO/CMO (3% of Q2 turnover). The CDMO/CMO segment is working with 19 customers on 55 projects, with 28 commercial and 27 development stage projects, indicating a focus on late-stage projects. Management reiterated its FY25 EBITDA growth guidance of 10-12% and a long-term EBITDA growth goal of approximately 15% annually. Key expansion projects, including the Vapi USFDA Intermediate Manufacturing Site and the Akola solar power plant, are nearing or have reached commissioning. The Atali greenfield project is progressing well for a Q4 FY25 commissioning.

### Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Segment Focus:**
    *   **Xanthine Derivatives:** Increasing focus on the pharmaceutical market for regulated approvals (USFDA DMF, EUGMP CEP) in FY25. Aiming to shift 20-25% of capacity towards pharma (20% regulated, 5-10% non-regulated) to stabilize margins. Long-term partnership with global giants and dominant Indian player position leveraged against Chinese competition.
    *   **API & Intermediates:** Strategic focus on regulated and semi-regulated markets, supported by successful regulatory inspections (Brazilian, visa). New customer product commercializations driving volume growth.
    *   **CDMO/CMO:** Strong pipeline growth with 55 projects. Primarily working with innovator companies (~90%). Focus on late-stage projects (28 commercial, 27 development). Benefiting from "China Plus One" and Biosecure Act resulting in increased customer engagement and RFPs.
*   **Capacity Expansion & Infrastructure:**
    *   **Vapi USFDA Intermediate Unit (Semi-Commercial Block):** Completed and undergoing trial production, bridging the gap for small to medium batch sizes (gram to ton scale).
    *   **Akola Solar Power Plant:** Commissioned end of Q2 FY25, expected to deliver significant energy cost savings in H2 FY25.
    *   **Atali Project (CDMO/CMO & Intermediates):** Greenfield project progressing well, commissioning expected by Q4 FY25. Phase 1 will have 60 reactors with ~450 KL capacity, with scope for phased expansion. Full operationalization and utilization expected 12-18 months post-commissioning.
    *   **Xanthine Capacity Expansion:** 9,000 MTPA target phased over time, with full completion by Q3 of next fiscal year (FY26). Full operationalization expected 2-3 quarters thereafter.
*   **R&D & Innovation:** Continuous cost improvement initiatives, including process efficiency and solar power. Focus on multi-stage synthesis in CDMO/CMO to increase value addition and customer engagement.
*   **Sustainability:** Solar power plant contributing to cost reduction and reduced power dependency.

### Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

#### Historical Targets Audit:

*   **FY25 EBITDA Growth Guidance (10-12%):** Reaffirmed. H1 FY25 EBITDA grew 13% YoY, indicating the company is on track for the full-year target.
*   **CDMO/CMO Segment Growth (Previous 25% YoY for FY25):** Revised guidance to a slightly lower range due to project spill-overs. Management stated shift by one or two months can change the number by 5-7% on the lower side. However, conviction in long-term growth remains strong.
*   **Atali Project Commissioning (Q4 FY25):** Reaffirmed. Management expects the site to start contributing meaningfully in FY27 due to stabilization and ramp-up.
*   **Vapi Semi-Commercial Block Operationalization (Q2 FY25):** Completed and undergoing trial production, consistent with guidance.
*   **Xanthine Capacity Expansion (9,000 MTPA):** To be fully operational by Q3 FY26, with utilization taking another 2-3 quarters, consistent with previous timelines.
*   **Xanthine Raw Material Production Commencement:** Not explicitly mentioned in this call, but Xanthine facilities operated at near full capacity in Q2.
*   **Trading Business Exit:** Not explicitly mentioned in this call.

#### New Forward-Looking Promises, Guidance, or Timelines:

*   **FY25 EBITDA Growth Guidance:** 10% to 12% reaffirmed.
*   **Long-term EBITDA Growth Guidance:** Approximately 15% annual growth for the next three years. Management acknowledges that segment growth could potentially surpass this, but aims for cautious commitment.
*   **CDMO/CMO Segment FY25 Growth:** Significant deliveries expected in H2 FY25. While Q1 FY25 performance was around ₹50 crore, and a previous 25% growth target may be impacted by spill-overs, management is confident of strong growth next year.
*   **Atali Project Commissioning:** By Q4 FY25. Expected to contribute meaningfully to growth from FY27 onwards.
*   **Vapi Semi-Commercial Block Operationalization:** Completed and undergoing trial production.
*   **Xanthine Capacity Expansion:** Full operationalization by Q3 FY26. First phase operationalization in H2 FY26.
*   **FY25 Total Capex:** H2 FY25 spending of ~₹250 crore.
*   **FY26 Capex:** H1 FY26 spending of ~₹150 crore.
*   **Xanthine Pharma Market Focus:** Aim to push 20-25% of capacity to pharma segment (20% regulated, 5-10% non-regulated) to stabilize margins.
*   **Xanthine Regulatory Approvals (USFDA DMF, EUGMP CEP):** Filing in the current fiscal year, expecting approvals and triggering in FY26.
*   **CDMO/CMO Customer Engagement:** Increased engagements, visits, and RFPs due to Biosecure Act and "China Plus One."
*   **API Segment Growth:** Driven by capacity utilization and debottlenecking efforts.
*   **FY26 Growth Drivers:** Expected to be driven by product mix, pricing, and partial utilization of CDMO/CMO and Xanthine phase 1 expansion. FY27 is seen as the year for bulk capacity operationalization and significant margin contribution from Atali and Xanthine expansions.
*   **Inventory:** Inventory days have increased due to long gestation CDMO/CMO projects with sizable orders for future dispatch. This is expected to fluctuate higher due to appetite for multi-stage synthesis.
*   **Xanthine Pricing:** Spot market pricing has deteriorated due to excess Chinese capacity, while contracted prices are under pressure but margins are protected by cost pass-through. Strategy is to move away from spot market towards long-term and regulated markets.
*   **Xanthine Revenue Potential (9,000 MT):** Range widened to $100-$150 million.
*   **Gross Margin Ranking (Highest to Lowest):** CDMO/CMO, API, Xanthine. Gross margin for Q2 FY25 was 65% overall.
