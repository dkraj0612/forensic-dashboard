## Executive Overview

Aarti Pharmalabs (AARTIPHARM) reported Q2 FY24 results with consolidated revenues declining by 4% QoQ to INR 441 crores. However, consolidated EBITDA increased by 4% QoQ to INR 89 crores, and consolidated Profit After Tax (PAT) rose by 10% QoQ to INR 52 crores. The company is focused on its core segments: API & Intermediates, CDMO/CMO, and Xanthine derivatives. Key strategic initiatives include backward integration for Xanthine raw materials, capacity expansion at Atali, Gujarat, and a focus on regulated markets. Management reiterated its FY24 EBITDA growth guidance and expects ramp-up benefits from new capacities in H2 FY24.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Consolidated Revenue:** INR 441 crores (down 4% QoQ)
*   **Consolidated EBITDA:** INR 89 crores (up 4% QoQ)
*   **Consolidated PAT:** INR 52 crores (up 10% QoQ)
*   **Consolidated EPS:** INR 5.78

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarti Pharmalabs' strategy remains centered on:

*   **Backward Integration:** Project to manufacture a key Xanthine raw material is on track to commence operations in Q2 FY24, aiming to reduce import dependency, particularly from China.
*   **Regulated Market Focus:** Approximately 58% of intermediates and APIs were sold to regulated markets in Q2 FY24.
*   **Specialized Verticals:** Continued focus on specific chemistries and therapies.
*   **CDMO & CMO Services:** Management aims for this segment to contribute 10-12% of overall revenue in FY24, with an increasing number of commercialized and development-stage products. The focus is shifting towards scale-up and commercial manufacturing expertise.
*   **Xanthine Derivatives:** Operating at full enhanced capacity (~90% utilization). Management is evaluating Brownfield expansion options for potential capacity enhancement.
*   **Capacity Expansion:** The Greenfield project in Atali, Gujarat, is progressing towards an H2 FY25 completion target. A semi-commercial production block at the USFDA intermediate manufacturing site in Vapi is slated for completion in Q2 FY25 to cater to smaller batch sizes.
*   **Sustainability:** One Xanthine plant received an EcoVadis Silver Medal, with efforts underway to extend this to the entire corporate entity.

*   **FY24 EBITDA Growth (10-15%):** Management reiterated this guidance, stating they are on track to meet it, driven by growth in CDMO/CMO and API & Intermediates segments.
*   **Ramp-up of new expanded blocks at Unit IV, Tarapur & Xanthine Derivatives:** Management confirmed that utilization ramp-up from new blocks will start in H2 FY24.
*   **Adding 40+ value-added products each year:** Management confirmed R&D aims for this, with a consistent focus on APIs, intermediates, and CMO/CDMO projects.
*   **Capex of approximately 350-500 Crs for a new unit in Gujarat, with commercialization expected in FY25:** The Atali project completion is now guided for H2 FY25. The Capex range for Atali remains INR 350-500 Crs.
*   **Nine hydrogen reactors added in FY24:** Management confirmed these are added and operational.
