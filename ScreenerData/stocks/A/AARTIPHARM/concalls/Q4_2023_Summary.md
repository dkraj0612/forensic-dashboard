## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aarti Pharmalabs' strategy is built upon several core strengths and growth initiatives:

*   **Backward Integration:** Developing and manufacturing key intermediates for APIs, reducing supply chain risks.
*   **Regulated Market Focus:** Significant exports to lucrative regulated markets such as the US, EU, and Japan, contributing 53% of total exports. Dedicated approvals for corticosteroids and anti-cancer products in these regions.
*   **Specialized Verticals:** Dedicated manufacturing blocks for Anti-Cancer and Cortico Steroids products, along with High Potent APIs (HPAPIs) development and manufacturing capabilities.
*   **CDMO & CMO Services:** Offering custom synthesis from laboratory to commercial scales, working with 14 innovator and Big Pharma companies, and providing CMO offerings for regulated markets.
*   **Xanthine Derivatives:** Positioned as the largest Indian manufacturer for Xanthine derivatives (including Caffeine), with applications in beverages, nutraceuticals, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. The company benefits from the "China + 1" strategy.
*   **R&D and Innovation:** Strong R&D centers, developing over 150 intermediates and commercializing over 75 APIs. A focus on process innovation, flow chemistry, and developing 40 new products per year. The company holds over 52 patents filed.
*   **Capacity Expansion:** Increasing capacities of existing products and adding new value-added products. Plans include adding 400+ KL reactor volume in Phase 1 and a Capex of approximately 350-500 Crs for a new unit in Gujarat, with commercialization expected in FY25.
