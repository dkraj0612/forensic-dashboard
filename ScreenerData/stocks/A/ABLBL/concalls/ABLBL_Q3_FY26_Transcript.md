Overall business delivered 6% like-to-like growth, despite the festive shift continuing its strong performance. Consolidated EBITDA up 21% in this quarter, driven by strong operating leverage at the back of double-digit sales growth. In absolute terms EBITDA stood at INR431 crores compared to INR355 crores in the same quarter last year. EBITDA margin expanded by 180 bps from 16.6% in Q3 FY25 to 18.4% in Q3 FY ‘26.
