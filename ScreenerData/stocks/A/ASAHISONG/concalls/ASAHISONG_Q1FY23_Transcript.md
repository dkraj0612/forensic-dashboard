### 👤 Asahi Songwon Colors Limited
“I am pleased to report to you on our performance for Q1FY23. While we witnessed robust growth in our topline, our profitability was under pressure. Despite the fact that our results appear to be lower than expected, I am reasonably pleased with our performance given our current operating environment. In the last two years, we witnessed a relentless increase in raw material prices and a resultant increase in product realisations. The tide seems to have turned with raw material prices starting to come off their highs, and so have the product realisations. The decline in realisation coupled with inventory losses has negatively impacted our profitability. While we have indeed outperformed the industry in our blue business and have had a strong start to the 2nd quarter, I would like to mention that the operating environment remains highly uncertain. On a standalone basis, our blue business is operating at decadal low margins. Barring a few quarters in an uncertain environment, we remain highly optimistic about a strong bounce back in our operating performance.”

### 👤 EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR
“I am pleased to update you on the quarter gone by. We expect all the investments made during the last two years indeed to hold the Company in good stead, going forward. While we have taken positive strides in the Azo business, it is yet to be reflected in the financial performance. Undoubtedly, we have lagged in our plans to ramp up our Azo operations. However, slowly but surely, we are witnessing traction in the business and remain confident of achieving 50% capacity utilisation by the end of the year. The current market conditions are very challenging and we expect this cyclical dip to play itself out in a few months. On the API front, we have recorded a record top line however the margins here too were under pressure and the operating performance will significantly improve going forward. We also remain on track with our CAPEX plans for the API business. On a consolidated basis, we expect a strong bounce back in our operating performance coupled with robust revenue growth as our investments in API and Azo's start to pay off.“
