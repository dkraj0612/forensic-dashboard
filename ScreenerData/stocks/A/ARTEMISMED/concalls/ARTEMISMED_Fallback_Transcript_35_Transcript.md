### 👤 Financial Highlights – Q4 and FY23
We have had a great year where we have shown improvement in most financial and operational parameters. During the year, we have steadily ramped up occupancy in the 2nd Tower which was commissioned in FY22 and the construction of the 3rd Tower with ~200 beds is going on in full swing with 22 new OPDs on the ground floor of the 3rd Tower already inaugurated.” During the fiscal, Artemis Hospital was awarded for its excellence in Spine Surgery by FICCI at Advantage Healthcare 2023. The Company was awarded a Diamond status for Stroke Treatment by World Stroke Organisation (WSO). It has reinforced its Robotic Surgery programme by introducing Robotic Joint Replacement and enhanced its capability to undertake high end surgeries by adding 6 Operation Theatres during the year. As part of its growth initiatives, The Company inaugurated two new centres under the 'Daffodils by Artemis' banner, one each in Jaipur and South Delhi (East of Kailash). It has introduced a new concept of hospitals under the banner 'Artemis Lite'. These hospitals are positioned as friendly, neighbourhood multi speciality hospitals focussing on personalised care and secondary/tertiary services. The first such unit has been opened in South Delhi (New Friends Colony). Another unit will be inaugurated in Gurgaon during the first quarter of FY24. Further, the first of the two hospitals in Mauritius as part of the Operations and Management Agreement is ready to be commissioned. This 80+ bed facility under the name of 'Artemis Curepipe Hospital' will be operational in the first half of FY 24. The Company’s subsidiary, Artemis Cardiac Care, a joint venture with Philips Medical Systems Nederland BV, has also opened five new cardiac care centers during the year. Additionally, the Company has launched comprehensive homecare services under the brand of 'Solace by Artemis' during the year, catering to all home healthcare needs of patients. The Company has plans in place to continue increasing its footprint and reach through an asset-light strategy in the coming year.
