### 👤 Message from Joint MD
It is our pleasure to present the performance of Anuh Pharma Ltd. for the first half of FY 2025, covering the period April to September. The first half was eventful, with notable progress in operational efficiency and capacity expansion. We achieved an increase in sales and added new products to strengthen our portfolio. Continued investments were made in compliance, product quality, and long-term growth drivers. This review will cover our financial performance, key operational updates, progress on strategic initiatives, and the outlook for the second half. Our commitment remains focused on delivering sustainable growth, enhancing reliability for our customers, and creating value for all stakeholders. Financial performance:

### 👤 Key Reasons for
EBITDA Decline: Lower sales of certain high-margin products compared to the previous year resulted in reduced contribution, with expected recovery in the second half. Non-recurring maintenance expenses impacted profitability during the period. Adverse currency movements led to unrealised and realised losses affecting margins.

### 👤 Company Overview
The Company derived ~55% of its revenues from exports in FY24‐25. The Company has strong marketing partnerships with 360 customers in over 59 countries including Europe, Mexico and South Africa. The expansion project with state of the art manufacturing facility targeted at regulated market is completed and commercial production from the said new facility started w.e.f. 21st December, 2019. Anuh Pharma, a bulk drug manufacturing company is part of the INR 8.5bn SK Group, which employs ~2000 people across businesses such as manufacturing of pharma formulations, trading, distribution and logistics (primarily for large MNC brands) Anuh Pharma is one of the largest manufacturers of Macrolides and Anti‐TB products in India, besides being a major player in Anti‐bacterials, Anti‐malarial, Anti‐hypertension and Corticosteroids The Company owns one manufacturing facility at Tarapur over 11,400 sq. mtrs of land (Incl. newly acquired plot of 7,800 sq. meters) and an R&D facility at Mahape spread over 10,000 sq.ft.
