### 👤 Focus on Shareholder Value Creation
Anuh Pharma has consistently focused on shareholder value creation and has maintained dividend payout ratio in the range of 32-40% of post tax profits over the last 3 years (FY12-FY15) Dividend payments have increased at a CAGR of 6% over the same period The growth has continued in FY15 as well, with operational revenue, operational profit and post tax profits registering a growth of 6.5%, 17% and 25% respectively, as is evident from the Financial Highlights Anuh Pharma has rewarded it’s shareholders with a 2:1 bonus issue i.e. 2 new shares for every 1 share held
