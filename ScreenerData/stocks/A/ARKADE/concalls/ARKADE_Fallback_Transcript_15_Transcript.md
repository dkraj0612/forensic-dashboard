Revenues for H1 FY '26 stood at Rs. 430 crores compared to Rs. 329 crores in H1 FY '26, reflecting a growth of 31% year-on-year. EBITDA for the half year stood at Rs. 98 crores as against Rs. 101 crores in H1 FY '25. The EBITDA margin for H1 FY '25 stood at 23%. The area sold during the quarter stood at 1.1 lakh square feet up 4% year-on-year. Collections for Q2 FY '26 came in at Rs. 320 crores, registering a year-on-year growth of 7%.
