On the operational side, Adani Total Gas has achieved during this quarter robust year-on-year volume growth of 16% to 267 MMSCM which was largely driven by increase in the CNG volume by 21% to 185 million metric standard cubic metre, MMSCM and achieved 82 MMSCM of a PNG volume which grew by 6%. This increase in the volume was achieved despite our higher gas cost because we passed through very calibratedly to the end consumers.
