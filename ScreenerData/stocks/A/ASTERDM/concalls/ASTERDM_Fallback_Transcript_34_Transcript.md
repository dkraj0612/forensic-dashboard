### 👤 ▪

### 👤 ▪
Dr. Azad Moopen will continue in his role as the Executive Chairman; Mr. Varun Khanna (Group MD, QCIL) and Mr. Sunil Kumar (CFO, Aster) will be promoted to the position of MD & Group CEO and Group CFO of the Merged Entity respectively
