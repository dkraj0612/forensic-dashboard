### 👤 APL Apollo Tubes Limited (APL Apollo) [BSE
533758, NSE: APLAPOLLO] is one of India’s leading branded steel products manufacturers. Headquartered at Delhi NCR, the Company operates seven manufacturing facilities with a total capacity of 1.75 Million MTPA. It has a PAN-Indian presence with units strategically located in Sikandarabad (3 units), Bangalore, Hosur, Raipur and Murbad. APL Apollo’s multi-product offerings include over 1,000 varieties of MS Black pipes, Galvanized Tubes, Pre-Galvanized Tubes, Structural ERW Steel tubes and Hollow Sections. With state-of-the-art-manufacturing facilities, APL Apollo serves as a ‘one-stop shop’ for a wide spectrum of steel products, catering to an array of industry applications such as urban infrastructures, housing, irrigation, solar plants, greenhouses and engineering. The Company’s vast 3-tier distribution network of over 650 dealers is spread all across India, with warehouses cum- branch offices in over 20 cities
