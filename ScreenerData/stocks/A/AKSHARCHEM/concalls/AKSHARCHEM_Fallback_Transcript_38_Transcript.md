The revenue from Brownfield expanded capacity of CPC Green is expected to reflect in the books from Q4 FY’18. Keeping in view the market conditions and the dollar/rupee rate, we expect the revenue for FY’18 to remain flat; however, we expect the market conditions to improve and with expanded capacities coming into play, we expect revenue growth of 15-20% in FY’19.
