## Executive Overview

Aarti Industries Limited (AIL) presented its Q1 FY20 performance, highlighting sustained revenue growth driven by strong volume expansion in key segments and an optimized product mix. The company emphasized its strategic position as a global player in benzene-based derivatives with integrated operations and a strong focus on R&D and sustainability. Key developments include the commissioning of the Hydrogenation of Nitro Toluene facility in Q4 FY19 and continued expansion in both specialty chemicals and pharmaceuticals. A significant corporate action was the demerger of the Home & Personal Care (HPC) undertaking into a separate entity, Aarti Surfactants Ltd. (ASL), aimed at enabling dedicated focus and improving AIL's financials. The company also secured substantial long-term multi-year contracts with global majors, reinforcing its trusted partnership status.
