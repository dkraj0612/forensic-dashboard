### 👤 Explanatory Note on IND AS 115 Adjustments on Revenue
Investor Presentation | Quarter and Half Year Ended 30th September 2025 An entity shall account for consideration payable to a customer as a reduction of the transaction price and, therefore, of revenue. Consideration payable to a customer includes cash amounts that an entity pays, or expects to pay, and also includes credit or other items. The reduction towards variable consideration in our revenue under IND AS 115 represents stockist incentives, discounts, schemes and claims passed on to the customers. The flat growth of Comfy in Q2 FY26 is on account of Quantity Purchase Scheme (QPS) were given as products has been changed to credit note.
