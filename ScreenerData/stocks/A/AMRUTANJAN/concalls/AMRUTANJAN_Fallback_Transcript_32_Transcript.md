### 👤 Explanatory Note on IND AS 115 Adjustments on Revenue
Investor Presentation | Quarter and Period Ended 31st December 2025 An entity shall account for consideration payable to a customer as a reduction of the transaction price and, therefore, of revenue. Consideration payable to a customer includes cash amounts that an entity pays, or expects to pay, and also includes credit or other items. The reduction towards variable consideration in our revenue under IND AS 115 represents stockist incentives, discounts, schemes and claims passed on to the customers.
