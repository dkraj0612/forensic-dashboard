### 👤 Allsec Technologies Limited (BSE
532633, NSE: ALLSEC), headquartered in Chennai, is a pioneer in the HRO space and is today India’s second largest Payroll Service Provider. Founded in 1998, Allsec has emerged as a global brand, offering high-end business process solutions across key industry verticals in 40 countries. With ~ 4,000 employees across India, UK, the Philippines and the US, Allsec processes more than 8 lakh pay slips each month for over 200 legal entities around the world. Allsec’s unique SmartHR and SmartPay platforms are designed to address the complex challenges in today’s HR environment, and the company has integrated the latest in tech - Robotic Process Automation (RPA), Smart Analytics, Chatbots and Mobility for enhanced employee engagement.
