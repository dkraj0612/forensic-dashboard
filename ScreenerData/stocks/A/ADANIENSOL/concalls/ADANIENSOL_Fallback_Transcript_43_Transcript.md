### 👤 O&M Case Studies
Restoration of 765 kV S/C Tiroda - Koradi Line II on ERS.

### 👤 Problem Statement
Due to thunderstorm and rain followed by heavy wind, multiple towers collapsed and damaged in 765 kV Tiroda - Koradi line Ckt-II near Nagpur-Maharashtra

### 👤 Key Findings
The line was restored on ERS within 15 days and on permanent coordinates within 32 days Proper planning, managing available resources effectively and precise execution stayed as a key component to the restoration plan

### 👤 Outcome Achieved
Emergency Restoration System (ERS) of lines Low (lagging) power factor due to inductive auxiliary load Variation in power factor due to site specific conditions Variation in auxiliary load with respect to operational requirements

### 👤 Problem Statement
Correction of power factor leading to sizeable savings in Aux power cost Total Voltage Harmonic Distortion’s (VTHD) at 33 kV switchgear was well within the acceptable IEEE519 limits Variation of load profile during the measurement was -15% with respect to maximum loading By using STATCOM system can provide variable compensation between ± 1000 kVAR at 33 kV based on PWM technique

### 👤 Key Findings
Power factor of Auxiliary supply system increased from 0.76 to unity. It has decreased the Auxiliary power consumption and has led to considerable savings in auxiliary power expense
