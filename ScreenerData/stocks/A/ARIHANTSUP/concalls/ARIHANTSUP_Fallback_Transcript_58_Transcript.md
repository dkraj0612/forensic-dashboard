### 👤 CMD’S MESSAGE
“After years of consolidation, the Real Estate sector has witnessed a recovery in home buyer demand, especially Affordable Housing. When the first lot of demand was witnessed post Covid 1st wave, it was said to be ‘pent up’ demand. Post that when the central government and state governments gave sops, the increased demand was attributed to these sops and low home loan rates. Now even after some of the sops have been withdrawn, we continue to see increasing demand which points to the fact that we are in a sustainable long term up-cycle. We at Arihant, have been preparing for this phase, and look forward with a lot of confidence.”
