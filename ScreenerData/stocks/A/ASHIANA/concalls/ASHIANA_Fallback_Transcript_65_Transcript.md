Discontinuation of Ashiana Navrang (Halol) project and consequential write off

### 👤 =
The company commenced operations in Halol (Gujarat) in 2014 with the project ‘Ashiana Navrang’ under the JDA (Joint Development Agreement) model, with a total saleable area of 6.71 Isft.
