Commenting on the Q3 & 9MFY22 results Mr. Vinay Tripathi, Managing Director of the company said: “We are delighted to post strong revenue & profitability growth for Q3 & 9MFY22 on the back of strong demand for logistical services from multiple sectors. The growth has also primarily driven by our three-fold strategy of revenue diversification, industry diversification and addition of wallet share in our existing customers. We are happy to state that the revenues were up by ~137% on Y-o-Y basis for 9MFY22 and PAT growth was as high as 384% (Y-o-Y) registering a PAT of Rs. 22.5 crs for 9MFY22. With relationships across the fleet owners, our network and strategic locations of showrooms & workshops, we are optimistic about the growth prospects. The dealership will also enable us to strengthen our existing relationships further by catering to the needs of fleet owners for new purchase, sale of spare parts and servicing of their fleet. Going forward, we are optimistic of the demand scenario across industries our revenue diversification strategy coupled with addition of wallet share from the existing customers helps us to maintain a positive outlook on the revenue growth and profitability. At ASL, we are committed towards providing end to end logistics services across the value chain and innovating our business model as per the needs of the customers and business
