Our waste-to-energy plant delivered a strong performance as always, operating at a healthy PLF of about 84%. In Q1 FY '26, our PCMC-WTE generated over 25 million green units, underscoring our commitment to reducing fossil fuel dependency and cutting carbon emissions. I would like to highlight the commercial launch of our extended producer responsibility initiative in the WTE division.
