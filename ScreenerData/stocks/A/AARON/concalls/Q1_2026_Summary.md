## Executive Overview

Aaron Industries Limited, in its Q1 2026 investor presentation, highlights ongoing progress in expanding its market presence and product offerings within both the elevator and stainless steel segments. The company has officially launched its new stainless steel brand, STELIX, and introduced the EVOQ360 Home Lift Solutions, emphasizing smart technology, energy efficiency, and customizable designs. The narrative reinforces the strategic importance of its vertically integrated model, leveraging in-house stainless steel processing for its elevator components. Management points to significant investments in automation, particularly the Salvagnini line in Unit 3, enhancing production efficiency and capacity for auto door systems, now exceeding 5,000 units per month. While specific Q1 financial metrics for FY2026 are not present in this transcript, the presentation focuses on strategic initiatives and market outlook. The company is actively pursuing expansion in North and South India, participating in industry events to boost brand visibility, and exploring international markets, particularly in Nepal and Africa.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **New Brand Launch (STELIX):** Strategic expansion into premium stainless steel solutions, emphasizing strength, durability, and innovation.
*   **New Product Launch (EVOQ360 Home Lift Solutions):** Advanced, energy-efficient, and minimalistic home lift solution with pit-less installation, smart door design, battery backup, and customizable cabin designs. Targets high-end luxury markets.
*   **Capacity Utilization:** Current auto door system capacity exceeds 5,000 units/month. Projected utilization over the next 1-2 years is around 3,500 units/month.
*   **Vertical Integration:** Continues to leverage its SS Polishing Division for in-house processing of stainless steel sheets for auto doors and elevator cabins, enhancing quality and efficiency.
*   **Market Expansion (Domestic):**
    *   **North India:** Appointed senior sales professional for business development in Punjab, Haryana, UP, and northern states. Showcased products at an Elevator exhibition in February 2026.
    *   **South India:** Tied up with an elevator company in Hyderabad for AP & Telangana sales. Expanded reach in Karnataka by appointing traders in Bengaluru and Belgaum.
*   **Market Expansion (International):**
    *   **Nepal:** Receiving encouraging orders from established channel partners.
    *   **Africa:** Key focus for expansion with strong demand potential.
*   **Distribution Network:** Actively broadening reach by adding more distributors and channel partners for both Elevator products and Stainless Steel Sheets.
*   **Marketing & Brand Building:** Leveraging traditional and digital platforms, participating in trade fairs (e.g., Smart Lift & Mobility World 2026, Nairobi exhibition), and sales promotion events (Raipur).
*   **Contract Manufacturing:** Aiming for long-term partnerships with leading elevator manufacturers for consistent SS sheet supply and contract manufacturing.
*   **Technology & Automation:** Facilities equipped with modern SALVAGNINI automation and precision stainless steel sheet polishing lines.
*   **No Major Capex:** Focus on utilizing existing capacity.
