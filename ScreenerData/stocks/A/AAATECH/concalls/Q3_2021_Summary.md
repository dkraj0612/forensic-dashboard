## Business Strategy & Drivers

AAATECH's business strategy is centered on its exclusive focus on cybersecurity services, aiming to capitalize on the growing market demand driven by increased digitization, cyberattacks, and data breaches. Key drivers and strategies include:

*   **Expansion of Service Portfolio:** Diversifying services beyond IT Audit to minimize concentration risk.
*   **Strengthening Brand Name:** Enhancing visibility and industry position through empanelments.
*   **Employee Retention:** Investing in employee training to improve service quality and foster trust.
*   **Better Working Capital Management:** Utilizing IPO funds for improved working capital, enabling bidding for larger orders and managing high receivables.
*   **Expansion of Business PAN India:** Establishing branches in key cities to broaden outreach.
*   **Customer Diversification:** Reducing dependence on specific clients or sectors by bifurcating revenue between public and private sectors, with a noted growth in private sector revenue for FY21.
*   **Empanelment Strategy:** Becoming a preferred provider for cybersecurity audit needs and securing empanelments with government organizations and banks for recurring business.
*   **Service Offerings:** Positioning itself as one of the few companies qualified to provide specific cybersecurity services, including various types of audits, penetration testing, and security architecture design.
*   **Market Growth:** Capitalizing on the projected growth of the Indian cybersecurity market from USD 1.97 billion in 2019 to USD 3.05 billion by 2022, with a CAGR of 15.6%.
