### 👤 Rs. In Crs
On account of All India lockdown during the peak summer season, we witnessed a sharp revenue drop of 79% on Y-o-Y in Q1FY21 As the lockdown restrictions were eased off in a phased manner across the country, we witnessed a surge in the retail demand for Air-Conditioners in the month of May & June 2020, especially from Tier 2 & Tier 3 cities The pent-up demand from retail led to release of high inventory levels from channel, which in turn led to increase in manufacturing orders by OEM’s. We however envisage that the demand scenario has been improving as the lockdown restrictions are eased off and should achieve normalcy in Q3/Q4 FY21 On the back of Covid-19, we witnessed a significant drop in revenues and de-operating leverage play out.
