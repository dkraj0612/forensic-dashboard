## Executive Overview

Aadhar Housing Finance Limited (AADHARHFC) held its Q4 FY24 earnings call on June 4th, 2024, marking its first post-listing investor call following its IPO on May 15th, 2024. The company reported a strong financial year with Assets Under Management (AUM) crossing INR 20,000 crores, reaching INR 21,120 crores, a 23% year-on-year growth driven by a 20% increase in disbursements. Profit After Tax (PAT) grew by 33% to INR 750 crores. Key operational highlights include a diversified borrowing base, a consistent focus on the EWS/LIG segment (71% of AUM), an average ticket size of INR 1 million, and an extensive network of 523 branches. The company emphasizes its commitment to financial inclusion, technology investment, and data science for risk mitigation and operational efficiency. Management expressed confidence in sustained growth, citing strong demand in their core segment and a strategic plan to deepen penetration in smaller towns and districts.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

Aadhar Housing Finance's strategy revolves around:

*   **Deepening Penetration:** Continuing the "deeper impact strategy" by expanding into smaller towns and districts, targeting 70-75 new branches annually.
*   **Core Segment Focus:** Maintaining a strong focus on the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) and Lower Income Group (LIG) segments, which constitute 71% of AUM.
*   **Technology & Data Science Investment:** Leveraging technology and data analytics for improved underwriting, risk mitigation, customer understanding, and operational efficiency.
*   **Product Diversification:** Offering a range of mortgage products, including home loans (75% of book) and micro loans against property (25% of book).
*   **Asset Quality Management:** Maintaining in-house collection mechanisms and a proactive approach to managing asset quality, evidenced by declining GNPA.
*   **Diversified Funding:** Continuing to diversify borrowing sources and explore higher tenor loans.
*   **Green Housing Promotion:** Partnering with IFC to promote green housing initiatives.
