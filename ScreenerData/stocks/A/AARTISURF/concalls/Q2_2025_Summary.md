## Executive Overview

Aarti Surfactants Limited (ASL), incorporated in 2018, is a leading Indian manufacturer of specialty surfactants for the Home and Personal Care segment. The company, a demerged entity from Aarti Industries Limited, focuses on quality, innovation, and customer-centricity, serving both domestic and international markets. ASL's mission is to deliver high-performance, sustainable surfactant solutions, while its vision is to be the Global Partner of Choice for leading FMCG companies in its domain. The 7th Annual General Meeting presentation on September 23, 2025, highlights the company's journey, including a shift in its registered office to Madhya Pradesh, a successful Rights Issue, and the establishment of a functioning R&D Center.
