Aarti Surfactants Limited's strategy is driven by several key factors:
*   **Focus on Eco-friendly Products:** Increasing demand for eco-friendly and biodegradable surfactants, aligned with consumer preferences.
*   **Emerging Market Growth:** Capitalizing on rapid urbanization and rising disposable incomes in emerging markets like India, China, and Southeast Asia, which fuels demand for personal and home care products.
