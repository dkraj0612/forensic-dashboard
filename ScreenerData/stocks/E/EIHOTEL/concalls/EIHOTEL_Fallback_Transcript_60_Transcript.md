### 👤 CONCEPT
The Oberoi at Home is an initiative that brings the best of our restaurants to the doorsteps of our guests. The offering is based on the tenets of trust our guests have reposed in us over the years - the finest fresh ingredients, authentic preparations, creative presentation and highest standards of hygiene and care at every stage. The repertoire has the following offerings - Ready-to-eat classic dishes or custom curated menus or mini-meals from our award winning restaurants. Working lunches, high-teas, dinners in offices. Working lunches, high-teas, dinners to a group of corporates located in different parts of a city or the country and connected via virtual meetings. Do-it-yourself selections as an ode to the “Chef in you” - from semi-finished selections to exactly portioned raw ingredient sets.
