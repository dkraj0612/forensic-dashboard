I'm pleased to share that we continued the momentum and began this year on a strong positive note. During the quarter, we clocked the domestic revenue of INR109.4 crores, representing a robust growth of 19.3% year-on-year. This broad-based growth was driven by both our radio and digital segments, which grew by 10.8% and 4x year-on-year, respectively. EBITDA for the year, excluding digital, stood at INR20.5 crores as compared to INR19.2 crores in Q1 FY '24. EBITDA margins were at 21.8%. We have grown ahead of industry, both in volume and value. Our FCT revenue for the quarter was at INR75.2 crores as compared to INR67.8 crores, a growth of 10.8% year-on-year.
