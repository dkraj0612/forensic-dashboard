### 👤 Q3 & 9M FY24 Results Highlights (Standalone)
In Q3FY24, Excel Industries recorded a revenue of ₹180 Cr, experiencing a 9% dip from the previous quarter. This decline was attributed to adverse global economic conditions, inventory destocking, and continuous pressure on sales realizations. Despite the challenging economic landscape, the company continues to maintain its market share in key product categories. The export market contributed 13% and 15% of revenue in Q3 FY24 and 9M FY24 respectively The company's EBITDA margins for Q3 FY24 stood at 1%, while 9M FY24 stood at 1.9%. The subdued margins in Q3 were attributed to challenges in international business, product mix and a sharp decline in realizations. Other income includes Rs 7.4 Cr interest on income tax refunds on account of favorable orders received from income tax authorities pertaining to previous years. Excel Industries is strategically focused on diversifying its product portfolio and introducing new products to adapt to market dynamics.
