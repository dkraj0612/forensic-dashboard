### 👤 Management Commentary
Commenting on the results Mr. Pankaj Bajaj, Chairman & Managing Director said, FY26 marks a milestone year for EHIL, with a standout launch at Eldeco Solano Gardens, record sales bookings and collections, a strengthened growth pipeline, and steadily scaling execution… “In Q4FY26, we delivered our best-ever quarter in terms of sales bookings and collections, supported by strong momentum at Eldeco Solano Gardens, the securing of three large projects in Lucknow’s prime growth corridors with cumulative GDV of nearly ₹2,000 crore, and steadily scaling construction intensity. For FY26, we delivered record area booked, booking value, and collections, reflecting strong underlying demand, disciplined execution, improved cash conversion, and continued pipeline build-up through land aggregation and approvals.” The launch of Eldeco Solano Gardens witnessed an exceptional market response, with 343 units sold out of 433 units launched. This translated into meaningful volume traction, with ~5.11 lakh sq. ft. booked across plots & villas with the sales value of ₹384.50 crores.
