### 👤 Management Commentary
Commenting on the results Mr. Pankaj Bajaj, Chairman & Managing Director said, The two trends of consolidation and premiumisation continued in the quarter just gone by. While demand for premium real estate continued to rise, arranging supply through land assembly and getting timely approvals remained the key challenge. We are actively working on our new project pipeline to address the growing demand. We successfully launched our project Eldeco Latitude 27 in July 2023 and we have received a good response on the launch. The project has total saleable area of above 5 lac sqft. We hope to launch phase 2 of our successful township Eldeco Imperia in the next couple of months. Between Apr’23 to June’23 we purchased a new land of 4.65 acres and plan to continue with this primary land aggregation in the coming months. In July, we added a new land parcel of over 3 acres at a prime location and this will be a high-rise luxury project called Eldeco Trinity; the approvals for this project are under process. Based on these launches and a couple of other projects in the pipeline, we expect our booking value to more than double in the current year. During the quarter gone by, our bookings witnessed a growth of 40% standing at Rs. 31.4 crores compared to Rs. 22.4 crores in Q1FY23. The collection stood at Rs. 25.5 crores. We handed over 68,686 sq.ft during Q1FY24. No new launches happened in the quarter. We are getting ready for the festive season demand with the aforementioned launches. The demand for quality real estate is strong and we are well placed to capitalise on it. Marching toward growth
