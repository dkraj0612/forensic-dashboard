Additionally, our domestic operations delivered an improvement in margins, aided by prudent inventory management and operational efficiencies. On a standalone basis, revenue stood at Rs. 244.3 crores, marking a YoY increase of 18.2%, with EBITDA of Rs. 36.5 crores and margins of 14.9%, reflecting the progress in our domestic operations. International operations faced challenges, with margins impacted by the nature of order booking in the U.S. and a difficult operating environment in Dubai. Despite these challenges, we remain focused on enhancing operational resilience to drive profitability across our global markets in the coming quarters.
