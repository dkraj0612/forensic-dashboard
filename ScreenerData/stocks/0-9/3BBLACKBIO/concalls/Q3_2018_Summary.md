## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited ("the Company") operates through two main segments: Agro-inputs (under Kilpest India Limited) and Healthcare Diagnostics (under 3B BlackBio Biotech). The Agro-input division leverages 44 years of experience in pesticide formulation in Central India, with a focus on brand building for bio-fertilizers, bio-pesticides, and public health products. The company boasts strong brand equity and distribution in Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh, supported by in-house R&D recognized by the Government of India. The Healthcare Diagnostics segment specializes in proprietary molecular diagnostic kits (TRUPCR® brand) for oncology, infectious diseases, and human genetics. This segment is CDSCO licensed and ISO 13485:2016 certified, emphasizing in-house R&D, quality, competitive pricing, and import substitution. The company is actively developing new assays and expanding its export market reach.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

**Agro-Input Segment (Kilpest India Limited):**

*   **Brand Building & Product Focus:** Increase focus on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Development of Bio-Fertilizers and Bio-Pesticides to capitalize on organic farming trends.
*   **Government Supplies:** Continue to be a trusted supplier for public health products to various Government Departments (e.g., Ministry of Defence, MP State Warehousing & Logistic Corporation, Central Warehousing Corporation, Directorate of Health Services Government of Maharashtra, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare).
*   **Distribution & Geographic Strength:** Leverage established brand equity and distribution network in Central India (especially Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh).
*   **R&D Recognition:** Utilize in-house R&D recognition from the Government of India, Ministry of Science & Technology.
*   **Export Market Penetration:** Continue regular bulk exports to Bangladesh and exports on demand to Afghanistan, Nepal, and Jordan.
*   **Debt Reduction:** Aim to become "ZERO DEBT" in the near future with the receipt of remaining preferential issue funds, leading to substantial savings on interest costs.
*   **GST Implementation:** Expect a boost from GST implementation.

**Healthcare Diagnostics Segment (3B BlackBio Biotech):**

*   **Proprietary R&D & Assay Development:** Design, develop, and validate proprietary kits (TRUPCR®) in-house. Focus on continuous R&D for international quality products.
*   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Offer a wide range of diagnostic kits covering infectious diseases, oncology, and genetics. Continuously develop new assays for rapid growth.
*   **Market Penetration & Import Substitution:** Achieve nationwide presence with competitive pricing and timely supplies, offering an import substitution advantage.
*   **New Customer Acquisition:** Continuously add new customers across India, including Super-Speciality Hospitals and Diagnostic Labs.
*   **Export Marketing:** Initiate efforts for export marketing, expecting results in the next financial year.
