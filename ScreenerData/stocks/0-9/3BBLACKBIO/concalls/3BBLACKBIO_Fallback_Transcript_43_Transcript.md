### 👤 Utilization of Funds
We have been looking for options to acquire a company in similar business area to get inorganic growth in FY22 and onwards. We were able to find a potential company in November 2022, however, as we moved forward with the due-diligence, certain facts emerged, which raised serious concerns for future business viability of the target company’s products due to emerging stiff competition from far-east companies. So seeing this future concern and negative finding we decided not to proceed with the acquisition in best interest of the company and its shareholders. Due to COVID, most of the lifesciences and molecular diagnostic companies are seeking very high valuations. M&A valuations globally have gone high due to demand from molecular diagnostic companies which are flushed with cash. We will only go for the acquisition if it makes financial sense and meets our internal IRR criteria. We would also be looking at share buyback in FY22/23 post merger, if we are unable to secure a desired acquisition candidate. Our aim is to create maximum value for our shareholders. We will be deploying some funds in the joint venture created in Manchester, UK depending on the need.
