## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (the "Company") operates through two segments: Agro-inputs (Kilpest India Limited) and Healthcare Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Biotech). The Agro-input segment, with 46 years of experience, focuses on brand building for bio-fertilizers, bio-pesticides, and public health products, leveraging strong brand equity and distribution in Central India. The Healthcare Diagnostics segment offers proprietary molecular diagnostic kits (TRUPCR®) for oncology, infectious diseases, and human genetics, emphasizing R&D, quality, and import substitution, and is actively developing new assays and expanding its export market.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

**Agro-Input Segment (Kilpest India Limited):**

*   **Brand Building & Product Focus:** Continued emphasis on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Development of Bio-Fertilizers and Bio-Pesticides to align with organic farming trends.
*   **Government Supplies:** Maintain status as a trusted supplier for public health products to various Government Departments.
*   **Distribution & Geographic Strength:** Leverage established brand equity and distribution network in Central India.
*   **R&D Recognition:** Utilize in-house R&D recognition from the Government of India.
*   **Export Market Penetration:** Continue bulk exports to Bangladesh and demand-based exports to Afghanistan, Nepal, and Jordan.
*   **Debt Reduction:** Aim to become "ZERO DEBT" in the near future with receipt of remaining preferential issue funds to save on interest costs.
*   **GST Implementation:** Expected positive impact from GST implementation.
*   **Raw Material Challenges:** Acknowledging a difficult year for agrochemicals due to raw material price increases and shortages.

**Healthcare Diagnostics Segment (3B BlackBio Biotech):**

*   **Proprietary R&D & Assay Development:** Design, develop, and validate proprietary TRUPCR® kits in-house, focusing on continuous R&D for international quality products.
*   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Offer a wide range of diagnostic kits covering infectious diseases, oncology, and genetics, with ongoing development of new assays.
*   **Market Penetration & Import Substitution:** Achieve nationwide presence with competitive pricing and timely supplies.
*   **New Customer Acquisition:** Continuously add new customers across India and expand globally.
*   **Export Marketing:** Expand global footprint, with products undergoing validation in the USA and price negotiations in Latin America & Southeast Asia.
*   **Contractual Business:** Secure long-term (2-3 year) contracts with large customers for assured business.
*   **Tender Business:** Enter government tenders, expecting significant business.
*   **Technological Advancement:** Focus on Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) assays, coming soon.
