## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (the "Company") operates in two primary segments: Agro-inputs and Healthcare Diagnostics. The Agro-input division, leveraging a 46-year history in pesticide formulation in Central India, continues to focus on strengthening its brand for bio-fertilizers and public health products. This strategy emphasizes its R&D recognition and established distribution network, particularly in Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh, aiming to capitalize on organic farming trends and government tenders. The Healthcare Diagnostics segment, through its subsidiary 3B BlackBio Biotech, specializes in proprietary molecular diagnostic kits for oncology, infectious diseases, and human genetics under the TRUPCR® brand. The subsidiary is CDSCO Licensed, ISO 13485:2016 certified, and GMP compliant, with a strong emphasis on in-house R&D, quality standards, competitive pricing, and import substitution. Recent developments include expansion of its product range, securing long-term contracts, and investment in Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) capabilities.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

**Agro-Input Segment (Kilpest India Limited):**

*   **Brand Building & Product Focus:** Continue increasing focus on brand building for Micro fertilizers/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Development of Bio-Fertilizers and Bio-Pesticides capitalizing on organic farming trend.
*   **Revenue Diversification:** Supplying to HLL Life Care Ltd for Public Health Supplies, Central Warehousing Corporation, and U. P. State Warehousing Corporation.
*   **Geographic Strength & Government Tenders:** Strong brand equity and distribution in Central India (Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh). Trusted supplier for public health in various Government Departments.
*   **Monsoon Impact:** Management foresees a very good Rabi season due to exceptionally good monsoon, expecting reasonable growth in revenue and profitability.
*   **Export Market Penetration:** Regular exports to Bangladesh manufacturers in bulk, with products approved for export to Afghanistan, Nepal, and Jordan on demand.

**Healthcare Diagnostics Segment (3B BlackBio Biotech):**

*   **Proprietary R&D & Assay Development:** Focus on molecular diagnostics using PCR-based kits, enzymes, and reagents. Development of proprietary kits under the TRUPCR® brand. Emphasis on clinically validated, reliable, precise, and accurate assays.
*   **Market Penetration & Import Substitution:** Aiming for a nationwide presence with competitive pricing, timely delivery, and an "open system" compatible with most Real-time PCR Equipment. Recognized as India's leading healthcare company in molecular biology fine-chemicals for diagnostics.
*   **New Product Launches & Pipeline:** Launched TRUPCR® HPV 14 High Risk PCR kit, TRUPCR® STD Panel, and TRUPCR® Bacterial Respiratory Panel in Q2 FY20. Products in pipeline include a comprehensive Respiratory Panel, BRCA 1 & 2 Mutation Detection kit for Breast cancer, and a Panel for Lung Cancer with 10 genes (using NGS).
*   **Contractual Business:** Actively signing long-term (2-3 year) contracts with large customers. Six contracts currently running, ensuring yearly business of up to ₹ 40-45 Million.
*   **Infrastructure Development:** Investment in Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) for assay development and exploring clinical genetics opportunities.
*   **Global Expansion:** Regular exports to South East Asia & Middle East. Product validation in advanced stages for USA & UK, and product validation & price negotiation in Africa, Southern & Central Europe.
*   **Government Business:** TRUPCR® H1N1 Kit approved by NIV, Pune, opening doors for government business. Entering government tenders expecting business of ₹ 10-20 Million. Secured Rate Contract approvals in Rajasthan and Kerala.
