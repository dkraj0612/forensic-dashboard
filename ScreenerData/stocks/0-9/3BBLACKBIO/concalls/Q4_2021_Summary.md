## Executive Overview

This Q4 FY22 earnings update from Kilpest India Limited and 3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited reflects a year of transition and strategic recalibration, particularly in the diagnostics segment. While FY21 was heavily influenced by COVID-19, FY22 saw a deliberate pivot towards becoming "COVID Relevant, not COVID Dependent." Financials indicate a significant drop in PAT compared to FY21, primarily attributed to the drastic reduction in COVID-19 testing prices and the one-time nature of pandemic-driven sales. The company has made substantial progress on its amalgamation with 3B BlackBio and its planned NSE listing. Key growth drivers include expanding non-COVID diagnostics, strengthening the agro-chemical division through brand building and government business, and developing new verticals like NGS and Rapid Kits. International expansion is a significant focus, with increased exports and the establishment of a European subsidiary. Management is actively exploring M&A opportunities while considering a share buyback.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Transition from COVID-19 Dependence:** The company is actively positioning itself as "COVID Relevant, not COVID Dependent," aiming for sustainable growth in non-COVID diagnostics.
*   **Agro-chemical Division Focus:** Continued emphasis on Government business (GeM/Tenders), Public Health/Warehousing, and Exports. Limited financial exposure to the private agriculture market due to COVID-19 uncertainty. Focus on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. The division is cash flow positive with low debt.
*   **International Expansion:** Significant focus on expanding international business, with products accepted in over 25 countries. Exports increased substantially in FY21-22. Establishment of a European subsidiary, "TRUPCR Europe Limited," to support EU customers and expand reach. Participation in global trade shows like Medlab Middle-East 2022.
*   **Diagnostics Product Development:**
    *   **Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS):** Reviving NGS kit development and revalidations; expecting revenue contribution in later quarters of FY22-23 due to pricing challenges in India.
    *   **Rapid Kit Vertical:** Manufacturing plant ready for trial runs. Launching Dengue NS1 and Dengue IGM/IGG kits. COVID Rapid Antigen Test is ICMR approved but demand is currently low.
    *   **Molecular Diagnostics (MDx):** Maintaining focus on TRUPCR® (Real-Time PCR), TRUNGS® (NGS), and TRURAPID® (Lateral Flow). Over a decade of R&D experience with over 100 tests developed.
*   **Inorganic Growth & Capital Allocation:** Actively seeking acquisitions in similar business areas but facing high valuations for life sciences and molecular diagnostic companies. Will only proceed if financially viable and meets IRR criteria. Share buyback in FY22/23 is being considered as an alternative if suitable acquisitions are not found. Funds may be deployed in the UK joint venture.
*   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Progressing with the amalgamation scheme of 3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited. BSE approval received; application filed with NCLT. Expected to unlock the value of the subsidiary. Plans to list on the NSE post-completion.
*   **R&D and Quality Focus:** Continuous investment in R&D, with dedicated teams for human genetics, infectious diseases, and oncology. Maintaining high-quality standards with ISO 13485:2016, GMP compliance, and rigorous QC protocols for TRUPCR® products.
