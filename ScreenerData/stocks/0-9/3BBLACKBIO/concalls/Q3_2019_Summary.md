## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited operates across two distinct segments: Agro-inputs (Kilpest India Limited) and Healthcare Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Biotech). Kilpest India leverages 46 years of brand equity in bio-fertilizers, bio-pesticides, and public health products, with a strong distribution network in Central India. 3B BlackBio Biotech specializes in proprietary molecular diagnostic kits (TRUPCR®) for oncology, infectious diseases, and human genetics, emphasizing R&D, quality, and import substitution. The company is actively developing new assays, expanding its export market, and aims for debt-free status.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

**Agro-Input Segment (Kilpest India Limited):**

*   **Brand Building & Product Focus:** Continue brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Develop Bio-Fertilizers and Bio-Pesticides to capitalize on organic farming trends.
*   **Government Supplies:** Maintain trusted supplier status for public health products to various Government Departments (e.g., Ministry of Defence, State Warehousing Corporations).
*   **Distribution & Geographic Strength:** Leverage established brand equity and distribution in Central India (Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh).
*   **R&D Recognition:** Utilize in-house R&D recognition from the Government of India, Ministry of Science & Technology, DSIR.
*   **Export Market Penetration:** Regular bulk exports to Bangladesh. Demand-based exports to Afghanistan, Nepal, and Jordan.
*   **Debt Reduction:** Substantially reduced debt with preferential issue proceeds. Target to reduce working capital limit to ₹1 Crore by the end of the next financial year.
*   **GST Implementation:** Expected to boost the company.
*   **Market Conditions:** Agrochemical sales flat due to less pest infestation in Rabi crops. Sales increase of 16% for 9 months ended Dec 2018 driven by supplies to New Delhi municipal corporations and marginal export increase.

**Healthcare Diagnostics Segment (3B BlackBio Biotech):**

*   **Proprietary R&D & Assay Development:** Design, develop, and validate proprietary TRUPCR® kits in-house. Focus on India-specific tests with comprehensive validation on multiple gene pools and unique designing strategies.
*   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Offer a wide range of diagnostic kits covering oncology, infectious diseases, women's health, and human genetics. Developing new assays including Respiratory Panel, drug resistance parameters for TB, Neuro Panel, NUP214 rearrangement kit, PIK3CA kit, BRCA1&2 mutation detection, Lung Cancer panel, and Familial Hypercholesterolemia panel.
*   **Market Penetration & Import Substitution:** First licensed IVD manufacturing facility in India under Medical Device Rule 2017. CDSCO Licensed, ISO 13485:2016 certified, GMP compliant. Focus on providing reliable and affordable healthcare solutions.
*   **New Customer Acquisition:** Added new customers in Q3 2019-20. Product validations and order received in UK. Advanced stage validation in USA. Price negotiation in Africa, Southern & Central Europe.
*   **Contractual Business:** Actively signing long-term (2-3 year) contracts with large customers, currently has seven contracts ensuring yearly business of up to ₹45-50 Million.
*   **Tender Business:** Entering government tenders with an expected business of ₹10-20 Million. Received Rate Contract approvals for Molecular Diagnostic Kits in Rajasthan and Kerala.
*   **Technological Advancement:** Focus on qPCR assays and commercialization. Exploring opportunities in Clinical Genetics with NGS. Launching Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) assays soon. Developing TRUPCR cDNA, TRUPCR Sample Enrichment kits, and Liquid Biopsy extraction kit.
*   **Facility Development:** Established own cell culture facility. Acquired a Next-Generation Sequencing System. BSL-2 extraction rooms and Clean Rooms for assay preparations.
*   **Quality & Validation:** TRUPCR® kits are calibrated to WHO standards, clinically validated, reliable, precise, accurate, and all-inclusive. Open system compatible with most Real-time PCR equipment. Stringent QC protocols for raw materials and final lots.
