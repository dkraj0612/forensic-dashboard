## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited (formerly Kilpest India Limited) presented a mixed but largely stable financial update, with a focus on its dual Agrochemicals and Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) segments. The MDx segment, driven significantly by its UK subsidiary TRUPCR® Europe Limited, continues to be the primary growth area, though the transcript does not provide consolidated PBT and PAT figures for the current reporting period. The Agrochemical division maintains stability, with a focus on government tenders and brand building, and the company is anticipating increased export sales to Bangladesh. Key strategic priorities remain global expansion in MDx, portfolio diversification with advanced diagnostic technologies, and exploring inorganic growth via M&A. The company has completed its amalgamation and is preparing for an NSE listing.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Agrochemicals and Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Dx Limited).
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Continued focus on government procurement via e-tenders (GeM).
    *   Expansion of customer base into new regions.
    *   Intensified efforts in brand recognition for micro fertilizers, bio fertilizers, and public health products.
    *   Sales remain stable, and the business operates with minimal debt.
    *   Anticipate a favorable increase in export sales to Bangladesh for the current fiscal year.
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Dx Limited) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration & Geographical Expansion:** Increasing sales with existing customers and channel partners by expanding product portfolios. Penetrating new laboratory markets and untapped territories through direct engagement or distributors. TRUPCR® assays accepted in over 40 countries, with ongoing registrations and appointments of new channel partners globally. Securing annual rate contracts through international tenders. Exploring partnerships in Australia, Africa, LATAM, APAC, and the Middle East.
    *   **Portfolio Expansion:** Enhancing product offerings in qPCR and developing Digital PCR (dPCR) technology. Launched new NGS panels (PAN-MYELOID NGS Panel, BRCA Plus Kit, Endometrial Cancer Panel Kit). Developing RT-PCR and lateral flow assays for Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR). Offering automated extraction systems for OEM sales/reagent-rental.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):** Moved to a new, larger facility with dedicated labs for Production, QC, and R&D. ISO 13485:2016 accredited by BSI, UK. Manufacturing of assays for EU customers as "Made in UK." Increased visibility through participation in international conferences, leading to distributorship and OEM inquiries. Validation of several parameters ongoing in new labs.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Amalgamation process is completed. The company will internally discuss the next steps for an NSE listing.
    *   **Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A):** Exploring strategically targeted M&A for inorganic growth, contingent on financial viability and meeting internal IRR criteria. Considering share buyback if a suitable acquisition candidate is not secured.
    *   **Long-Term Contracts:** Reagent-Rental Model contracts projected to contribute 15-20% of business for the current and coming years.
    *   **Expansion and Additional Capacity:** Continuous investment in building additional manufacturing and R&D facilities/capacities.
    *   **Quality & Regulatory:** CDSCO licensed (equivalent to FDA for top-selling parameters in India). Over 120 Parameters CE-IVD marked. ISO 13485:2016 & GMP Compliant manufacturing.
