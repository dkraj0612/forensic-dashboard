## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (formerly Kilpest India Limited) is firmly entrenched in its strategic pivot towards diagnostics, with the amalgamation of its diagnostics subsidiary into the parent entity progressing. The company emphasizes its TRUPCR® brand for molecular diagnostics in oncology and infectious diseases. The success of its COVID-19 RT-PCR kits, including US FDA EUA and government approvals, continues to be a significant revenue driver, though sales volume and realization are expected to normalize. Future growth hinges on expanding the product portfolio with TRURAPID® rapid antigen and antibody tests, leveraging R&D for NGS capabilities, and penetrating domestic and international markets. The agrochemical business is being de-emphasized, with a focus on brand building for public health products and an active search for a buyer for its agrochemical brands. Financial stability is being bolstered through debt reduction initiatives.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Strategic Pivot to Diagnostics:** Continued and reinforced focus on the diagnostics sector, leveraging 3B BlackBio Biotech's established expertise and infrastructure.
*   **Brand Consolidation & Amalgamation:** Amalgamation process is progressing, with the listed entity slated for renaming to "3B BlackBio Dx Ltd." to present a unified diagnostics-focused brand. NCLT order expected in FY22.
*   **Product Diversification:** Expansion into TRURAPID® rapid antigen and antibody tests is in advanced R&D stages, with commercialization targeted for Q1/Q2 FY21-22. NGS development work is being reinitiated with commercialization expected in 2-3 quarters.
*   **COVID-19 Product Success:** Significant traction for TRUPCR® SARS-CoV-2 RT-qPCR Kits (versions 2.0 and 3.2), with over 6 million tests sold. Approvals from ICMR, CDSCO, and US FDA EUA are key achievements.
*   **R&D and Quality Excellence:** Emphasis on in-house R&D and stringent QC protocols for TRUPCR® kits, guaranteeing precision, accuracy, and reliability.
*   **Market Penetration:** Aggressive efforts to acquire new customers domestically, aiming to expand beyond the current base of over 400 customers.
*   **Export Market Focus:** Increased focus on exports post-COVID, targeting ₹ 60-80 million in FY21-22 (without COVID impact), contingent on government export license policies.
*   **Government Business & Tenders:** TRUPCR® H1N1 Kit approved by NIV, Pune, opening opportunities in H1N1 testing. Participation in various government tenders is expected to generate business.
*   **Contractual Business:** Signing long-term (2-3 year) contracts with large customers to ensure assured, predictable revenue streams, with seven contracts already in place generating ₹ 70-80 million annually (without COVID).
*   **Agrochemical Division Strategy:** Continued focus on brand building for Micro fertilizers/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Actively seeking a buyer for agrochemical brands and intangible assets, with a temporary focus on Public Health/warehousing and exports due to private agriculture market uncertainty.
*   **Asset Utilization:** All immovable assets will be fully utilized for the growth and expansion of 3B BlackBio Dx Ltd. and R&D activities.
*   **Financial Management:** Substantial debt reduction achieved. Plan to reduce working capital limit to ₹ 1.00 crore by March 2021.
*   **Inorganic Growth:** Exploring acquisitions in similar business areas for inorganic growth from FY22 onwards.
*   **Share Buyback:** Consideration of share buyback in FY22/23 if a suitable acquisition candidate is not secured.
*   **Dividend Policy:** Continuation of dividend payment policy with an increasing trend, with a higher full and final dividend payment this year.
