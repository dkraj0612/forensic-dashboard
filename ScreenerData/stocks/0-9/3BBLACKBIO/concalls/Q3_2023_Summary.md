## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited, through its subsidiary 3B BlackBio, continues its strategic dual focus on its established Agrochemical business and its growing Molecular Diagnostics division. The current transcript (Q1 FY 23-24) highlights robust revenue growth in diagnostics, with exports comprising a significant portion, entirely non-COVID related. The Agrochemical division is focused on government tenders and brand building, and is now debt-minimal. Key developments include the NCLT's approval for the amalgamation of 3B BlackBio into Kilpest India Limited, paving the way for an NSE listing. The company is actively pursuing expansion through new product development in diagnostics, international market penetration, and has successfully relocated its UK subsidiary, TRUPCR Europe Limited, to a larger facility with expanded capabilities. While M&A is still considered, the company is prioritizing financially sensible opportunities and is also evaluating a share buyback for shareholder value creation.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Kilpest maintains its Agrochemical business while driving growth in its Molecular Diagnostics subsidiary, 3B BlackBio.
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Focus on Government Business via e-tenders (GeM).
    *   Expanding customer base in new regions.
    *   Brand building for Micro fertilizers, Bio fertilizers, and Public Health Products.
    *   Minimal debt achieved, with a focus on exports (e.g., Bangladesh).
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration:** Increase sales with existing customers/channel partners by adding more products to their portfolios.
    *   **Geographical Expansion:** Reach new labs post-pandemic and expand into new markets directly or via distributors/channel partners. Registered assays in over 35 countries, adding new partners in South-East Asia and the Middle East. Pursuing registrations in high-demand countries and participating in international tenders.
    *   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Launching new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR Diagnostic Kits, and NGS verticals. Over 120 CE-IVD marked assays developed, with more in the pipeline.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):**
        *   Relocated to a new, state-of-the-art facility in Manchester with dedicated labs for Production, QC, and R&D.
        *   ISO 13485:2016 accredited by BSI, UK.
        *   Manufacturing of assays now occurring in the UK ("Made in UK" for EU customers).
        *   Focus on reducing Turn-Around-Time (TAT) and enhancing technical support.
        *   Increased visibility through participation in international conferences, leading to distributorship and OEM inquiries. Secured a high-volume annual order of Hemato-Oncology assays in the UK.
    *   **Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A):** Considering selective M&A for inorganic growth but will only proceed if it makes financial sense and meets internal IRR criteria.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** The NCLT has approved the Scheme of Amalgamation between 3B BlackBio and Kilpest India Limited. Implementation steps have begun, with NSE listing to follow post-completion.
    *   **Share Buyback:** Considering a share buyback in FY23/24 post-merger if a desired acquisition candidate is not secured.
