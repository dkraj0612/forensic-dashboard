## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited, comprising both its agrochemical business (Kilpest India Limited) and its healthcare diagnostics subsidiary (3B BlackBio Biotech), is undergoing a significant transformation. The company is strategically amalgamating the diagnostic business into the listed entity, Kilpest India, which will be subsequently renamed "3B BlackBio Dx Ltd." This move aims to consolidate and focus resources on the high-growth diagnostics sector, which currently represents over 90% of projected revenues. The agrochemical division, historically characterized by low margins and long working capital cycles, will be divested. The diagnostics business, driven by its TRUPCR® brand, has shown strong momentum, particularly with its COVID-19 RT-PCR kits, achieving US FDA Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) and securing substantial government orders. The company is also expanding its product range in oncology and infectious diseases and aims to leverage its established distribution network and R&D capabilities for future growth in both domestic and international markets.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Business Transformation:** Strategic decision to divest the low-margin, high-working capital agrochemical business (Kilpest India) and focus entirely on the high-growth diagnostics sector.
*   **Amalgamation:** 3B BlackBio Biotech is being amalgamated with Kilpest India, which will be renamed "3B BlackBio Dx Ltd." This will consolidate all assets and operations under a single diagnostic-focused entity.
*   **Diagnostics Focus:** Continued design, development, and commercialization of superior quality molecular diagnostic products under the TRUPCR® brand. Emphasis on Oncology and Infectious Diseases segments, with expansion into Rapid Cards (Antigen and Antibody based) and other scalable business verticals.
*   **R&D and Innovation:** Commitment to in-house R&D for developing new and improved diagnostic assays, including those for India-specific needs.
*   **Market Penetration & Expansion:** Leveraging the existing customer base (over 180 customers across 50 cities in India) and expanding into international markets (UK, South East Asia, Middle East, East Africa, USA, Europe).
*   **COVID-19 Response:** Continued strong sales of COVID-19 RT-PCR kits, significant government orders, and US FDA EUA for its COVID-19 RT-PCR Kit.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Plans to fully utilize Kilpest's immovable assets (land, factory building) for diagnostic business expansion and R&D.
*   **Brand Divestment:** Kilpest will continue to operate as an independent division temporarily until a suitable buyer for its agrochemical brands and intangible assets is found.
