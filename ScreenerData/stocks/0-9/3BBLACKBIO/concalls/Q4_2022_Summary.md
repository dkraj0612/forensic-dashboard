## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited's (through its subsidiary 3B BlackBio) 50th Annual General Meeting for FY 2021-22 highlights strong consolidated performance and strategic progress. The company celebrated its Golden Jubilee, emphasizing its long-standing commitment to both agrochemicals and diagnostics. 3B BlackBio continues its robust growth trajectory in the molecular diagnostics sector, demonstrating significant non-COVID revenue growth and expanding its international presence. Key initiatives include the establishment of a UK subsidiary (TRUPCR Europe Limited) to penetrate the European market, advancements in rapid kit and NGS verticals, and ongoing efforts towards amalgamation and potential NSE listing. Management remains focused on inorganic growth through acquisitions, while also considering a share buyback if suitable candidates are not found. The company declared a final dividend of 25% for FY 2021-22.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **FY 2021-22 Consolidated Revenue:** INR 80.64 Crores (INR 806.40 Million)
*   **FY 2021-22 Consolidated Profit Before Tax (PBT):** INR 43.22 Crores (INR 432.20 Million)
*   **FY 2021-22 Consolidated Profit After Tax (PAT):** INR 32.20 Crores (INR 322.00 Million)
*   **FY 2021-22 Earnings Per Share (EPS):** Rs. 42.89
*   **FY 2021-2022 Non-COVID Revenue (3B BlackBio):** INR 265.30 Million
*   **Baseline Revenue (Pre-COVID FY 2019-20):** INR 153.63 Million

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Consolidated Focus:** Commemorating 50 years, Kilpest maintains a dual focus on its established Agrochemical business and its growing Diagnostics subsidiary, 3B BlackBio.
*   **3B BlackBio Growth Trajectory:**
    *   **Non-COVID Revenue Focus:** Consistent year-on-year growth of 30-35% for non-COVID products, with FY21-22 revenue at INR 265.30 Million, exceeding the pre-COVID baseline.
    *   **Market Penetration:** Leveraging the increase in the number of labs post-COVID to expand the footprint for non-COVID products.
    *   **R&D and New Products:** Continued development and launch of new products from R&D to capture larger market segments.
*   **International Expansion:**
    *   **Global Presence:** Present in over 29 countries, including the USA, UK, Europe, Africa, Middle East, and APAC.
    *   **UK Subsidiary (TRUPCR Europe Limited):** Established through a JV with HS Biolabs Limited to accelerate the growth of TRUPCR® kits in Europe. The subsidiary is working towards ISO13485:2016 accreditation, R&D, and manufacturing capabilities. Increased visibility through participation in international conferences is expected to drive distributorships and OEM solutions.
*   **Rapid Kit & NGS Verticals:**
    *   **Rapid Kit Plant:** Plant is ready; R&D is optimizing non-COVID products with market potential. Novel products are planned for launch in FY 2022-23.
    *   **NGS Kits:** R&D and validation work has resumed for NGS kits, focusing on global market catering.
*   **Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A):**
    *   Actively seeking inorganic growth through acquisitions in similar business areas, but valuations globally are high. Acquisitions will only proceed if financially sensible and meeting internal IRR criteria.
    *   Deployment of funds in the UK joint venture is a possibility depending on need.
*   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** The National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) has granted an early meeting date (November 2nd, 2022) for the amalgamation process, with hopes for completion in the near future. A subsequent NSE listing is planned post-amalgamation.
*   **Agrochemicals:** Continued trust from farmers as an agro-input partner for 50 years.
