## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited's Q3 FY21-22 earnings transcript reveals a mixed performance across its Agrochemical and Diagnostic Kit (3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited) divisions. The Agrochemical division saw static sales in Q3, impacted by uncertainty in the private agriculture market due to COVID-19, leading management to focus on Public Health, Warehousing, and Exports. Management also noted a potential loss in production/sales during Q3 FY22. 3B BlackBio's diagnostics segment continues its strategic expansion, with a focus on increasing non-COVID sales and penetrating new markets. The development of new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR, and NGS verticals remains a priority. Progress on the amalgamation with 3B BlackBio and the eventual listing on the NSE are key ongoing initiatives. The company continues to explore inorganic growth through acquisitions but faces challenges with high valuations.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Agrochemicals:**
    *   Continued focus on Public Health, Warehousing, and Exports.
    *   Sales remained static in Q3 due to uncertainty in the private agriculture market post-COVID.
    *   Emphasis on developing novel, less harmful, and cost-effective formulations for agriculture and public health.
    *   Increased focus on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products.
    *   The division is cash flow positive with low debt.
*   **Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited):**
    *   **Non-COVID Sales Growth:** Aggressively pitching to add new customers, aiming for a substantial increase in non-COVID sales with improving market conditions and new product launches.
    *   **Market Penetration:** Plans to increase sales with existing customers and penetrate new labs.
    *   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Developing new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR Diagnostic Kits, and NGS verticals.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):** JV agreement signed with distributor. Infrastructure and lab setup is progressing, with recruitment underway. Vision to realize major success in 2022 through increased export sales and R&D outcomes.
    *   **International Business:** Participating in events like Medlab Middle-East 2022 to drive interest and explore opportunities in the Middle-East and African regions. Expecting substantial growth in international business with export restrictions removed.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Received "Observation Letter" from BSE for amalgamation. Will apply for NCLT approvals, with completion expected in approximately one year. Post-completion, the company will initiate listing on the NSE to maximize shareholder value.
    *   **Inorganic Growth:** Actively seeking acquisitions in similar business areas for inorganic growth in FY22 and beyond, but have not yet found suitable candidates due to high valuations.
    *   **Share Buyback:** Consideration for share buyback in FY22/23 if a desired acquisition candidate is not secured.
    *   **Rapid Antigen Business:** Received ICMR approval for "TRURAPID® COVID-19 Ag Test." Developing Rapid Antigen Test Kits for parameters other than COVID, with expected launches in FY22-23. Building new manufacturing facilities.
    *   **Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS) Vertical:** Reviving NGS kit revalidation and development, focusing on global markets due to unremunerative domestic pricing. Expected to start adding revenue in later quarters of FY22-23.
    *   **R&D and Quality:** Continuous investment in R&D and manufacturing. Strict QC protocols, ISO 13485:2016 certified, GMP Compliant, and USFDA-EUA for COVID RT-PCR Kit.
