## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited, through its subsidiary 3B BlackBio, continues its strategic dual focus on its Agrochemical and Molecular Diagnostics businesses. The Q2 FY 23-24 transcript highlights significant progress in diagnostics, particularly in international market penetration and product development, while the Agrochemical division maintains a stable, debt-minimal performance focused on government tenders and brand building. Key developments include the progress towards the amalgamation of 3B BlackBio into Kilpest India Limited, with completion expected by December end, paving the way for an NSE listing. The company is actively expanding its diagnostics portfolio, launching new AMR rapid tests and an NGS solid tumor panel, while enhancing its UK subsidiary's capabilities and global reach.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Kilpest operates both an Agrochemical and a Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio) business.
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Focus on Government Business via e-tenders (GeM).
    *   Expanding customer base in new regions.
    *   Brand building for Micro fertilizers, Bio fertilizers, and Public Health Products.
    *   Minimal debt achieved; sales are stable.
    *   Hopeful for decent export sales in Bangladesh.
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration:** Increase sales with existing customers/channel partners by adding more products.
    *   **Geographical Expansion:** Reach new labs post-pandemic, expand into new markets directly or via distributors. TRUPCR® assays accepted in over 40 countries; adding more channel partners in South-East Asia and Middle East. Pursuing registrations in high-demand countries and participating in international tenders, securing annual rate contracts. Discussions with large international distributors for projects to materialize this financial year. Marketing efforts in untapped markets expected to yield results next financial year.
    *   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Launching new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR Diagnostic Kits, and NGS. Launched TRUPCR® DPYD Mutations Detection Kit. Successfully completed collaborative R&D for Rapid Tests for Anti-Microbial Resistance (AMR), with products like TRURAPID® RESIST ACINETO Rapid Test and TRURAPID® RESIST 5 OKNVI to be launched post-regulatory approvals. Launched "TRUNGS® Solid Tumor Panel" for cancer diagnosis. Reviving NGS kit revalidations and development. TRUPCR® cDNA Kits are "Coming Soon".
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):** Relocated to a new, larger facility with dedicated labs for Production, QC, and R&D. Achieved ISO 13485:2016 accreditation by BSI, UK. Manufacturing of assays now occurring in the UK ("Made in UK" for EU customers). Focus on reducing Turn-Around-Time (TAT) and enhancing technical support. Participating in international conferences (e.g., IBMS Congress 2023, ACGS 2023) increasing visibility and generating distributorship/OEM inquiries. Secured a high-volume annual order of Hemato-Oncology assays in the UK. Real benefits of the Manchester Site expected this financial year. Discussions with EU distributors to expand reach in untapped European countries.
    *   **Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A):** Considering selective M&A for inorganic growth but will only proceed if it makes financial sense and meets internal IRR criteria.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** NCLT Indore bench sanctioned the scheme of amalgamation on August 4th, 2023. Implementation initiated with satisfactory progress. Merger expected to be completed by December end, making 3B BlackBio Dx Ltd fully functional. NSE listing to follow post-completion.
    *   **Share Buyback:** Considering a share buyback in FY23/24 post-merger if a desired acquisition candidate is not secured.
