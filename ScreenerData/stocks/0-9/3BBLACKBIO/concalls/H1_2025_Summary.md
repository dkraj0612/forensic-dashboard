## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited presented a H1 2025-26 investor update indicating significant growth and strategic progress, particularly in its Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) segment. The company reported strong revenue from operations of INR 5,613.67 Lakhs and Profit Before Tax (PBT) of INR 3,548.37 Lakhs for the period. The acquisition of Coris Bioconcept SRL Belgium on August 29, 2025, is a key development, aiming to bolster the IVD segment and the fight against Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR), with Coris expected to contribute positively to EBITDA in H2. The Agrochemicals division maintained stability, with exports remaining consistent. The company's strategic focus remains on global MDx expansion, portfolio diversification with advanced technologies like Digital PCR (dPCR), and pursuing inorganic growth through M&A. The company is also progressing towards an NSE listing post-amalgamation.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Agrochemicals and Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Dx Limited).
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Continued focus on government procurement via e-tenders (GeM).
    *   Exports to Bangladesh remain a key market.
    *   Sales remain stable, operating with minimal debt.
    *   Vision to supply exclusively to old customers, government tenders for vector-borne disease control, and for exports.
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration & Geographical Expansion:** Aiming to boost sales with existing customers and channel partners by expanding product portfolios. Entering untapped regions through strategic partnerships and direct engagement. Expanding presence in Africa via channel partners. Securing annual rate contracts in Middle East, Southeast Asia, and Europe. Exploring future partnerships in Africa, LATAM, APAC, and the Middle East.
    *   **Portfolio Expansion:** Continuous evolution of qPCR, Lateral Flow Immunoassay, and NGS assays. Development of Digital PCR (dPCR) technology with a hopeful commercial launch in Q3 FY 2025-26. Launch of new NGS panels: PAN-MYELOID NGS Panel, BRCA Plus Kit, and Endometrial Cancer Panel Kit. Development and sampling of RT-PCR and lateral flow assays for Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR), with TRURAPID® RESIST 5 OKNVI Rapid Test showing promising initial orders. Automated extraction systems are now available for sales/reagent-rental models. Offering integrated solutions for Rapid Diagnostics (Lateral Flow) & NGS.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):** "Made in UK" portfolio gaining acceptance across Europe, with expansion into LATAM and Africa. ISO 13485:2016 certified. Expected to grow at 20-25% for the full year.
    *   **Acquisition of Coris Bioconcept SRL, Belgium:** Strengthens IVD segment and AMR focus. Complements existing molecular diagnostics product range. Coris product portfolio includes rapid IVD test kits focused on AMR markers and a globally monopolistic LFT for African sleeping sickness. Expected to be EBITDA positive next year with reasonable growth.
    *   **Digital PCR (dPCR):** Hopeful for commercial launch by Q3 FY 2025-26.
    *   **Long-Term Contracts (Reagent Rental Model):** Expected to contribute 20-25% of total revenue for the current and coming years. Over 15 contracts are already in place.
    *   **Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A):** Actively looking for M&A opportunities globally, preferably in the EU and USA, focusing on IVD segment manufacturers or distributors. Coris acquisition is the first step after extensive evaluation.
    *   **Quality & Regulatory:** Licensed by CDSCO (India), over 120 CE-IVD marked parameters, ISO 13485:2016 & GMP Compliant manufacturing. On track for IVDR compliance by mid-FY 2026-27.
    *   **Market Position (India MDx):** Holds an estimated 12%-15% market share in India's MDx market (TAM ₹350-450 Cr), projected to grow at 8%-10% CAGR. Company aims to grow at 15%-20% for FY 2025-26.
    *   **Capacity Utilization:** Operating at ~65% of installed capacity, with potential to reach full capacity during seasonal spikes.
