## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited celebrates its "Golden Jubilee" year, reflecting on five decades of experience in agrochemicals and a decade of its subsidiary, 3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited, in healthcare diagnostics. The Q2 FY22-23 period highlights continued strategic focus amid sectoral challenges. The agrochemical division faced a sales slowdown in Q2 due to heavy monsoon, but management anticipates benefits in the upcoming Rabi season. The diagnostics segment (3B BlackBio) saw a significant drop in export sales, attributed to the European team's focus on ISO accreditation for its new UK subsidiary. However, non-COVID diagnostics sales formed the bulk of revenue, with efforts to increase penetration and expand the product portfolio.

Progress continues on key strategic initiatives including the amalgamation with 3B BlackBio, with shareholder and creditor meetings voting in favor. The company is actively pursuing inorganic growth through acquisitions but faces high valuation challenges, maintaining consideration for a share buyback post-merger if suitable targets are not found. R&D remains a core focus, with the revival of Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS) and the readiness of the Rapid Kit manufacturing plant.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Agrochemicals:**
    *   Continued focus on Government business (GeM/Tenders), Public Health, Warehousing, and Exports.
    *   Sales were impacted in Q2 by heavy monsoon; benefits are expected in the upcoming Rabi Season.
    *   Cash flow positive with low debt.
    *   Increased focus on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products.
    *   Development of novel, less harmful, and cost-effective formulations.
*   **Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited):**
    *   **Market Penetration & Reach:** Plans to increase sales with existing customers by expanding product offerings and penetrate new labs post-COVID and new markets directly or via distributors.
    *   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Developing new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR Diagnostic Kits, and Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS) verticals.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Ltd. (UK Subsidiary):** Successfully completed BSI Audit for ISO 13485:2016 accreditation. Aims for commercial production in the UK to leverage "Made in UK" acceptability, reduce lead times, and increase revenue. Sales and marketing team is being expanded.
    *   **NGS Vertical:** Revival of development and revalidation efforts. Expected to add revenue in later quarters of FY22-23, with a focus on global markets due to unremunerative domestic pricing.
    *   **Rapid Kit Business:** Manufacturing plant is ready for trial runs. Optimizing products like Dengue NS1, Dengue IGM/IGG for the current season, and plans to launch novel products in FY2022-23. COVID Rapid Antigen Test is ICMR approved but faces low demand.
    *   **Inorganic Growth:** Actively considering selective mergers and acquisitions but facing high valuations in the life sciences and molecular diagnostics sectors. Acquisitions will proceed only if financially viable and meet internal IRR criteria.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Equity shareholders and unsecured creditors have voted in favor of the amalgamation. The company plans to initiate listing on the National Stock Exchange (NSE) post-completion of the merger scheme.
    *   **R&D and Quality:** Continuous investment in R&D and manufacturing facilities. Stringent QC protocols, ISO 13485:2016 certified, GMP Compliant, CDSCO licensed, and USFDA-EUA for COVID RT-PCR Kit.
