## Executive Overview

This Q2 FY22 transcript for 3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (formerly Kilpest India Limited) reflects a period of transition as the company navigates the post-COVID landscape, with a focus on normalizing operations and expanding its non-COVID diagnostics portfolio. While acknowledging the impact of COVID-19 sales in the previous year, the company is actively pursuing growth in diagnostics, including rapid antigen and NGS kits, and maintaining its debt-light agrochemical division. Significant progress is noted in the planned amalgamation with its subsidiary, 3B BlackBio, and the establishment of TRUPCR Europe Limited. The company is also exploring inorganic growth opportunities and potential share buybacks.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Post-COVID Normalization:** The company is observing a gradual pick-up in non-COVID sales as COVID-19 infections subside. FY2020-21 is explicitly excluded from comparable analysis due to its COVID-centric sales.
*   **Diagnostics Portfolio Expansion:**
    *   **Rapid Antigen Test Kits:** In advanced stages of development for COVID-19 and other parameters. Manufacturing facility under construction. Expected launch in FY22-23, with a focus on global markets due to lower Indian price realization.
    *   **Next Generation Sequencing (NGS):** Activities are being revived, including revalidations and development of new parameters. NGS is expected to start contributing revenue in "later quarters of FY22-23 only" due to unremunerative pricing in India.
    *   **qPCR Diagnostic Kits:** Continued development and refinement of existing products.
*   **Agro-chemical Division Focus:** Continues to prioritize Public Health, Warehousing, and Exports. Management is cautious about increasing financial exposure to the private agriculture market due to COVID-19 related uncertainty. The division operates on a debt-light, cash-flow positive model. Increased focus on brand building for Micro and Bio fertilizers, and Public Health Products. Bank borrowing has been brought to zero as of September 30, 2021, with plans for short-term borrowing below ₹1 Cr if needed.
*   **Global Expansion (TRUPCR Europe Limited):** The acquisition of a 70% stake in HS Biolabs Ltd. (now TRUPCR Europe) is a strategic move to offer "Made in UK" products, accelerate R&D and manufacturing in the UK, increase global brand image, and ensure faster supplies to European customers. Significant investment will be directed towards setting up R&D and production facilities, expanding sales & marketing teams, and obtaining necessary accreditations. Expected to realize major benefits in 2022.
*   **Inorganic Growth & Share Buyback:** Actively seeking acquisition candidates in similar business areas for FY22 onwards, but only if financially sensible and meeting internal IRR criteria. A share buyback in FY22/23 is being considered if a suitable acquisition is not secured. The search for suitable businesses is ongoing.
*   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** The scheme of arrangement/merger is progressing. BSE has requested clarifications, and after fresh approval, applications will be made to SEBI and NCLT. The process is expected to take approximately one year to complete, aiming to unlock the value of 3B BlackBio. Following completion, the company plans to list on the National Stock Exchange (NSE).
*   **R&D and Quality:** Continued investment in R&D with specialized teams and laboratory infrastructure (BSL 2, Clean Rooms). Stringent QC protocols for all TRUPCR® products, with kits QC-checked on customer's real-time PCR cyclers to guarantee performance.
