## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited hosted its Q1 FY26 earnings call, focusing on the Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) segment and the recent acquisition of Coris Bioconcept SRL. The company reported a 18% year-on-year increase in Q1 FY26 MDx sales to INR 19.96 crores, with EBITDA growing 11% to INR 16.19 crores (or 17% excluding one-time M&A expenses). Management highlighted their leading market position in India's MDx sector (12-15% share of a INR 350-450 crore TAM) and reiterated their target to grow at 15-20% in FY25-26, outpacing the projected industry CAGR of 8-10%. The acquisition of Coris Bioconcept SRL, a Belgian manufacturer of rapid diagnostic solutions, was a key discussion point. While Coris had a revenue of EUR 5.22 million and a strong gross margin (58.7%) in FY24, it reported a slight EBITDA loss of EUR 0.215 million. The company expects Coris to become EBITDA positive in FY26-27, driven by geographical expansion, cross-selling opportunities, and the growth of its Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR) product line. The company continues to actively pursue M&A opportunities globally, particularly in the IVD segment.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Agrochemicals (low priority, stable, not significantly growing) and Molecular Diagnostics (primary focus).
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Leadership & Growth:** Maintain market leadership in India and grow at 15-20% in FY25-26, exceeding industry CAGR.
    *   **Geographical Expansion:** Expand reach through existing channels and new partnerships, particularly in Europe and leveraging the Coris acquisition for new territories.
    *   **Portfolio Diversification:** Continuous development of qPCR, Lateral Flow, and NGS assays. Progress on Digital PCR (dPCR) with a targeted Q3 FY25-26 launch. Launch of new NGS panels (PAN-MYELOID, BRCA Plus, Endometrial Cancer) and AMR-focused RT-PCR and lateral flow assays.
    *   **Acquisition Synergies (Coris):** Leverage Coris's AMR product line, Belgium base, and R&D pipeline. Cross-sell PCR kits through Coris's network and vice-versa. Aim for EBITDA positivity in Coris by FY26-27.
    *   **M&A Focus:** Actively seeking global IVD acquisitions in the EU and USA.
    *   **Reagent Rental Model:** Expected to contribute 20-25% of revenue (FY25-26 and beyond).
    *   **Technology Relevance:** Developing assays on NGS and dPCR to remain technologically advanced.
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:** Maintain stable, low-margin business, focusing on exports (Bangladesh), government tenders, and existing long-term customers. Open to divesting if a suitable opportunity arises.
