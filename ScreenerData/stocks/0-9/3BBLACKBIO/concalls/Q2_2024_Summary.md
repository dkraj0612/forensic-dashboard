## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited, operating under Kilpest India Limited, continues to demonstrate robust growth primarily driven by its Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) segment, with a notable contribution from its UK subsidiary, TRUPCR® Europe Limited. The company reported strong Q2 FY 2024-25 financial performance for the diagnostics division, with revenues of INR 2,457.55 Lakhs and EBITDA of INR 1,929.17 Lakhs. The Agrochemical division remains stable, focusing on government tenders and brand building, though exports to Bangladesh face uncertainty due to the political climate. The company has successfully implemented its amalgamation and is preparing for an NSE listing. Key strategic priorities include expanding the global footprint, diversifying the product portfolio with new qPCR, NGS, and dPCR assays, and exploring inorganic growth opportunities through M&A.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Agrochemicals and Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Dx Limited).
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Continued focus on government procurement via e-tenders (GeM).
    *   Expansion of customer base into new regions.
    *   Intensified efforts in brand recognition for micro fertilizers, bio fertilizers, and public health products.
    *   Sales remain stable and the business operates with minimal debt.
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio Dx Limited) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration & Geographical Expansion:** Bolstering sales with existing customers and channel partners by expanding product portfolios. Penetrating new laboratory markets and untapped territories through direct engagement or distributors. TRUPCR® assays accepted in over 60 countries. Appointing new channel partners globally, with ongoing product registrations. Securing annual rate contracts through international tenders. Exploring partnerships in Africa, LATAM, APAC, and the Middle East.
    *   **Portfolio Expansion:** Enhancing product offerings in qPCR and NGS, and developing Digital PCR (dPCR) technology. Launched new NGS panels (PAN-MYELOID NGS Panel, BRCA Plus Kit, Endometrial Cancer Panel Kit). Developing RT-PCR and lateral flow assays for Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR). Offering comprehensive panels for syndromic testing. Automated extraction systems sourced for OEM sales/reagent-rental.
    *   **TRUPCR® Europe Limited (UK Subsidiary):** Continued growth at the Manchester Site with a projected growth rate of 40-50% this fiscal year. Integrated sales, marketing, technical support, and R&D functions to reduce TAT and enhance customer support. "Made in UK" manufacturing as a key selling point for EU customers. Increased visibility through participation in international conferences, leading to distributorship and OEM inquiries. Ongoing validation processes in new labs.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Amalgamation is complete. The company will review eligibility criteria and formalities for an NSE listing post-completion.
    *   **Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A):** Exploring strategically targeted M&A for inorganic growth, contingent on financial viability and meeting internal IRR criteria.
    *   **Long-Term Contracts:** Over 15 contracts in place (Reagent-Rental Model) projected to contribute 20-25% of business for the current and coming years.
    *   **Expansion and Additional Capacity:** Continuous investment in building additional manufacturing and R&D facilities/capacities.
    *   **Sales Models:** Utilizing Tender Sales (5-7%), Reagent Rental Model (20-25%), and Other Supplies (65-70%).
    *   **Credit Policy:** Maintaining a liberal credit policy to sustain competition, leading to a higher debtor level which is being managed.
