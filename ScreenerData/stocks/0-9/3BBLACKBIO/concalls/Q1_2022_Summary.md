## Executive Overview

This Q1 FY23 earnings transcript for Kilpest India Limited and 3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited highlights a period of transition as the company navigates the post-pandemic landscape. While acknowledging the significantly higher, non-comparable results of Q1 FY21 due to COVID-19 driven sales and higher pricing, the company emphasizes its efforts to maintain brand leadership in RT-PCR. The transcript indicates potential production/sales losses in Q1 FY22 and static agrochemical sales due to nationwide lockdowns. Management remains focused on the Public Health/Warehousing and Exports sectors for agrochemicals, avoiding increased financial exposure in the uncertain private agriculture market. The company is actively pursuing inorganic growth through acquisitions and considering a share buyback. Progress continues on the amalgamation with 3B BlackBio, with an expected unlocking of subsidiary value and a subsequent NSE listing. R&D remains a core focus, with advancements in Rapid Antigen and Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS) verticals.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Diagnostics Focus:** Maintaining brand leadership in RT-PCR market despite challenges. Developing Rapid Antigen Test Kits for COVID-19 and other parameters, with new manufacturing facilities being built. Advanced stages of development for Rapid Test Kits.
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:** Static sales in Q1 FY22 due to lockdowns. Continued focus on Public Health/Warehousing and Exports. Deliberate avoidance of increased financial exposure in the private agriculture market due to uncertainty. Emphasis on brand building for Micro/Bio fertilizers and Public Health Products. Developing novel, less harmful, and cost-effective formulations.
*   **International Expansion:** Exports were significantly impacted by restrictions in Q1 FY22, but the company aims to recover and grow with potential removal of restrictions. TRUPCR® is a global brand present in over 20 countries.
*   **Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS):** Development work has restarted after COVID-19 slowdown. Expected commercial launch of NGS assays in the next 2-3 quarters.
*   **Inorganic Growth:** Actively seeking acquisitions in similar business areas if financially viable and meets internal IRR criteria. Share buyback is being considered for FY22/23 if acquisition targets are not secured.
*   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Scheme of Amalgamation vetted by BSE. Working on faster NCLT approval to unlock the value of 3B BlackBio Biotech India Ltd. Post-completion, the company plans to initiate listing on the National Stock Exchange (NSE).
*   **R&D and Quality:** Continuous investment in R&D for new and improved products and processes. Maintaining stringent Quality Control (QC) protocols for all TRUPCR® products.
*   **Dividend:** Recommended a final dividend of 10% for FY20-21 and declared a special interim dividend of 80% for FY21-22, totaling 90% per share.
