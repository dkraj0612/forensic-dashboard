## Executive Overview

Kilpest India Limited, through its subsidiary 3B BlackBio, continues to balance its legacy Agrochemical business with its growth-oriented Molecular Diagnostics division. The current earnings transcript (Q3 FY 22-23) highlights continued revenue growth in diagnostics, albeit with a significant decrease in COVID-related sales. The company is actively pursuing international expansion, with its UK subsidiary, TRUPCR Europe Limited, making strides towards operational readiness. Amalgamation proceedings with the NCLT are in the final stages, paving the way for a potential NSE listing. The company has also re-evaluated potential acquisitions due to high valuations and competitive pressures in the life sciences sector, and is considering a share buyback as an alternative for shareholder value creation.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Dual Business Focus:** Kilpest continues its dual strategy, maintaining its 50-year-old Agrochemical business while aggressively growing its Molecular Diagnostics subsidiary, 3B BlackBio.
*   **Agrochemicals Strategy:**
    *   Focus on Government business (GeM/Tenders), Public Health, Warehousing, and Exports.
    *   Development of novel, less harmful, and cost-effective formulations for agriculture and public health.
    *   Emphasis on brand building for Micro fertilizers, Bio fertilizers, and Public Health Products.
    *   Sales in Q2 were impacted by excessive monsoon, delaying agricultural activities. The division is now cash-flow positive with low debt.
*   **Molecular Diagnostics (3B BlackBio) Strategy:**
    *   **Market Penetration:** Increasing sales with existing customers/channel partners by expanding product portfolios and reaching new labs post-pandemic.
    *   **Geographical Expansion:** Expanding into new markets directly or via distributors/channel partners. Exports are a key focus, with recent traction in LATAM countries and ongoing registration processes in high-demand countries.
    *   **Product Portfolio Expansion:** Launching new products in Rapid Antigen, qPCR Diagnostic Kits, and NGS verticals. Over 100 tests developed, with more in the pipeline.
    *   **R&D Investment:** Continuous investment in R&D for new product development and optimization, especially in Rapid Kits and NGS.
    *   **International Operations (TRUPCR Europe Limited):**
        *   Relocated to a new, larger facility in Manchester with dedicated labs for Production, QC, and R&D.
        *   Working towards ISO 13485:2016 certification from BSI UK, with commercial production expected to commence in the UK.
        *   Focus on reducing Turn-Around-Time (TAT) for deliveries and enhancing technical support due to a dedicated team in the same time zone.
        *   Increased visibility through participation in international conferences, leading to distributorship and OEM inquiries.
    *   **Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A):** Actively seeking selective M&A for inorganic growth, but valuations remain high globally. Future acquisitions will be pursued only if financially sensible and meeting internal IRR criteria.
    *   **Amalgamation & NSE Listing:** Proceedings with NCLT are in the final stages, with completion expected in a few months, followed by listing on the NSE.
