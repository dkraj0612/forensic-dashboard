## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited reported strong performance for FY2026, with consolidated revenue reaching approximately INR 142 crores and Profit After Tax (PAT) at nearly INR 60 crores. The company saw healthy growth across its diagnostics business, benefiting from contributions from TRUPCR Europe and the recently acquired Coris BioConcept. The 3B India molecular diagnostics (MDx) segment achieved INR 86.47 crores in revenue, representing approximately 10% growth year-on-year. Management attributed this growth rate to facing exceptionally high demand in the prior year for flu and vector-borne disease diagnostics, alongside geopolitical impacts on exports. TRUPCR Europe continued its growth trajectory, generating GBP 1.75 million (INR 20.67 crores) in sales. Coris BioConcept, consolidated since August 2025, contributed INR 35.91 crores in revenue and INR 8.76 crores in PAT over seven months, notably boosted by a profitable one-time HAT order. Looking ahead, management is optimistic, projecting a 15%-20% revenue growth for FY2026-27, with strategic focuses on global expansion, securing regulatory approvals (IVDR, US FDA), and continued R&D investment.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Core Diagnostics Business (MDx):**
    *   **Market Focus:** Expanding presence in India (government and private labs), international markets (Europe, LATAM, Africa, APAC, Middle East), and pursuing US market entry.
    *   **Product Strategy:** Continued focus on qPCR (TRUPCR®), expanding NGS (TRUNGS®) and dPCR offerings, developing rapid AMR tests (TRURAPID®), and introducing an Automated Extraction System for reagent-rental models.
    *   **Growth Drivers:** Increasing adoption of molecular testing, government healthcare initiatives, expansion in export markets, and long-term reagent-rental contracts.
*   **Coris BioConcept Integration & US FDA:**
    *   **Strategic Focus:** Successful integration of Coris BioConcept, with a primary objective of securing US FDA approval for selected AMR products.
    *   **US Market Access:** US FDA clearance is expected to unlock significant growth in the US diagnostics market. Initial US AMR market contribution is targeted at INR 11-16 crores (EUR 1-1.5 million), with aspirations to grow this segment to 20% of Coris's revenue.
*   **Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A):**
    *   **Strategy:** Actively seeking strategic global M&A opportunities, particularly in the EU and USA, targeting companies with a minimum topline of $2 million in growing sectors, open to turnaround or EBITDA-positive entities.
*   **Technology & Quality:**
    *   **Regulatory Compliance:** Progressing with IVDR transition in Europe, with QMS audits completed and technical documentation review underway.
*   **Financial & Operational:**
    *   **Capacity Utilization:** Operating at approximately 65% of installed capacity, providing ample room for demand surges.
    *   **Reagent-Rental Model:** Aiming for 20%-25% of total revenue from reagent-rental models in the coming years.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Previous Commitments:

*   **FY2026 Revenue & PAT:** Management reported consolidated revenue of ~INR 142 crores and PAT of ~INR 60 crores, aligning with expectations for a strong year, driven by Coris integration and the performance of existing segments.
*   **3B India MDx Growth:** Management reported 10% growth in 3B India's MDx business. This is stated as slightly lower than projections due to exceptional demand in FY25 for flu/dengue/chikungunya and geopolitical impacts on exports, which aligns with previously stated headwinds and external factors. The standalone non-export revenue growth was stated as 7.1%, contributing to an overall consolidated growth of around 10% for FY26.
*   **TRUPCR Europe Growth:** Sales grew to INR 20.67 crores from INR 13.9 crores in the previous year, indicating significant growth consistent with stated global expansion priorities.
*   **Coris Acquisition & Integration:** The acquisition was completed in August 2025, and its revenue and PAT contribution for the seven months of consolidation were reported. The narrative confirms active integration and the pursuit of US FDA clearance for Coris products.
*   **Product Development & Launch:** The commercial launch of dPCR products in Q3 FY2025-26 was noted, with initial nominal sales. The development of rapid AMR tests (TRURAPID®) is progressing, with initial orders received. The Sample-to-Answer system is in validation and expected for offering in 1-2 quarters.
*   **M&A Strategy:** Management confirmed the Coris acquisition as part of their M&A strategy and continues to actively pursue opportunities, particularly in the EU and USA, with advanced discussions ongoing.
*   **US Market Entry (Coris):** The US FDA submission process for selected AMR products is ongoing, with expected clearance in 1-2 years. Direct sales are not yet established but are a key forward-looking initiative.
