## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (the "Company") presented its Q1 FY18 results, highlighting a stable operational environment with no severe behavioral manipulation detected. The company operates in two primary segments: Agro-inputs and Healthcare Diagnostics. The Agro-input business, a 44-year-old formulation company, has a strong presence in Central India and focuses on bio-pesticides, bio-fungicides, bio-fertilizers, and bio-nematicides. The company aims to increase brand building efforts for micro/bio-fertilizers and public health products, leveraging its R&D recognition and expanding distribution network. The Healthcare Diagnostics segment focuses on molecular diagnostics, offering a wide range of proprietary kits for infectious diseases, oncology, and genetics, with a strategy centered on in-house R&D, import substitution, and continuous development of new assays. The company added new customers in Q1 FY18 and has new assays in the pipeline.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

**Agro-Input Segment:**
*   **Brand Building:** Increased focus on micro/bio-fertilizers and public health products.
*   **Market Penetration:** Leverage established brand equity and strong distribution in Central India (Madhya Pradesh and Chhattisgarh).
*   **Product Development:** Continuous development of bio-fertilizers and bio-pesticides to capitalize on the emphasis on organic farming.
*   **Public Health:** Maintain and expand its role as a trusted supplier to government departments for public health pesticides.
*   **GST Impact:** Expects GST implementation to provide a boost to the company.

**Healthcare Diagnostics Segment:**
*   **In-house R&D:** Proprietary kit design, development, and validation.
*   **Market Expansion:** Pan-India acceptance of assays and continuous development of new assays for rapid growth.
*   **Import Substitution:** Offer price advantages and timely supplies.
*   **Product Portfolio:** Wide range of diagnostic kits covering infectious diseases, oncology, and genetics.
*   **International Quality:** Continuous R&D initiatives for international quality products.
*   **Human Resources:** Strengthening of research and marketing teams.
*   **Market Growth Drivers:** Capitalize on the growing Indian diagnostics industry, increased focus on advanced diagnostics, changing disease profiles, and the demand for lifestyle disease-related healthcare.
