## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Biotech India Limited (formerly Kilpest India Limited) continues its determined strategic pivot towards the high-growth diagnostics sector, with a strong emphasis on its TRUPCR® molecular diagnostics brand. Q4 FY20 saw the diagnostics segment achieve significant traction, driven by its rapid response to the COVID-19 pandemic. The company successfully launched and scaled production of its TRUPCR® SARS-CoV-2 RT-qPCR Kits, receiving crucial approvals from ICMR and CDSCO, and notably became the first Indian molecular diagnostics company to receive US FDA Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) in June 2020. This positioned the company to secure substantial government orders for COVID-19 testing kits and viral RNA extraction kits post-Q4.

The agro-input segment, while reporting flat agricultural sales for FY20, continued its strategic shift towards public health and warehousing sectors, along with increased focus on exports, mitigating the uncertainties in the private agriculture market due to COVID-19. Financial stability was bolstered by substantial debt reduction from a preferential issue, with plans to further reduce working capital limits. The company is actively expanding its domestic and international presence for diagnostics and investing in advanced R&D, including Next Generation Sequencing (NGS).

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **COVID-19 Response & Market Leadership:** Rapid development, ICMR/CDSCO approvals, and launch of TRUPCR® SARS-CoV-2 RT-qPCR Kits (V2.0 & V3.2) including a single-tube assay and Viral RNA Extraction Kit. Securing large government orders through HITES demonstrates strong capability and market penetration during the pandemic.
*   **Global Recognition & Market Access:** Achieving US FDA Emergency Use Authorization (EUA) for its COVID-19 RT-qPCR Kit on June 19, 2020, significantly enhances global credibility and opens avenues for international market expansion.
*   **Advanced R&D & Product Portfolio:** Continued focus on in-house R&D, including the development of Next Generation Sequencing (NGS) panels and a dedicated NGS facility ("Launching soon"). The TRUPCR® brand covers oncology, infectious diseases, women's health, and human genetics.
*   **Quality & Operational Excellence:** Emphasis on stringent QC protocols, ISO 13485:2016 certification, and ensuring product compatibility with various real-time PCR cyclers at customer sites.
*   **Domestic & International Market Penetration:** Expansion to over 180 customers in 50+ cities domestically. Active exports to Bangladesh, approvals in Afghanistan, Nepal, Jordan, and ongoing product validation/orders in East Africa, UK, and USA for TRUPCR® kits.
*   **Strategic Contracts & Tender Business:** Ongoing long-term contracts with large customers for assured revenue (6 contracts securing ₹ 50-60 Million annually). TRUPCR® H1N1 Kit approval by NIV, Pune, opens government tender opportunities, complemented by participation in various government tenders with COVID-19 kits.
*   **Agro-Input Strategic Shift:** De-emphasis on the uncertain private agriculture market due to COVID-19, with continued focus on public health products, warehousing, and exports. Development of bio-fertilizers and bio-pesticides aligning with organic farming trends.
*   **Financial Prudence:** Substantial debt reduction through preferential issue and a commitment to further reduce working capital limits.
