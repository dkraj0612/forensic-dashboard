## Executive Overview

3B BlackBio Dx Limited's disclosed financial results for FY 2024-25, presented on May 28, 2025, highlight a year of mixed performance and continued strategic development. The company reported Revenue from Operations of INR 8,428.25 Lakh and EBITDA of INR 6,507.13 Lakh for FY 2023-24. For FY 2024-25, the company reported Revenue from Operations of INR 9,646.86 Lakh, Profit Before Tax of INR 6,399.82 Lakh, and Profit After Tax of INR 55.66 Lakh. The presentation indicates a quarter-on-quarter decline in Q4 FY25 revenue compared to Q4 FY24, attributed to the expected seasonal drop in infectious disease diagnostics (flu, dengue) post-Q3 and a shift in some TRUPCR Europe orders. Despite this, the company emphasizes its strong market position in India's Molecular Diagnostics (MDx) sector, holding a 12-15% share of an INR 350-450 Cr TAM, and expects to grow at 15-20% in FY26. Global expansion remains a key focus, with exports to over 70 countries and TRUPCR Europe showing significant growth (over 90% in FY25), projecting a 20-25% growth in FY26. The company is actively pursuing M&A opportunities, having evaluated several targets and now seeing more favorable valuations. R&D remains a priority, with advancements in Next-Gen Sequencing (NGS) and development of Digital PCR (dPCR) assays slated for launch in H1 FY26, alongside an Automated Extraction System. The Agrochemicals division continues to operate with a stable, minimal-debt model, focusing on government tenders and exports to Bangladesh.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Core Diagnostics Business (MDx):**
    *   **Market Focus:** Infectious diseases (syndromic testing), oncology, Antimicrobial Resistance (AMR).
    *   **Product Strategy:** Expanding comprehensive panel offerings in qPCR (TRUPCR®), introducing new NGS assays (TRUNGS®), and launching rapid AMR tests (TRURAPID®). Development of dPCR assays for commercial launch in H1 FY26. Introduction of an Automated Extraction System.
    *   **India Market:** Maintaining leadership through broad product portfolio, strong customer base, and leveraging R&D strengths. Expecting 15-20% growth in FY26.
    *   **Global Expansion:** Continued focus on expanding presence in 70+ countries, with TRUPCR Europe as a key driver. Targeting new partnerships in Africa, LATAM, APAC, and the Middle East.
    *   **R&D and Product Development:** Continuous pipeline expansion for qPCR, Lateral Flow, and NGS assays. Focus on assay sensitivity, specificity, and workflow efficiency. Developing dPCR and aiming for "sample to answer" solutions.
*   **Agrochemicals Division:**
    *   **Market Focus:** Government procurement (GeM tenders) for vector-borne disease control and exports (primarily Bangladesh).
    *   **Strategy:** Stable sales, minimal debt, supplying to old customers, government tenders, and exports. Focus on developing less harmful and cost-effective formulations.
*   **Mergers and Acquisitions (M&A):**
    *   **Strategy:** Actively seeking strategic M&A opportunities in the IVD space. Evaluating targets based on valuations, IRR, and future growth potential. Aiming to get a desired acquisition candidate in the near future as valuations cool down.
*   **Technology & Quality:**
    *   **Certifications:** ISO 13485:2016, GMP compliant manufacturing, CE-IVD marking for over 120 parameters, licensed by CDSCO.
    *   **Innovation:** Leveraging R&D for new assay development, including dPCR. Offering integrated solutions from Rapid Diagnostics to NGS.
*   **Financial Stability:**
    *   **Dividend:** Recommended final dividend of 40% (INR 4/- per share) for FY 2024-25, subject to shareholder approval.
    *   **CSR:** Continued support for Army Wives Welfare Association (AWWA), ARUSHI, Sightsavers, and Friends of Tribals Society (FTS).
