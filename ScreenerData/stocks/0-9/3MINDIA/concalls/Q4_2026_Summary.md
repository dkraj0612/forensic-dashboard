## Executive Overview

3M India Limited, in its presentation on May 27, 2026, reported strong financial performance for the fiscal year 2025-26, with consolidated sales reaching ₹5,090 crores, representing a significant 14.5% growth over FY2024-25. This growth was broad-based across its business groups, with Health Care, Transportation & Electronics, Safety & Industrial, and Consumer segments all posting double-digit increases. Profitability also saw an improvement, with Profit Before Tax (PBT) before exceptional items reaching 18.7% of sales, an increase of 1.6 percentage points year-over-year. After exceptional items, PBT stood at 17.4% of sales, a 0.3 percentage point increase. Earnings Per Share (EPS) grew by 9.7% year-over-year. The company highlighted its strategic focus on invigorating growth through commercial excellence, strengthening local R&D capabilities for "for-India" products, expanding its local supply chain, and fostering a culture of bold aspiration and performance within its talent base. External recognitions for performance, innovation, and sustainability underscore the company's operational achievements and commitment to responsible business practices.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Growth Pillars:**
    *   **Commercial Excellence:** Focus on strengthening sales pipelines, winning customer specifications, and delivering best-in-class customer experience and service. This includes category penetration and driving "Body Shop penetration."
    *   **Local R&D Capability:** Developing "for-India" new products aligned with priority markets and leveraging global New Product Introductions (NPIs) for the Indian market. This includes work on acoustic materials and finishing solutions.
    *   **Local Supply Chain:** Strengthening outsourcing and contract manufacturing capabilities, alongside capacity expansion and new manufacturing lines for selected product portfolios. Examples include acoustic materials, ear plug cording, and pipe coating.
    *   **Talent & Organization:** Cultivating a culture of "bold aspiration, speed & performance" and building capability and depth across the organization.
*   **Market Focus & Innovation Examples:**
    *   **Automotive:** "Finishing Solutions for Knee Joints," materials for new automotive models (e.g., acoustic panels), and localization of "absorber" for Tata Motors.
    *   **Infrastructure:** Touchproof terminations in solar and wind power projects, materials for airports (interiors and exteriors).
    *   **Consumer:** Strong growth in the consumer segment (20% mix).
*   **Operational Strengths:**
    *   Leveraging global 3M network, including 3 manufacturing sites and an R&D Center in Bangalore.
    *   Commitment to sustainability, recognized by Businessworld's Top 40 Most Sustainable Companies and CSR award for environmental protection.
    *   Strong supplier awards from major automotive players (Maruti Suzuki, Toyota Kirloskar Motors, Tata Motors) and recognition as an "Innovation Partner of the Year" from BSES Delhi.
