## Executive Overview

This document synthesizes key insights from 3M India's FY 2023-24 Results Summary presentation, delivered on May 31, 2024. The company reported solid consolidated financial performance for FY 2023-24, demonstrating revenue growth of 5.8% to INR 4189 crores. Profitability saw significant improvement, with EBITDA growing 24.5% to INR 837 crores (19.6% margin) and PAT increasing by 29.4% to INR 583 crores (13.7% margin). This margin expansion was primarily attributed to a 230bps reduction in material costs. The presentation highlighted operational strengths, including strong cash generation (114% of PAT) and customer recognition for contributions in areas like sustainability and supplier performance. Management emphasized alignment with India's macrotrends, particularly in automotive, electronics, infrastructure, and sustainability, positioning the company for attractive short and medium-term growth opportunities.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Consolidated FY 2023-24 Results:**
    *   **Revenue:** INR 4189 crores, +5.8% growth year-over-year (vs. PY).
    *   **EBITDA:** INR 837 crores, +24.5% growth. EBITDA margin was 19.6% (+230bps driven by material cost reduction).
    *   **Profit After Tax (PAT):** INR 583 crores, +29.4% growth. PAT margin was 13.7%.
    *   **Cash Generation:** INR 663 crores (pre-dividend), 114% of PAT.
    *   **EPS (consolidated):** INR 517.90/share, +29.4% increase over PY.

*   **Business Group Sales Mix & Growth (FY 2023-24 Consolidated):**
    *   **Health Care:** INR 1333 cr., 32% mix, +2.5% growth.
    *   **Transportation & Electronics:** INR 1649 cr., 39% mix, +5.1% growth.
    *   **Safety & Industrial:** INR 761 cr., 18% mix, +16.2% growth.
    *   **Consumer:** INR 428 cr., 10% mix, +4.5% growth.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Growth Focus Areas:**
    *   Leveraging India's macrotrends: Infrastructure development (roads, railways, metros, airports), automotive sector (including electrification), and the growing electronics industry.
    *   Sustainability and Climate Tech: Aligning with government initiatives for Net Zero emissions and growth in renewable energy (solar, wind) and green hydrogen.
    *   Digitalization: Tracking data center growth and supporting the development of a semiconductor manufacturing ecosystem.
*   **Market Opportunities:**
    *   **Automotive:** Differentiated "for India" products, localization of films, EV evolution, and capitalizing on increasing SUV production.
    *   **Infrastructure Penetration:** Targeting corporate identity programs.
    *   **Cleaning & Workplace Safety:** Focusing on industrial tapes and abrasives.
    *   **Electrical Markets:** Expanding offerings in this segment.
*   **Innovation & Operations:**
    *   R&D Center in Bangalore supporting customer interactions and product development.
    *   Manufacturing facilities like Ranjangaon Plant contributing to operations.
    *   Commitment to developing solutions for a cleaner world.
*   **Customer Recognition:** Acknowledged customer awards from Tata Power-DDL (sustainability), Asahi Glass, Tenneco, and Suzuki Motors Gujarat, highlighting strong supplier performance and quality.
*   **Diversity & Inclusion:** Recognition of the Legal team for championing diversity initiatives.
