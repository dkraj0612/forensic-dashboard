## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital Limited (5paisa) reported a strong Q1 FY2022, marked by record customer acquisition and sequential revenue growth. The company acquired 278,000 customers, a 35% increase from the previous quarter, with June alone seeing over 100,000 new clients. Despite increased acquisition, the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) decreased by 9% to Rs. 813, largely due to improved digital onboarding (60% DIY accounts) which reduced operational expenses by 37%. Revenue grew 22% sequentially, driven by customer growth and increased market activity. The company has successfully concluded its fundraising efforts, with the Rs. 250 crore capital infusion primarily allocated to technology and customer acquisition. Management emphasized a continued focus on evolving into a multi-product digital financial platform, enhancing technology, and improving user experience.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **New Customers Acquired (Q1 FY2022):** 278,000 (+35% sequentially)
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) (Q1 FY2022):** Rs. 813 (-9% sequentially, down from Rs. 897 in FY21)
*   **Digital Onboarding (DIY Accounts):** ~60%
*   **Operational CAC Reduction:** ~37%
*   **Revenue Growth (Q1 FY2022):** +22% sequentially
*   **Derivative Market Share (Retail):** ~3% (management is focused on improving this)
*   **Margin Funding Book:** Rs. 290-295 crores (end of Q1 FY2022), averaging Rs. 220 crores for the quarter.
*   **Technology Spend (Q1 FY2022):** ~Rs. 7 crores (excluding manpower cost) within "Other Expenses" of Rs. 17.7 crores.
*   **FY2021 Technology Cost (including manpower):** Rs. 25 crores out of Rs. 67 crores in "Other Expenses".
*   **Revenue Per Customer (R2) - Annualized:** ~Rs. 2,000
*   **Revenue Per Customer (R2) - Incremental:** ~Rs. 1,500
*   **EBITDA Margin:** ~24%-25%

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Multi-Product Digital Financial Platform:** Continued focus on expanding beyond discount broking to offer a comprehensive suite of financial products.
*   **Technology Investment:** Significant allocation of new capital towards strengthening the technology backbone, including infrastructure, data science, AI, and improving UI/UX for a better customer experience.
*   **Customer Acquisition:** Aggressive focus on acquiring new customers, with a goal to increase net client additions. Expectation that the 20% sequential active client base growth can be sustained or exceeded.
*   **Digital Onboarding:** Aim to achieve 100% digital, human-intervention-free account opening to further reduce Opex and CAC.
*   **Product Diversification:** Existing products include mutual funds, digital gold, P2P lending, and US stocks. Plans to add insurance, NPS, PPF, and Sovereign Gold Bonds.
*   **Customer Retention & Stickiness:** Continuous investment in product experience, UI/UX, and holistic investment journeys to retain customers, especially targeting the younger demographic and Tier-III town residents.
*   **Leveraging Niche Markets:** Targeting younger demographics (under 35) and Tier-III town residents, with an expectation that their per capita income and investment potential will grow over time.
*   **Sub-broker/Influencer Ecosystem:** While not a significant portion of their business, 5paisa engages with influencers and shares 12.5% of brokerage revenues with partners.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

**Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance (Q1 FY2022):**

*   **Record Customer Acquisition:** Management achieved a record customer acquisition of 278,000, surpassing previous quarters and demonstrating consistency with their growth-oriented strategy.
*   **CAC Reduction:** Successfully reduced CAC by 9% to Rs. 813, which is lower than the previous quarter's Rs. 897, aligning with the stated focus on efficient customer acquisition.
*   **Improved Digital Onboarding:** Achieved ~60% DIY account openings, which directly contributed to the operational CAC reduction of 37%, fulfilling the narrative of improving digital journeys.
*   **Sequential Revenue Growth:** Delivered 22% sequential revenue growth, indicating a positive operational performance driven by customer acquisition and market activity, consistent with growth objectives.
*   **Technology Investment:** Management explicitly stated that a significant portion of the newly raised capital is being invested in technology and infrastructure, which is reflected in the increased "other expenses" driven by technology costs. This aligns with prior guidance.
*   **Derivative Market Share:** While acknowledging a dip in derivative market share from a prior period (3.96% mentioned historically), management stated efforts are underway to improve this, with recent gains in June. This indicates a potential area of focus rather than a complete failure to meet prior levels.

**New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance for Next Quarter/Year:**

*   **Continued CAC Improvement:** Management believes there is scope for further CAC reduction through organic and referral channels, with a target payback period of 8-9 months for the entire CAC.
*   **Sustained Client Acquisition Growth:** Expectation to maintain or exceed 20% sequential active client base growth for at least a few quarters.
*   **Increased Net Client Additions:** Aiming to increase net client additions significantly, driven by the recent capital raise and a more aggressive acquisition strategy.
*   **Technology & Product Enhancement:** Ongoing investment in technology, including infrastructure, data science, AI, and continuous UI/UX improvements, is a priority.
*   **Margin Funding Book Growth:** Expects the margin funding book to grow organically with customer acquisition and trading activity.
*   **EBITDA Margin Outlook:** EBITDA margin is expected to be "subdued for some time" due to increased investment in customer acquisition and expansion following the capital raise, but operating leverage advantage is anticipated in 3-4 quarters.
*   **Platform Expansion:** Continued focus on adding new products such as insurance, NPS, PPF, and Sovereign Gold Bonds.
*   **Derivative Market Share Improvement:** Positive outlook on improving derivative market share through product enhancements and new features.
*   **Sub-broker/Influencer Model:** Continues to leverage influencers and provides 12.5% of brokerage revenues as payouts, with brokerage reported net of these expenses.
*   **Break-even for New Customers:** A payback period of 8-9 months is expected for the entire CAC, based primarily on brokerage and MTF revenues.
