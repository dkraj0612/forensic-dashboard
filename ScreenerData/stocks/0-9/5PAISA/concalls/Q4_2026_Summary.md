## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported its Q4 FY2026 results amidst a backdrop of heightened global geopolitical and macroeconomic uncertainty, including rising Brent crude prices and a weakening rupee. Despite this volatility, the Indian capital markets demonstrated resilience with strong domestic institutional and retail participation. While overall Demat account additions saw a slight moderation, trading activity remained robust, with significant QoQ growth in both F&O premium average daily turnover (ADTO) and exchange retail cash ADTO.

5paisa Capital reported positive operational and financial performance for Q4 FY2026. The company focused on acquiring high-quality customers and enhancing its trading and investing platforms. Key achievements include a substantial increase in customer acquisition (over 1 lakh customers, a 33% QoQ rise), growth in the F&O premium segment, and an increase in the average client funding book. Broking revenue, aligned income, and total revenue all saw healthy QoQ growth. Profit after tax (PAT) saw a slight decline primarily due to increased investment in product, technology, and AI teams. The company also successfully completed an oversubscribed rights issue, raising INR 468 crores, which significantly strengthened its net worth to over INR 1,100 crores.

Investments in technology and product development continued, with the launch of several new trading tools such as Scalper, multi-chart setup, strategy builder, option chain, and alpha scan. Enhancements were made to API and partner ecosystems, and the MTF (Margin Trading Facility) product was expanded. The company also introduced its AI trading companion, 5paisa MCP, in previous quarters and improved its functionality for analysis and execution via chat.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **High-Quality Customer Acquisition:** Focus on acquiring customers with high Lifetime Value (LTV) and deep engagement in trading.
*   **Trading Stack Enhancement:** Deepening capabilities in the trading platform with advanced tools for experienced traders.
*   **Investing Business Growth:** Expanding the wealth-building segment through products like mutual funds, ETFs, and MTF.
*   **Technology and AI Investment:** Continued investment in product, technology, and AI to improve trading experience, reduce latency, and enhance platform reliability.
*   **API and Partner Ecosystem Development:** Upgrading APIs and expanding the partner network to attract institutional participation.
*   **MTF Expansion:** Growing the MTF book as a key monetization driver and expanding eligible stock universe.
*   **Brand Development and Marketing:** Strengthening brand presence through targeted campaigns.
*   **Balance Sheet Strengthening:** Utilizing capital raised from the rights issue to support business expansion, market presence, and long-term growth.
