## Executive Overview

This transcript details the proposed acquisition of IIFL Securities Ltd's Online Retail Trading (ORT) business by 5paisa Capital Ltd, slated for April 1, 2023. The acquisition is expected to significantly scale up 5paisa's operations, increasing its customer base by approximately 1.52 million and its market share by number of customers from 3.2% to 4.5%. The transaction involves a share dilution of 16.58% for 5paisa and a swap ratio of 1:50. Management anticipates value unlocking for shareholders through cross-selling opportunities to the acquired customer base, revenue enhancement without substantial incremental costs, and improved profitability (PAT & ROE). While the transcript focuses on the acquisition, it also provides a snapshot of 5paisa's current business: INR 1,643 Mn in H1FY23 revenues, over INR 1.8 Tn ADTO from April to November 2022, 3.2 Mn+ total customers, and an improved app rating of 4.3 stars.
