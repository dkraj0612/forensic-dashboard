## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital concluded FY23 with a strong Q4, demonstrating significant revenue growth and a substantial improvement in profitability despite a challenging market characterized by subdued retail participation and slower Demat account growth. The company has successfully transitioned its strategy towards acquiring high-intent, quality trading customers, leading to a reduction in Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and payback periods. Investments in technology and product development, including the revamped Android app and the "FnO 360" terminal, have bolstered user experience and trading capabilities. The announced acquisition of IIFL Securities' online retail business is poised to significantly expand the client base, further driving growth in the upcoming fiscal year. Management is confident in achieving operating leverage and margin expansion as customer cohorts mature.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Revenue (FY23):** INR 339 crores (Up 14% YoY)
*   **Profit After Tax (PAT) (FY23):** INR 43.6 crores (Highest ever, up 216% YoY)
*   **PAT Margin (FY23):** 13% (Up from 5% in the previous year)
*   **Average Daily Turnover (ADTO) Total:** Improved by 146% YoY (March FY23 vs. March FY22)
*   **Derivative Market Share:** Increased to 3.1%
*   **Margin Funding Book Size (Average Q4 FY23):** INR 270 crores
*   **Other Operating Income (Treasury Income):** INR 18 crores (Q4 FY23)
*   **Employee Count (Digital Team):** Increased from 208 to 255

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Quality Customer Acquisition:** Strategic shift from mere customer volume to acquiring high-intent traders, resulting in reduced CAC and improved payback periods (6-7 months).
*   **Product & Technology Investment:** Revamped Android app (over 15 million downloads, 4.31+ rating) and launched "FnO 360" terminal for derivative traders.
*   **IIFL Securities Acquisition:** Appointment date of April 1, 2023, pending regulatory approvals, expected to add ~15 lakh customers.
*   **Operating Leverage:** Demonstrated ability to recover acquisition and servicing costs within the first year, with significant profit margin gains from the second year onwards, and over 50% EBITDA margins from customers after three years.
*   **Revenue Diversification:** Focus on broking revenue driven by derivatives, with plans to introduce lending and fixed income products.
*   **Market Share Growth:** Aiming for steady growth in derivative market share through product enhancements.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance

*   **Revenue Growth (FY23):** **Met Expectation.** Reported 14% YoY revenue growth, exceeding previous year's subdued market conditions.
*   **PAT Growth (FY23):** **Significantly Exceeded Expectation.** Achieved the highest ever PAT of INR 43.6 crores, a 216% YoY increase, surpassing previous growth targets.
*   **PAT Margin Expansion:** **Met Expectation.** Expanded PAT margins from 5% to 13%, reflecting improved operational efficiency and scale.
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** **Met Expectation (Trend).** While Q4 FY23 showed a slight increase due to fixed employee costs being spread over fewer acquisitions, management reiterated their sustainable range of INR 500-600 and a reduction from previous years.
*   **Payback Period Reduction:** **Met Expectation.** Reduced payback period from 8-10 months to 6-7 months, aligning with the "quality customer" strategy.
*   **Product Development (FnO 360):** **On Track.** Launched and gaining traction as a go-to platform for derivative traders.
*   **IIFL Securities Acquisition:** **On Track.** Acquisition completed with an appointment date of April 1, 2023, pending regulatory approvals.
*   **Operating Leverage:** **Met Expectation.** Management clearly articulated and demonstrated the realization of operating leverage through improved margins from mature customer cohorts.
*   **Cash Market vs. Derivative Focus:** Management's narrative consistently reflects a strong skew towards derivative revenue (75-80%), acknowledging industry shifts and regulatory impacts on cash market leverage.

### New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance for Next Quarter/Year

*   **IIFL Securities Acquisition:** Expected to be completed within FY24, subject to regulatory approvals.
*   **Customer Acquisition (FY24 Projection):** Expected to be in the range of 7.5 lakh to 10 lakh customers.
*   **Brokerage Rates:** Higher probability of increasing brokerage rates in the next couple of quarters due to rising compliance costs and potential regulatory changes impacting income streams.
*   **Cross-Sell Income Growth:** While currently not a primary focus (5% growth in FY23), plans include introducing lending and fixed income products.
*   **Margin Funding Book Growth:** Expected to increase as cash market turnover picks up.
*   **Steady State PBT Margin:** Management previously targeted 35-40% in the next 4-5 quarters, implying continued margin expansion from current levels.
*   **Working Capital Requirements:** Anticipates an additional requirement of INR 100-125 crore due to regulatory changes regarding bank guarantees on customer funds, with an estimated marginal cost increase of INR 4-5 crore.
*   **Customer Cohort Margins:** Expectation that margins will continue to improve for older customer cohorts (CY18, CY19, CY20) as they age and for newer cohorts (CY22 onwards) due to a focused approach on acquiring quality customers.
