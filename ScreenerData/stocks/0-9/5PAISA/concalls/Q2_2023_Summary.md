## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital's Q2 FY24 (quarter ended September 2023) demonstrates a significant rebound with strong growth across key metrics. The company reported a 25% increase in revenue and a 5% increase in Profit after Tax (PAT) QoQ. Customer acquisition also saw a healthy uptick of 25% QoQ, with 1.36 lakh new clients added. Notably, the Average Daily Turnover (ADTO) experienced substantial growth, particularly in derivatives, and the client base expanded to 3.73 million. Operational efficiency is highlighted by an improved STP acquisition rate of 87% and a focus on 100% paperless client activation. The company emphasizes its technological prowess with a high proportion of tech and product staff and a strong app rating, contributing to high customer satisfaction (75% NPS). The transcript also points to a successful optimization of Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) and a focus on acquiring high Lifetime Value (LTV) customers.
