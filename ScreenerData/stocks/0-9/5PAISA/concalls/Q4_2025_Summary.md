## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported Q4 FY2025 results in a challenging market environment marked by the full impact of SEBI F&O regulations and increased market volatility. These factors led to a significant decline in industry-wide ADTO and new customer acquisition. Despite these headwinds, the company achieved a YoY revenue decline of 9% to INR 360 crores for FY2025, but demonstrated improved profitability with a 25% YoY increase in profit to INR 68 crores, resulting in a margin expansion from 14% to 19%. The net worth crossed INR 600 crores by March 31, 2025. In Q4 FY2025, broking revenue declined 23% QoQ due to industry-wide drops in derivative volumes and a 11% reduction in MTF income following a decrease in MTF interest rates. Total expenses were optimized, falling 9% QoQ to INR 58 crores. The company acquired 91,000 new customers in Q4 FY2025, a 19% QoQ decline, bringing the total customer base to 48.3 lakhs. ADTO saw a 36% QoQ decline to INR 1.93 trillion, attributed to regulatory changes. The client funding book remained stable at INR 260 crores, and Mutual Fund AUM grew to INR 1,392 crores. Product and technology investments continued, with key rollouts including an F&O 360 upgrade, QTV live streaming, and the "IntraEdge" AI-powered recommendation tool. Significant improvements in order placement latency (15 milliseconds reduction) were achieved.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Technology and Product Enhancement:** Continued investment in digital platforms for superior trading and investing experience. Key initiatives include F&O 360 upgrade, QTV live streaming, "IntraEdge" AI-powered recommendations, and charting upgrades.
*   **Performance Optimization:** Achieved a 15-millisecond reduction in order placement latency to boost execution speed and platform reliability.
*   **Customer Acquisition Focus:** Emphasis on acquiring high-quality customers with healthy Lifetime Value (LTV) and sustainable payback periods.
*   **Cost Management:** Aligned variable expenses with income trends and optimized costs, leading to a 9% reduction in total expenses QoQ.
*   **Market Normalization Expectation:** Belief that markets will normalize over the next few quarters, allowing a return to historical profitability levels.
*   **SIP via UPI:** Streamlined SIP setup through automated mandates for mutual funds.
