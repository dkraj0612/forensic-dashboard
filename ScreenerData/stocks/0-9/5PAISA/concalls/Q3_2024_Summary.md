## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported Q3 FY25 results in a challenging industry environment marked by significant regulatory changes impacting client participation and turnover. Despite a 24% industry-wide drop in new customer acquisition and a 10% index correction, the company acquired 1.11 lakh new customers, bringing its total base to 47.4 lakhs. While broking revenue declined 8% QoQ to INR 243.9 crore and total revenue saw a 15% YoY decline to INR 85.3 crore due to new F&O norms and "true-to-label" regulations, the company achieved an 8% YoY PAT growth to INR 16.2 crore, with a 19% PAT margin. Significant product enhancements were rolled out, including a revamped web platform and a fully live, flutter-based mobile app with advanced trading tools and streamlined onboarding. Management is focusing on thoughtful client acquisition and optimizing its product and technology to navigate regulatory headwinds and drive long-term sustainable growth.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Navigating Regulatory Changes:** Adapting to SEBI's F&O norms (weekly expiry limits, lot size increases, higher margins) and "true-to-label" regulations, which have impacted turnover and allied income.
*   **Product & Technology Enhancements:** Launched a revamped web platform with an all-in-one dashboard for multi-asset management. Fully deployed a flutter-based mobile app with advanced F&O features, upgraded MTF journey, and new trading tools (VTT, OCO, position grouping). Streamlined onboarding and re-KYC processes.
*   **Focus on Quality Acquisition:** Continuing to acquire customers who can benefit from products long-term and are good for the company's lifetime value and cost of acquisition. The strategy remains principled, with potential fine-tuning of target cohorts based on how norms play out.
*   **Cross-Selling Opportunities:** Emphasizing cross-selling between broking and mutual fund products, leveraging the significant opportunity within the investing and trading space.
*   **MTF Book Management:** While the MTF book grew, management is cautious about speculating on the impact of future interest rate changes, noting that pricing is adjusted based on market conditions.
*   **Cost Optimization:** Reduced client acquisition costs and ongoing cost optimization efforts contributed to a decrease in other expenses.
