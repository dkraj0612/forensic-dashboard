## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported Q2 FY2026 results amidst a volatile equity market characterized by FII outflows and geopolitical tensions. Despite industry-wide declines in cash and F&O ADTO, the company focused on enhancing customer acquisition quality and lifetime value. Key initiatives included SEO improvements, brand awareness campaigns, and focused engagement events, which resulted in a 20% QoQ increase in new customer acquisition to over 95,000, while simultaneously reducing customer acquisition costs (CAC) by 15% and improving first-year revenue (FYR) by 25%. The total customer base surpassed the 5 million milestone. The company also accelerated its Margin Trading System (MTS) business growth through competitive interest rates and an expanded eligible securities universe, leading to a 22% QoQ increase in MTS income. While total income remained stable at INR 77.3 crore, a marginal rise in expenses due to marketing investments and higher finance costs impacted profitability, with Profit After Tax (PAT) declining 18% QoQ to INR 9.48 crore. Significant product and technology enhancements were implemented, including an improved digital onboarding journey, a dedicated ETF dashboard, revamped MTF features, and new trading platforms like Scalper and an FNO360 strategy analyzer.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Focus on Quality Acquisition:** Continued emphasis on relevant and cost-efficient customer acquisition, evidenced by improved CAC and FYR.
*   **Platform Enhancements:** Rolled out significant upgrades to the digital onboarding process, ETF dashboard, MTF features, and introduced new trading platforms (Scalper, FNO360 strategy analyzer) to improve user experience and trading efficiency.
*   **MTS Business Growth:** Accelerated growth through competitive interest rates and expanded eligible securities for Margin Trading System.
*   **Technological Modernization:** Ongoing investment in core system re-platforming and front-end upgrades (e.g., Flutter) to build a robust trading ecosystem.
*   **API Offerings:** Continues to support and develop algorithmic trading capabilities through open APIs.
*   **Brand Awareness:** Increased marketing and branding investments to raise profile and attract new customers.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance

*   **Customer Acquisition Quality & Cost Optimization:** **Confirmed Execution.** Management explicitly stated a focus on "relevant and cost-efficient acquisition," which is validated by the reported 20% QoQ increase in new customers acquired, a 15% reduction in CAC, and a 25% improvement in FYR. This aligns with the strategic shift towards higher quality customers from the previous quarter's commentary.
*   **Platform & Product Development:** **On Track/Delivered.** The transcript details numerous product and technology upgrades implemented during the quarter, directly addressing the strategy of enhancing trading efficiency and user experience across mobile and web platforms.
*   **MTS Business Growth:** **Exceeded Expectations.** The 22% QoQ growth in MTF income and a 17% QoQ expansion in the average client funding book demonstrate strong execution in this segment, driven by strategic pricing and product expansion.
*   **Profitability:** **Below Previous Quarter but within Market Context.** While PAT saw an 18% QoQ decline, management attributed this to increased marketing/finance costs and, importantly, to industry-wide volume moderation impacting total income. The decline is presented as a consequence of market headwinds rather than internal operational failures.
*   **Customer Base Milestone:** **Achieved.** The company successfully crossed the 5 million customer base milestone, reinforcing progress in customer acquisition.

### New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance for Next Quarter/Year

*   **Continued Focus on Execution:** Management remains "heads down into execution on improving our product" as their primary focus.
*   **H2 FY2026 Outlook:** Expects H2 to be better than H1, contingent on market performance.
