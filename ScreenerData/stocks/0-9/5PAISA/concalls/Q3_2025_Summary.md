## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported Q3 FY2026 results amidst a positive market sentiment with Nifty and Sensex reaching all-time highs, coupled with steady DII investments. The quarter saw a resurgence in retail participation, with a 15% QoQ improvement in F&O ADTO and stable cash ADTO. The Margin Trading Facility (MTF) book crossed INR 1.1 lakh crore, indicating strong adoption. The company acquired approximately 78,000 new customers, bringing the total to 5.08 million, while maintaining a focus on acquiring customers with higher lifetime value. Operationally, notional ADTO grew by a robust 24% QoQ to INR 3.31 trillion. Financially, total revenue increased by 3% QoQ to INR 79.4 crore. Employee benefit expenses saw a 9% rise, partly due to new talent and a one-time implementation cost of new labor laws. Profit After Tax (PAT) demonstrated strong growth, increasing by 30% QoQ to INR 12.3 crore, with a PAT margin of 16%. Net worth stood at INR 639 crore. Product and technology enhancements focused on improving trading efficiency, user experience, and platform depth, particularly in derivatives trading and onboarding processes. Technological improvements also targeted enhanced trading performance and optimized market feed APIs.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Focus on Quality Acquisition:** Continued emphasis on acquiring customers with higher first-year revenue, faster payback periods, and improved lifetime value.
*   **Platform & Product Enhancements:** Strengthened derivatives trading with position grouping, direct management from option chain, and advanced candlestick indicators. Introduced instant margin credit on stock selling and expanded Pay Later MTF.
*   **Onboarding Streamlining:** Enabled MTF activation from day zero, reduced friction with OCR data capture, penny drop verification, intelligent bank pre-fill, and improved e-sign flow.
*   **Technology Performance Optimization:** Enhanced trading performance via static data caching, reduced API calls, and optimized market feed APIs with reduced payload size. Improved charting experience with faster load times and rendering.
*   **Leveraging AI:** Commitment to continuous innovation and strengthening the product/technology stack by increasingly leveraging AI to enhance customer experience and management.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance

*   **Customer Acquisition Quality & Cost Optimization:** **Confirmed Execution.** The focus on improving customer acquisition quality, as stated in the Q2 FY2026 context, continues. While new customer numbers decreased QoQ to ~78,000 from over 95,000, the emphasis remains on "higher first year revenue, faster payback periods and improved lifetime value," suggesting a continued strategic shift.
*   **Platform & Product Development:** **On Track/Delivered.** The transcript details significant product enhancements in derivatives trading, MTF, and onboarding, directly aligning with the strategy to improve user experience and trading efficiency mentioned in the prior period.
*   **MTS Business Growth:** **Exceeded Expectations.** The MTF book crossing INR 1.1 lakh crore and improved MTF adoption (with features like instant margin credit and expanded coverage) indicate strong execution in this segment, building on the prior quarter's growth.
*   **Profitability:** **Strong Performance.** PAT saw a robust 30% QoQ growth, a significant improvement from the previous quarter's decline. The PAT margin of 16% is healthy. The rise in employee benefit expenses, including a one-time cost, was managed effectively, leading to improved profitability.
*   **Customer Base Milestone:** **Achieved.** The company surpassed the 5 million customer base milestone, reaching 5.08 million, reinforcing progress in customer acquisition.
*   **Industry Trends (Pricing):** Management's commentary on potential industry pricing adjustments (charging for services like equity delivery) from the previous period is not directly revisited with new guidance, but the existing INR 20 charge for equity delivery remains, and the company is not planning immediate changes while continuing to "assess from time to time."
