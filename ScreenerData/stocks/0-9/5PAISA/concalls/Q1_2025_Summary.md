## Executive Overview

5paisa Capital reported Q1 FY2025 results with strong growth in revenue and a significant increase in Profit After Tax (PAT), driven by positive market conditions and enhanced product offerings. The company acquired 2.34 lakh customers, a 116% year-on-year increase, bringing the total customer base to 44.7 lakhs. While there was a slight sequential dip in customer acquisition, management clarified this was a conscious decision to focus on acquiring higher quality customers. Key financial highlights include a 21% YoY growth in total revenue to INR 102 crore and a substantial 248% QoQ and 39% YoY PAT growth to INR 20.1 crore, achieving a PAT margin of 20%. The company continues to prioritize product development, technology investment, and customer experience, with significant advancements in its mobile and web platforms.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Total Revenue (Q1 FY2025):** INR 102 crore (21% YoY growth)
*   **Broking Revenue (Q1 FY2025):** INR 40 crore (11% YoY growth)
*   **Allied Income (Q1 FY2025):** INR 29.7 crore (27% YoY growth)
*   **Profit After Tax (PAT) (Q1 FY2025):** INR 20.1 crore (248% QoQ growth, 39% YoY growth)
*   **PAT Margin (Q1 FY2025):** 20%
*   **Customers Acquired (Q1 FY2025):** 2.34 lakh (116% YoY growth)
*   **Total Customer Base (End of Q1 FY2025):** 44.7 lakhs
*   **Average Daily Turnover (ADTO) (Q1 FY2025):** INR 3.67 trillion (27% YoY growth)
*   **Client Funding Book (Q1 FY2025):** INR 271 crore (20% YoY growth)
*   **Mutual Fund AUM (Q1 FY2025):** INR 1,124 crore (90% YoY growth)
*   **Employee Benefit Expense (Q1 FY2025):** INR 13.6 crore (includes annual increments, RSU/ESOP costs, and a one-time positive impact of INR 8.66 crore from unvested ESOP/RSU costs).

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Product-Led Innovation:** Enhancing trading experience through new features like straddle chains, bulk order placement from option chains, custom option strategy execution, and basket strategies in FnO 360. Introduction of QR code scanning for UPI fund transfers.
*   **Platform Revamp:** Revamped mobile app (iOS & Android) to be live by end of Q2 FY'25. Trade Station 2.0, the revamped web platform, is now accessible to all users, offering an enhanced UI and smoother experience.
*   **Customer Acquisition Focus:** Conscious decision to prioritize acquiring high-quality customers, focusing on revenue, margin, and profit, rather than aggressive volume chasing.
*   **Market Share Growth:** Continued pursuit of a 6% market share target, emphasizing quality acquisition and maintaining focus on key metrics like RPC and CAC.
*   **No Lending Business:** Currently focused on core broking business, with no immediate plans to enter the lending business for cross-selling income.
*   **Technology Modernization:** Ongoing efforts to modernize applications and infrastructure for technical excellence.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance

*   **Customer Acquisition Quality:** **Confirmed Shift/Execution.** Management reiterates the conscious decision made "from the last one to two months" to improve customer quality, explaining the sequential dip in customer acquisition numbers. This aligns with the stated priority of acquiring "quality customers" and focusing on "revenue, margin, and profit" as mentioned in the previous historical summary. The 116% YoY growth in customers acquired indicates strong overall traction, with the sequential change being a deliberate strategic shift.
*   **Product Development & Platform Enhancement:** **On Track/Delivered.** The transcript details significant progress in product and technology, including enhanced features for option traders, the FnO 360 product, QR code fund transfers, and the upcoming revamped mobile app and accessible Trade Station 2.0 web platform. This execution is consistent with the strategy of investing in technology and product improvement.
*   **Profitability:** **Exceeded Expectations.** The reported PAT of INR 20.1 crore, with a 248% QoQ growth and 20% margin, significantly surpasses the growth seen in the previous quarter. This indicates strong monetization from the existing customer base and effective cost management, especially considering the one-time ESOP cost adjustment.
*   **Market Share Target:** **Reaffirmed with New Strategy.** While the historical summary noted a dip from 3.3% to 2.5%, management acknowledges this and reaffirms the goal of achieving 6% market share. The strategy now explicitly emphasizes "only the quality acquisition will work," a clear continuation of the quality-focused narrative from the prior period.
*   **ESOP Reversal Impact:** **Explained and Accounted For.** The one-time positive impact of INR 8.66 crore related to unvested ESOP and RSU costs is explicitly stated and linked to the departure of Mr. Narayan Gangadhar and other employees, providing transparency on the PAT surge.
