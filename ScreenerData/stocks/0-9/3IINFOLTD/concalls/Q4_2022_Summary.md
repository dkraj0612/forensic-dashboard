## Executive Overview

The investor presentation regarding the RailTel WiFi Monetisation Project, dated December 21, 2022, signifies a new strategic initiative for 3i Infotech Limited. This project focuses on monetizing the captive portal at over 6,100+ railway stations across India. The core of the strategy revolves around three revenue streams: advertising on the captive Wi-Fi portal, the development of a "Railways' Super App" to offer a range of services beyond internet access, and a content delivery platform ("2WayRelay") at selected stations. The company anticipates significant opportunities in reaching a large user base, creating a media platform with a physical footprint, and generating valuable consumer data. This initiative aligns with the company's "Build" pillar, aiming to create new, value-driven business lines, particularly in the MediaTech and Digital Marketing sectors, leveraging large-scale public Wi-Fi networks.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Projected Monetization:**
    *   **2 Way AP – Content Delivery:** Quoted MG of ₹14 Cr or 40% revenue share, whichever is higher.
*   **User Base Statistics (Current & Projected):**
    *   **Railway Stations:** 6,102+ with 18,292 Wi-Fi hot spots.
    *   **Total Annual User Logins:** Estimated 2.2 Cr.
    *   **Average Unique Users (Daily):** Over 20,000 new users daily.
    *   **Average Unique Users (Monthly):** 3.13 Cr (August 2022 peak).
    *   **Passenger Traffic:** Average 526 million per month, with 210 million non-suburban passengers (October 2022).
    *   **Projected User Growth:** Potential to reach 31 million by 2028 assuming 10% YoY passenger growth.
    *   **Data Capture:** Information on over 138 million users / 45 million unique users to be generated.
*   **Network Capacity:** Current network manages over 60,000 users concurrently, with a capacity for up to 120,000 users.
*   **Awareness:** Low awareness of the Free Internet via Captive Portal.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **RailTel WiFi Monetisation Project:** The central theme, focusing on unlocking revenue from a large captive Wi-Fi network.
*   **Revenue Streams:**
    1.  **Captive Wi-Fi:** Monetization through advertising on the basic free Wi-Fi service (30 mins - 120 mins).
    2.  **Railways' Super App:** A "greenfield project" offering premium Wi-Fi (up to 240 mins), Railway services, entertainment, infotainment, e-commerce, education, gaming, and e-coupons. Revenue primarily from advertising, supported by content/services.
    3.  **2WayRelay – Content Delivery:** Paid content and advertising at selected stations for users in waiting rooms/retiring rooms.
*   **"Build" Pillar Focus:** This initiative strongly aligns with the company's "Build" strategy, focusing on creating new products, intellectual property, and value-driven revenue models.
*   **Market Expansion:** Entering the MediaTech and Entertainment sectors, and expanding Digital Marketing Services through data mining.
*   **Technology & Infrastructure:**
    *   **Unified Network Orchestration:** Meeting RailTel's technical and security requirements.
    *   **Data Utilization:** Creating own measuring and tracking systems to process and compile user data, which is currently not shared by RailTel.
    *   **Potential for Large App:** The Super App has the potential to be one of the largest apps in the country by usage and time spent.
*   **Commercial Models:** Emphasis on advertising models, content sales, service-based revenue, and e-commerce within the Super App.
