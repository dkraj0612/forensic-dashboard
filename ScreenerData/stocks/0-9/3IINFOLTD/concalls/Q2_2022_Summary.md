## Executive Overview

3i Infotech Limited's Q2 FY23 earnings call and investor presentation reveal a company actively executing its "Run, Grow, Build" strategy, laying the groundwork for future growth. The company emphasizes a renewed focus on its IT services business following the carve-out of its product segment. Key initiatives include strengthening its global presence, expanding service lines, and investing in next-generation technologies. Management reiterates its ambitious vision of becoming a billion-dollar organization by 2030, driven by digital transformation and innovation. While specific profit figures were not detailed, the company highlights revenue growth in certain regions and service lines, alongside efforts to improve operational efficiency and secure new business.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **"Run, Grow, Build" Framework Evolution:**
    *   **Run:** Focus on retaining clients ("Retain"), resetting practices and frameworks ("Re-Boot"), and reskilling employees ("Re-Train"). Emphasis on delivery excellence, value creation, and consultative selling.
    *   **Grow:** Aggressive new client acquisition globally ("Acquire"), increasing wallet share through "Altiray"-powered services ("Accelerate"), and disrupting commercial models ("Annihilate").
    *   **Build:** Innovation through service orchestration ("Innovate"), incubating product startups ("Incubate"), and developing 5G Cognitive ready products/platforms in partnership with telecom players ("Invent").
*   **Technology & Service Focus:**
    *   **Application Automation Analytics (AAA):** High revenue contributor, focusing on low-code/no-code, digital testing, and AI-powered platforms.
    *   **Infrastructure Management Services (IMS):** Significant revenue stream.
    *   **CloudFirst:** Identified as the fastest-growing new line of business with 17% Gross Margin in H1 FY23.
    *   **Edge Security & 5G:** Established 5G Lab as a Service and implementing NuRe EDGE product solutions for Zero Trust enabled cloud. FLEXIB+ & NuRe EDGE expected for market launch by end Nov 2022.
    *   **Digital & NxtGen Business:** Focus on creating differentiated digital transformation services.
*   **Vertical & Geographic Strategy:**
    *   **Indian Business Region (IBR):** Demonstrating strong YoY and QoQ growth.
    *   **Emerging Business Region (EBR):** Showing positive YoY and QoQ growth.
    *   **Global Business Region (GBR):** Experiencing negative growth YoY and QoQ, driven by US & Middle East. Proactive closure of negative accounts impacted topline but is expected to improve EBITDA margins in H2.
    *   **Industry Focus:** Leveraging existing US accounts in BFSI to launch new "Build" service lines.
*   **Customer Strategy:**
    *   Focus on delivering digital transformation powered by outcome-centric commercial models.
    *   15 new logos added in Q2 FY23.
    *   Two large deals of INR 5 Cr annual contract value won.
    *   NuRe 3i & 3i + contributing 75% to revenue, iCXO (CLM, F&A, Zenith) 25%. iEmpower (HRSS) 77%.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

**Target Hit/Miss Analysis:**

*   **Revenue Growth & Stabilization:**
    *   **Hit:** Indian Business Region (IBR) shows strong YoY (16%) and QoQ (7%) growth, aligning with the strategy of strengthening the domestic market. Emerging Business Region (EBR) also shows positive growth.
    *   **Miss/Headwind:** Global Business Region (GBR) experienced negative YoY (-5%) and QoQ (-5%) growth. Management attributes this to proactive closure of "negative accounts" and mentions this impacted the topline but expects a positive impact on EBITDA margins in H2. This indicates a shift in focus towards profitability over pure revenue growth in certain segments.
*   **Profitability & Margin Improvement:**
    *   **Mixed:** H1 FY23 Adjusted EBITDA is negative (-23.2 Cr), indicating ongoing investment and restructuring. However, management explicitly states that the proactive closure of negative accounts "will contribute to the EBITDA margins in H2." They also note that "Last year's Variable /Performance pay and annual increments has impacted on EBITDA," suggesting historical cost pressures. CloudFirst is a bright spot with a 17% Gross Margin.
*   **New Business Acquisition & Client Addition:**
    *   **Hit:** 15 new logos were added in Q2 FY23, and two large deals with INR 5 Cr annual contract value were secured, demonstrating traction in acquiring new clients and larger contracts.
*   **Execution of "Run, Grow, Build":**
    *   **Consistent:** The presentation clearly outlines activities within each pillar, demonstrating ongoing execution. The launch of NuRe EDGE and FLEXIB+ product solutions for market launch by end November 2022 signifies progress in the "Build" phase.
*   **Talent & Operations:**
    *   **Progress:** Oracle COE hired 50 trainee engineers, with 18 in deployable pools, indicating investment in skilled talent. The mention of proactive closure of negative accounts suggests an effort to streamline operations and focus on profitable relationships.
