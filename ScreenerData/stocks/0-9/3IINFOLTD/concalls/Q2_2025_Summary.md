## Executive Overview

3i Infotech's Q2 FY26 earnings presentation, dated November 13, 2025, showcases a strong quarter marked by significant QoQ growth in both revenue and profitability. Consolidated revenue for the quarter ended September 30, 2025, reached INR 174.9 Crore, a 2.5% increase from the previous quarter (Q1 FY26). This revenue growth, coupled with operational efficiencies, led to a substantial surge in EBITDA, which rose 74% QoQ to INR 31.3 Crore, reflecting an improved EBITDA margin of 18%. Profit After Tax (PAT) saw a remarkable 151% QoQ increase, reaching INR 18.9 Crore. The US and India emerged as the top-performing geographies, each contributing 46% and 43% of revenue, respectively. The Application-Automation-Analytics (AAA) vertical continues to be the primary growth driver, generating INR 123 Crore in revenue, followed by Infrastructure Services (IS) and Digital Business Process Services (Digital BPS). The company also highlighted the addition of 14 new clients and a strategic alliance with HCL Software to accelerate digital transformation.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Digital Transformation Focus:** Continued emphasis on modernizing applications, automation, and analytics, leveraging emerging technologies for intelligent insights.
*   **Cloud and Edge Solutions:** Addressing security and efficiency from core to edge, enabling businesses to move to the cloud safely.
*   **Product Development:** In-house development of new-age products, including "NuRe Campus" (cloud-based enterprise automation for higher education) and "Flexib+" (platform for functional and non-functional automation testing).
*   **Strategic Alliance:** Partnership with HCL Software to accelerate digital transformation across India, US, Middle East, and Southeast Asia.
*   **Client-Centric Approach:** Outcome-based partnerships linked to business KPIs, focusing on fixing processes before technology, leveraging existing client investments, and providing a skilled workforce.
*   **Flexible Engagement Models:** Continued emphasis on "Try & Buy," "No Capex only Opex," and "Pay As You Go" models.
*   **Core Philosophy:** "First Future," "Invent with Hype," "Built to Bounce," "Outcome."
