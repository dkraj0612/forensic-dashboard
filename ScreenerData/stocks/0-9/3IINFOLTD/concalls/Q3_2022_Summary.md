    *   **Run:** Focus on retaining and fortifying clients, resetting practices and frameworks for the new decade, and re-skilling employees. Emphasis on delivery excellence and continuous value creation.
    *   **Grow:** Aggressively acquiring new clients globally across new service lines and industries, increasing wallet share with "Altiray"-powered services, and disrupting commercial models.
    *   **Build:** Innovating through orchestration of services, incubating and commercializing product startups, and building 5G Cognitive-ready products/platforms in partnership with telecom players.
*   **Technology & Service Focus:**
    *   **CloudFirst, Digital Inside, Cognitive, Edge Ready strategy:** Positioned as a challenger offering onshore profitable business.
    *   **Application-Automation-Analytics (AAA):** Continued to be a high revenue contributor.
    *   **Infrastructure Management Services (IMS):** A significant revenue stream.
    *   **NextGen Technologies:** Development and launch of products like NuRe Campus (SaaS based dedicated cloud ERP for student management), NuRe3i+ (Zero Trust Sovereign Cloud), and exploration in Computer Vision & AI, CXO Cockpit, and 5G Lab.
