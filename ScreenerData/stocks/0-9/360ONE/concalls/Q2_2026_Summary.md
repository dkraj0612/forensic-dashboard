## Executive Overview

360 ONE WAM reported a strong Q2 FY26, demonstrating resilience amidst market volatility. The company achieved its highest ever quarterly Profit After Tax (PAT) of ₹316 Crs, up 28% YoY. Total ARR AUM reached ₹2,95,000 Crs, a 22% YoY increase, with Wealth ARR AUM crossing ₹2 lakh Crs. Net flows for H1 FY26 were robust at ₹32,132 Crs, with Q2 net flows (excluding UBS integration) at ₹8,734 Crs, indicating strong client acquisition and reduced attrition impact. The strategic collaboration with UBS AG is progressing, with the warrant subscription and India business transfer completed, focusing now on rolling out the global collaboration framework. The integration of B&K Securities is yielding synergies, and the HNI business is showing encouraging early progress. The company declared a second interim dividend of ₹6 per share.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **UBS Collaboration Progress:** Warrant subscription and UBS India business transfer are complete. The focus is on implementing the global collaboration framework, unlocking synergies in Wealth and Asset Management, and integrating over ₹5,200 Crs of AUM from 80+ UHNI families.
*   **B&K Securities Integration:** The acquisition is contributing significantly to Q2 financials, with synergies realized by integrating treasury and equity offerings with the existing client base.
*   **HNI Business Ramp-Up:** Over 50 RMs and 380+ clients across 10 locations have been onboarded. The business is seen as a strong feeder for the UHNI proposition, with efforts to reduce break-even window through product addition and client network leverage.
*   **Asset Management Pipeline:** Gross flows remain stable, with visibility on new product launches in EIF and SIF segments. Continued strengthening of sub-strategies across listed, unlisted, credit, RE, and infra. Institutional mandates pipeline remains strong.
*   **Wealth Management Leadership:** Emphasis on client and AUM flows, alongside strengthening the position as an employer of choice with onboarding of high-quality senior teams.
*   **Technology Investment:** Continued investment in internal and client-facing developments, including pilots for AI in internal use cases.
*   **Full-Stack Player Consolidation:** Aim to solidify leadership in Wealth Management and Alternates, and grow in Global and Capital Markets segments.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets & Narrative Consistency:

*   **AUM Growth:** Achieved 22% YoY growth in ARR AUM, aligning with expectations for steady growth.
*   **Net Flows:** Demonstrated strong net flows in Q2 (₹8,734 Crs ex-UBS), indicating progress towards the full-year guidance, with reduced attrition impact compared to the previous quarter. Management expressed confidence in the 10-12% net flow target for the year.
*   **Revenue & Profitability:** ARR revenue grew 39.4% YoY, and PAT reached a quarterly record, validating growth and operational efficiency.
*   **UBS Integration:** Key steps of warrant subscription and business transfer are completed, demonstrating progress on a critical strategic initiative. The actual AUM inflow from UBS was approximately ₹5,000 Crs, lower than initial expectations of ~₹8,000 Crs, attributed to custody assets vs. active ARR assets.
*   **B&K Integration:** The business is performing strongly and contributing to financials, with synergies already being realized.
*   **HNI Business:** Early progress is encouraging, with client and RM onboarding on track. However, the segment is currently incurring losses due to increased recruitment costs, which management expects to continue for the next 3-4 quarters before stabilizing.
*   **Talent Acquisition:** Management acknowledges ongoing RM recruitment and associated costs, projecting an 18-24 month break-even period for senior hires in an established system.
*   **TBR Sustainability:** Integration of B&K has improved the sustainability of Transactional Business Revenue (TBR) and is expected to reduce earnings volatility.

### New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance:

*   **Full Year Net Flows:** Reiteration of confidence in achieving 10-12% net flows for the full year.
*   **Quarterly Run Rate Net Flows:** Expectation to maintain a quarterly run rate of ₹8,000-10,000 Crs (75% Wealth, 25% AMC).
*   **Cost to Income Ratio:** Expectation to reach 47-48% in the next 3-4 quarters and closer to 45-46% in the subsequent year, with core businesses potentially achieving 45-46% within a couple of quarters.
*   **RM Break-even:** Senior RMs in an established system expected to break-even within 18-24 months.
*   **HNI Segment Break-even:** Early signs of break-even expected in Q3-Q4 of FY27.
*   **TBR Growth:** Target for TBR to grow at 10-15% annually over the next 2-3 years, reaching ₹1,000-1,200 Crs from a steady state of ₹750-800 Crs.
*   **Equity Brokerage Growth:** Aim to grow brokerage income from ₹320-330 Crs to ₹500 Crs in 2.5-3 years, with significant under-penetration in the UHNI segment.
*   **Transaction Income Mix:** Future mix targeted to be broadly one-third equity brokerage, one-third unlisted transactions, and one-third yield-plus transactions.
*   **RM & Team Leader Hiring:** Target to recruit 7-10 teams (60-80 RMs) over the next 12-18 months and 15-20 team leaders and 100-120 RMs over the next 2-2.5 years for UHNI growth.
*   **UBS Collaboration Synergy Quantification:** Management aims to have a number-driven business plan for UBS collaboration synergies in 5-6 months.
*   **Mutual Fund Distribution Yield:** Long-term retention expected to be around 45 basis points, with potential for slight improvement as the mid-market business scales.
*   **B&K Transaction Revenue:** Quarterly contribution expected to be around ₹40-45 Crs.
*   **Alternate Asset Management Flows:** Expectation of strong collections on the Alternate side in the next 24 months, with redemptions from older funds now largely ceased.
*   **Carry Recognition Schedule:** Commitment to potentially include a schedule of schemes without carry numbers in the model data book.
*   **UHNI Market Share:** Aim to maintain or increase market share to 10-12% of a widening UHNI market.
*   **RM & Team Leader Numbers:** Approximately 100-110 Team Leads and 240-250 Relationship Managers as of Q2 FY26.
*   **Total Clients:** Over 4,200 families and corporates, including treasury operations.
