## Executive Overview

360 ONE WAM reported robust Q3 FY25 results, marked by a significant surge in revenue and profit, driven by strong AUM growth across its Wealth and Asset Management businesses. The company announced a strategic agreement to acquire B&K Securities, a leading brokerage house, signaling an intent to enhance its integrated service offering and expand market reach. AUM reached ₹5,79,222 Crs, with ARR AUM growing to ₹2,47,999 Crs. PAT saw a substantial increase of 41.7% YoY to ₹275 Crs, with Tangible ROE at 24.1%. The acquisition of B&K Securities is expected to be EPS accretive and strengthen 360 ONE's position as a comprehensive financial services platform.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Integrated Platform Enhancement:** The strategic acquisition of B&K Securities aims to create a powerful flywheel effect by integrating research, advisory, and execution capabilities, thereby expanding the service spectrum and market segments.
*   **Wealth Management Growth:** Continued focus on growing the 360 ONE Plus proposition (up 49% YoY), Distribution (up 32% YoY), and Lending businesses (up 40% YoY). Onboarding of 60+ clients with >₹10 Crs ARR AUM in Q2 FY25 highlights deep penetration in the UHNI segment.
*   **Asset Management Expansion:** Growth in Listed Equity (+26% YoY), Private Equity (+24% YoY), and Credit (+17% YoY) segments is driving ARR AUM expansion.
*   **ESG Commitment:** CRISIL assigned a "Strong" rating (CRISIL ESG 65), reaffirming the company's commitment to long-term ESG practices.
*   **Market Leadership:** The B&K acquisition is expected to solidify 360 ONE's position as a market leader with the most comprehensive platform offering.
