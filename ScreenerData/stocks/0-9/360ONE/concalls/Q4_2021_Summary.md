## Executive Overview

360ONE (formerly IIFL Wealth) reported strong Q4 FY21 results, showcasing robust PAT growth driven by increasing AUM and a strategic shift towards recurring revenue models. Total AUM surpassed ₹2.07 lakh crore, with a significant 62.9% YoY surge in ARR Assets to over ₹1.02 lakh crore, bringing the ARR share in total AUM to nearly 50%. Revenue from operations saw a 48% YoY increase, with ARR revenue constituting 63% of the total revenue mix. The company maintained its commitment to shareholder returns, declaring a dividend payout of 70% of FY21 PAT, alongside a special dividend. Management revised FY22 guidance upwards, anticipating net flows of ₹20,000 crore and a continued reduction in the cost-to-income ratio towards 50%.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Recurring Revenue Model:** Continued strategic emphasis on transitioning to a recurring revenue model. Over 95% of net flows in the last six quarters have been in ARR assets, reinforcing this shift.
*   **Asset Management Growth:** Strong traction with a 70% YoY increase in AUM for the full year, driven by differentiated offerings in Listed & Unlisted Equity, and institutional mandates.
*   **Digital and Technology Investment:** Ongoing significant investment in digital initiatives to enhance client engagement, embed intelligence, and improve internal efficiency.
*   **Team and Culture Building:** Commitment to investing in high-quality teams and fostering an innovative, knowledge-based service culture.
*   **Client Behavior:** Positive investor sentiment and market conditions are driving investments. Clients are showing a propensity for higher direct equity allocation and increased interest in alternative investments due to low fixed-income yields.
*   **Capital Allocation:** Commitment to an aggressive dividend payout policy (60-80% of PAT). Initiatives are underway for total capital deployment and allocation across businesses, with a focus on increasing ROE.
