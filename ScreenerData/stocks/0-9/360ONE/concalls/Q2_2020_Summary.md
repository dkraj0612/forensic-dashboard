## Executive Overview

360ONE (formerly IIFL Wealth) reported a solid Q2 FY21, demonstrating resilience and growth despite ongoing macroeconomic uncertainties. The company saw a notable 5.6% QoQ increase in Assets Under Management (AUM) to Rs 1,67,090 crore, excluding custody assets, with Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) assets growing by 8.2% QoQ to Rs 79,142 crore. Revenues rose 6.7% QoQ to Rs 212 crore, with ARR revenue up 9.7% QoQ to Rs 140 crore. Profit Before Tax (PBT) increased 13.4% QoQ to Rs 78 crore, and Profit After Tax (PAT) reached Rs 86 crore. Net new flows for the quarter were strong at Rs 6,442 crore, indicating healthy client acquisition and retention. The company is actively managing its business model in light of regulatory changes, particularly the Investment Advisors Regulation, and has provided medium-term guidance for FY21 and FY22. Strategic focus remains on enhancing productivity, optimizing costs, and leveraging industry consolidation, with the L&T Finance wealth management acquisition pending approval.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Seamless Operations & Hybrid Work Model:** Continued seamless operations and planning for a hybrid work culture (work from home and work from office) to enhance productivity.
*   **Client Acquisition & Net Flows:** Sharp rise in new client acquisition (500+ relevant families in H1 FY21, excluding L&T acquisition) and healthy net new flows of Rs 6,442 crore, driven by completed transactions post-lockdown.
*   **ARR & IIFL One Focus:** Strong traction in ARR assets and IIFL One proposition, with significant growth in Discretionary PMS within IIFL One. Retentions on DPMS and NDPMS improved.
*   **Asset Management Growth:** AUM grew over 8% to ~Rs 27,000 crore with positive net flows and sustained first quartile performance. Onboarding of a long-short strategy team in Singapore.
*   **Client Risk Appetite:** Client risk appetite remains subdued, with a preference for high-quality debt and specific high-conviction ideas.
*   **Regulatory Adaptation:** Detailed presentation of engagement types and licenses post-Investment Advisors Regulation, clarifying AUM, revenues, and retentions across various services.
*   **Medium-Term Guidance:** Provided FY21 and FY22 guidance for the first time, focusing on transparent communication with stakeholders.
*   **Productivity & Efficiency:** Ongoing focus on enhancing productivity and efficiency across the firm through optimization of resources and digital adoption.
*   **Cost Optimization:** Continued efforts to optimize costs and resources, particularly focusing on people productivity and digital transformation, with further potential in rental costs.
*   **Capital Rationalization:** Aiming for further capital efficiency through potential special dividends and maintaining a 75-80% profit payout policy.
*   **Geographic Expansion:** Gradual expansion into newer cities through a hub-and-spoke model, focusing on consolidating presence in existing 20-22 cities.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

*   **Stated Targets & Narrative Consistency:**
    *   **AUM Growth:** The reported QoQ AUM growth of 5.6% aligns with the general expectation of growth.
    *   **ARR Growth:** The 8.2% QoQ growth in ARR assets is a positive indicator of the ongoing shift towards recurring revenue streams.
    *   **Client Sentiment:** Management reiterates that while client sentiment for new investments has improved due to business recoveries, a cautious approach persists, particularly towards fixed income, which is consistent with previous commentary.
    *   **Productivity & Cost Optimization:** The narrative strongly emphasizes ongoing efforts in these areas, with expected visible impacts in the coming quarters, aligning with previous statements.
    *   **Regulatory Compliance:** The detailed explanation of business engagement types post-IA regulation demonstrates proactive adaptation and transparency.
    *   **L&T Acquisition:** Mentioned as pending statutory approval, maintaining consistency with prior updates.

*   **New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance:**
    *   **Productivity & Efficiency Gains:** Expected over a period of time with the stabilization of a hybrid work culture.
    *   **FY21 & FY22 Guidance:** For the first time, provided specific guidance for these years, which will be a key metric to track performance against.
    *   **Cost Optimization Impact:** Further cost optimization and resource optimization (people productivity, digital strategies) are ongoing priorities.
    *   **Dividend Payout Policy:** Commitment to maintaining a 75-80% payout policy of profits.
    *   **Capital Rationalization:** Aim to rationalize capital required by another 10-15% over the next 12 months, potentially leading to another special dividend.
    *   **Cost to Income Ratio Stabilization:** Expects stabilization around 50% over the next three years, with a push towards 52-53% in the next year.
    *   **IIFL One AUM Target:** Aiming to end FY21 closer to Rs 26,000-30,000 crore, slightly lower than initial plans.
    *   **Third-Party Product Recycling:** Expects 80-90% of the Rs 48,000 crore in third-party AUM to be recycled into fee-bearing assets over the next 12-24 months, with 60% of this moving into fee-bearing categories.
    *   **Lending Book NIM Improvement:** Expected benefits from new borrowing at a lower cost over the next 12-24 months, with initial impact of negative carry for the next 6 months.
    *   **Fixed Cost Growth:** Fixed cost growth is not a large component, with every 10-15% AUM addition marginally improving the cost-to-income ratio.
    *   **Office Space Reduction:** Anticipates reduction in rental costs over the next 3-6 months due to reduced office spaces.
