## Executive Overview

360 ONE WAM concluded FY26 with a robust performance, achieving its highest full-year Profit After Tax (PAT) of ₹1,225 Crores, a 20.7% increase year-on-year. The company demonstrated strong growth across its Assets Under Management (AUM), with total ARR AUM reaching ₹311,940 Crores (+26% YoY). Wealth ARR AUM stood at ₹216,000 Crores and Asset Management ARR AUM at ₹95,000 Crores. Net flows for FY26 were substantial at ₹55,875 Crores, with organic net flows rising 36% to ₹35,199 Crores (14% of opening AUM), indicating sustained client acquisition and wallet share expansion. Strategic initiatives, including the integration of 360 ONE Capital (formerly B&K Securities) and the UBS Global Collaboration Framework, are progressing, contributing to a comprehensive financial services platform. The company's focus remains on leveraging its strong brand, talent, diverse product offerings, and technological advancements to capture market opportunities.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Core Business Strength:** Robust net flows in Wealth Management, driven by UHNI franchise, maturing onboarded teams, and broad asset class demand. Continued strong performance in Asset Management across various strategies.
*   **Strategic Integrations & Rebranding:** 360 ONE Capital (formerly B&K Securities) integration is complete, enhancing institutional equities capabilities. Synergies are emerging, including increased brokerage revenue from UHNI clients and access to corporate treasuries. Investment Banking platform is under development.
*   **HNI & ET Money Development:** The HNI (Reserve) program is scaling with ~60 RMs managing ₹4,000 Crs AUM, serving as a feeder to the core UHNI franchise. ET Money is transitioning towards breakeven, with strategic business model execution.
*   **Alternate Asset Management:** The Alternatives business is a key differentiator, with strong client participation across various balance sheet needs. Private equity funds have delivered strong returns.
*   **Global & Capital Markets Expansion:** UBS Global Collaboration Framework is showing early traction in cross-referrals. UBS's global distribution is facilitating offshore access for 360 ONE's strategies. Investment banking contribution is expected within 12-18 months.
*   **Technology & Talent:** Continued investment in technology, including AI for analytics and reporting. Focus on attracting and retaining top talent, with a culture of integrity, discretion, and long-term thinking. Diversified client base and asset allocation are key to client retention.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

### Audit of Stated Targets & Narrative Consistency:

*   **AUM Growth & Net Flows:** The company reported strong ARR AUM growth of 26% YoY and total net flows of ₹55,875 Crs, aligning with and exceeding previous expectations. Organic net flows of ₹35,199 Crs at 14% of opening AUM demonstrate strong underlying business momentum.
*   **Profitability:** Achieved the highest full-year PAT, validating growth trajectory and operational efficiency. Tangible ROE of 19.3% is robust, with management expecting improvement as capital in Lending and Alternatives deploy.
*   **UBS Collaboration:** Cross-referral programs are showing positive early traction, with meaningful relationship conversion expected in FY27.
*   **B&K Integration:** Integration is complete with 360 ONE Capital. Synergies are materializing, with expected contributions from investment banking in 12-18 months. Broking revenue from UHNI clients has seen an uplift.
*   **HNI Segment:** The Reserve program has scaled to ₹4,000 Crs AUM with ~60 RMs, demonstrating progress towards breaking even as previously guided.
*   **ET Money:** The business is transitioning towards breakeven, indicating progress on strategic execution.
*   **Talent Acquisition & Retention:** Management emphasized the importance of attracting high-quality talent and retaining them through culture and platform offerings. The UHNI business is focused on building a partnership-level RM base.
*   **TBR Sustainability:** The integration of 360 ONE Capital has structurally improved the annuity-like component of TBR. The quarterly TBR guidance has been revised upwards.
