## Executive Overview

360ONE WAM (formerly IIFL Wealth) reported its Q4 FY23 and full-year results, demonstrating resilience and growth despite significant market volatility. The company highlighted robust growth in Assets Under Management (AUM), particularly in Recurring Revenue Assets (ARR), driven by strong net inflows. Profitability improved year-over-year, supported by steady retentions and effective cost management. Key strategic initiatives, including the rebranding to 360ONE and the completion of the Mumbai Angels acquisition, were reinforced, underscoring a continued focus on platform expansion and an enhanced client proposition. Management expressed confidence in navigating the challenging economic environment and outlined ambitious plans for future growth, including geographical expansion and a focused approach to the mid-market segment.

## Key Financial Metrics

*   **Total AUM (FY23):** Not explicitly stated in current transcript, but Q4 FY23 ARR AUM is > ₹167,000 Crs.
*   **ARR Assets:** > ₹167,000 Crs (up 15.7% YoY for FY23)
*   **Share of ARR Assets in Total AUM:** 61%
*   **ARR Net Flows (FY23):** ₹28,059 Crs
*   **Loan Book:** ₹5,367 Crs (up 24% YoY for FY23)
*   **Total Revenues (FY23):** ₹1,569 Crs (up 2.2% YoY)
*   **Recurring Revenues (FY23):** ₹1,050 Crs (up 15.1% YoY)
*   **Share of Recurring Revenues in Operating Revenue (FY23):** 67%
*   **ARR Retentions (FY23):** 69 bps (Wealth: 70 bps, Asset Management: 69 bps)
*   **Cost to Income Ratio (FY23):** 45.8% (vs. 51.1% in FY22)
*   **Total Expenses (FY23):** ₹718 Crs (down 8.4% YoY)
*   **PAT (FY23):** ₹668 Crs (up 14.7% YoY)
*   **Tangible ROE (FY23):** 26.7%

## Business Strategy & Drivers

*   **Transition to Recurring Revenue Model:** Management emphasized continued success in transitioning to ARR, with ARR AUM growing 2.5x in the last three years and ARR revenues growing at a 30% CAGR. They aim for ARR revenue share to trend towards 80% in FY24.
*   **Geographical Expansion:** Plan to expand presence to ~40 cities domestically, plus offices in Dubai and Singapore, leveraging a lighter onboarding and execution platform.
*   **Mid-Market Segment Focus:** Development of a proposition for clients in the ₹5-50 Crs segment, expected to be live in the second half of FY24, focusing on curated products, enhanced RM span of control, and technology.
*   **Asset Management Growth:** Driven by the TrueScale transaction and new leadership, with a focus on deepening footing in India-bound institutional mandates and leveraging the growing alternatives penetration in India.
*   **Client and RM Retention:** Strong underlying metrics on client and senior relationship manager retention highlight the company's position as a preferred wealth manager and employer.
*   **Digital Transformation:** Continued acceleration of digital and technology investments to enhance client experience and operational efficiency.
*   **Rebranding to 360ONE:** The rebranding is in progress, signifying the company's holistic client-centric approach.
