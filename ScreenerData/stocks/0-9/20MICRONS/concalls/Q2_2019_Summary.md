## Executive Overview

20 Microns Limited's Q2 FY20 results, as presented, show a continued top-line growth of 12.3% YoY to Rs. 136.1 crore, primarily attributed to a favorable shift in product mix and stronger performance from value-added products. This growth was achieved despite prevailing challenges in the domestic market and a broader economic slowdown impacting sales off-take. Profit After Tax (PAT) saw a modest increase of 1.4% YoY to Rs. 7 crore. Management reiterated their focus on expanding EBITDA margins, which experienced pressure (-180 bps YoY) due to rising raw material costs for specialty products and higher inventory levels. The company continues to leverage its robust R&D capabilities to develop customized and value-added solutions, aiming to cater to over 1,000 clients across various industries.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

The company's strategic direction remains centered on enhancing its competitive position through innovation and a diversified business model:

*   **Product Innovation:** Aiming to achieve a product fineness of 0.2 microns to further improve profit margins.
*   **Retail Push:** Expansion into the B2C segment with plans for three new waterproofing products (Nanotite, Liquid Cracksil & Instantsill) in addition to the existing fertilizer brand.
*   **Specialty Products:** Continuously seeking to enter new, untapped industries to broaden the client base.
*   **Transforming Business Mix:** Shifting focus towards retail channels and product branding to drive higher-margin businesses, and exploring strategic acquisitions to expand the product portfolio and market access through global joint ventures.
*   **Global Competitiveness:** Aspiring to achieve global competitiveness through sustained product innovation, technology upgrades, and continuous quality enhancement.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Moving towards becoming a "Zero Waste Company" and leveraging established R&D facilities and collaborations with external agencies for continuous improvement and innovation.
