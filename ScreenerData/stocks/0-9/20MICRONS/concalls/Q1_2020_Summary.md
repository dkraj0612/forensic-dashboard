## Executive Overview

20 Microns Limited reported a solid Q1 FY20, with consolidated revenue growing 17% year-over-year to Rs. 137.8 crore. This growth was primarily attributed to a strategic shift in product mix towards higher-value offerings. While EBITDA saw growth, the EBITDA margin experienced a decline due to increased raw material costs for specialty products. The company is focused on mitigating this by prioritizing higher-margin products. Profit After Tax (PAT) increased by 13% year-over-year to Rs. 8.5 crore. Exports remained steady at approximately 17% of revenue. Management highlighted their continued investment in R&D and micronization expertise as key enablers for strategic client engagements and future growth.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

The company's strategic focus for Q1 FY20 and going forward includes:

*   **Product Mix Enhancement:** Driving revenue growth through a strategic shift towards higher-value products and specialty chemicals, which are expected to improve margins.
*   **R&D and Micronization Expertise:** Leveraging deep understanding of micronization technology and R&D to engage in strategic client projects, focusing on product modernization and improvement.
*   **Export Growth:** Maintaining and potentially growing the export contribution, which stood at 17% in Q1 FY20, serving approximately 48 countries.
*   **Diversification:** Expanding client base and product applications across varied industry segments beyond traditional sectors.
*   **Retail Expansion:** Continuing focus on the retail segment with products like waterproofing agents and fertilizer brands, aiming for higher margins.
*   **Operational Efficiency:** Implicitly continuing efforts towards operational improvements and cost management to offset raw material cost pressures.
