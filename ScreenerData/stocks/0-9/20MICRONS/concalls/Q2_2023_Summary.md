## Executive Overview

20 Microns Limited reported a solid Q2 FY24 and H1 FY24 performance, with revenue from operations increasing by 6.35% to ₹ 1,997.03 Mn in Q2 FY24 compared to ₹ 1,877.78 Mn in Q2 FY23. This growth was driven by increased demand in key sectors like paints, rubber, and plastics, potentially boosted by an extended festive season. EBITDA saw a significant increase of 28.25% from ₹ 233.44 Mn in Q2 FY23 to ₹ 299.40 Mn in Q2 FY24, with margins improving to 14.99% from 12.43%. Profit After Tax (PAT) also grew by 34.24% to ₹ 160.54 Mn in Q2 FY24 from ₹ 119.59 Mn in Q2 FY23, with PAT margins improving to 8.04%. Exports contributed approximately 16.0% to total sales in H1 FY24. The company continues to emphasize its R&D focus and technological upgrades, including ERP integration and automation across business functions. Expansion efforts are underway in new international regions, and the company is exploring strategic initiatives, including a potential JV for construction chemicals.

## Business Strategy & Drivers

The company's strategy remains focused on:

*   **Product Innovation & Value Addition:** Continuing to develop advanced products, including specialty chemicals and functional additives through its subsidiary, 20 Microns Nano.
*   **Technological Advancement & Automation:** Implementing ERP, CRM, and HRMS integration for improved efficiency and data utilization. Automation of various business functions like logistics tracking and purchase order creation.
*   **Market Expansion:** Expanding global footprint through distribution agreements in the Middle East and Southeast Asia. Restructuring distributor relationships to optimize efficiency.
*   **R&D Investment:** Maintaining a dedicated R&D facility with a team focused on developing innovative solutions and enhancing product portfolio.
*   **Customer-Centric Approach:** Closely collaborating with customers to understand evolving demands and adapt product offerings.
*   **Diversification:** Venturing into new product segments like mineral-based fertilizers and construction chemicals, though currently a small portion of revenue.
*   **Strategic Alliances:** Exploring potential Joint Ventures and collaborations for growth, notably a pending JV for construction chemicals and building products with a German company.

## Management Accountability & Consistency Audit

*   **Stated Targets vs. Actual Performance:**
    *   **Revenue Growth:** The company reported a 6.35% revenue growth for Q2 FY24 YoY. This is below the previously guided 15.0%-18.0% for FY24. Management confirmed they will be able to cover the H1 gap in the remaining six months to meet the annual guidance.
    *   **EBITDA & Margins:** EBITDA increased by a strong 28.25% and margins improved significantly to 14.99% in Q2 FY24, exceeding the previous year's margin of 12.43%. This performance aligns with the narrative of cost efficiencies and improved negotiations.
    *   **PAT Growth:** PAT grew by a healthy 34.24% in Q2 FY24, demonstrating effective profit generation strategies.
    *   **Strategic Shift:** The narrative continues to emphasize innovation, R&D, and technological upgrades, consistent with the historical context. The focus on expanding into new international markets and exploring JVs also aligns with prior strategic statements.

*   **Narrative/Tone Shift Regarding Headwinds:**
    *   The transcript highlights reductions in "power and fuel cost" and a substantial decrease in "export logistic costs" as key drivers for margin improvement. This indicates a successful navigation of cost pressures previously mentioned, with a positive shift in tone regarding cost management.

*   **New Forward-Looking Promises/Guidance for Next Quarter:**
    *   **Revenue Growth Guidance:** Management expects to achieve the previously stated FY24 revenue growth guidance of 15.0%-18.0%, despite a slower start in H1, by leveraging the second half of the year.
    *   **Margin Improvement Guidance:** Management views the current 15% EBITDA margin as an "optimal figure" and the "task" will be to "maintain these kinds of margins" and "sustain them, and then we will be working on improving them as well." They anticipate a likely improvement of 50-100 bps for FY24 over the existing 15% benchmark.
    *   **JV Update:** Clarity on the JV with the German company for construction chemicals is expected in "another six to eight weeks."
    *   **Product Development:** Focus on developing superior quality products with higher profitability and expanding product range in specialty chemicals and functional additives.
    *   **International Expansion:** Continued focus on Asia, specifically the Middle East, Southeast Asia, and the Indian subcontinent, over Europe and America due to economic growth prospects.
    *   **Capacity Utilization:** Current capacity utilization for standalone operations is around 85%. Management sees sufficient room for growth for the next few quarters with minimal CapEx, utilizing toll and contract manufacturing for regular products.
    *   **Future Revenue Growth:** Confident in maintaining a steady 15% to 18% YoY growth for at least the next two years. Significant CapEx plans for further expansion are being evaluated and will be shared once finalized.
    *   **New Product Contribution:** New products launched in the last three years contribute between 5% to 6% of overall sales, with potential to grow to around 10% over the next two to three years.
    *   **Value-Added Products:** Mineral fertilizers and construction chemicals currently contribute ~1-2% of revenue (around INR 10 crore), with expectations to grow 5x to INR 50 crore in the next 5 years.
    *   **CapEx:** Traditional CapEx for the next six months to one year will be INR 10 crores to INR 15 crores for machinery upgrades. Significant CapEx plans are under rework.
    *   **Debt Repayment:** Long-term debt of INR 8-10 crores is expected to be repaid within the next few months.
    *   **Interest Costs:** High interest costs are attributed to discounting charges for major customers, which is a typical practice. Regular debt interest rates are significantly lower.
