### 👤 *Annualized
Overall good quarter across AUM, asset quality and operating efficiency. During the quarter, AUM grew 23% while PBT grew 20% and PAT was up 14% YoY due to one-time tax benefit of ~34 Cr in Q4 FY25. Excluding this one-time impact, normalized PAT growth would have been 20% in Q4 FY26. Opex to NTI was better in Q4 FY26 at 19.2% as against 21.8% in Q4 FY25. Asset quality was stable during the quarter with GNPA at 27 bps and NNPA at 11 bps while annualized credit cost was 19 bps. PBC was 60.88% in Q4 FY26 as compared to regulatory requirement of 60.00%. On full year basis, PBT grew 20% and PAT increased by 18% with annualized ROA at 2.3% and ROE at 12.1% in FY26. Opex to NTI improved 120 bps during the year from 20.9% in FY25 to 19.7% in FY26.
