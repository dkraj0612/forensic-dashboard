Brightcom Group is a global technology-driven digital media company operating at the intersection of advertising, data, and intelligent monetization. Over the past two decades, the Group has built a diversified international footprint spanning advertisers, agencies, publishers, and technology partners across multiple markets. Through its integrated AdTech ecosystem, Programmatic and Platforms, Brightcom delivers scalable digital advertising solutions powered by data, automation, and performance-driven execution. With a legacy of strategic acquisitions, global expansion, and operational resilience, the Group continues to focus on innovation, technology, and sustainable value creation for shareholders. Today, Brightcom stands on a foundation built over 28 years-combining global scale, technological expertise, and strategic vision to pursue its next phase of growth.

The Company and its partners are currently engaged across approximately six major defence and security initiatives spanning the Middle East, Latin America, Europe, and allied markets. ### 👤 Active Defence Modernization Programs – Middle East
Participation in programs focused on upgrading operational capabilities of military and security organizations through the introduction of modern defence equipment, enhanced force-readiness solutions, and associated support infrastructure. These initiatives are intended to support long-term modernization objectives and operational effectiveness.

### 👤 Autonomous Aerial Systems – Middle East
Engagement in opportunities involving unmanned aerial platforms for intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance, border-security, and mission-support applications. Discussions encompass both platform deployment and broader capability-development initiatives designed to support evolving security requirements.

### 👤 Military Training and Readiness Programs – Middle East
Evaluation of opportunities involving specialized training, operational readiness enhancement, forcedevelopment initiatives, and related support services intended to strengthen institutional capabilities and improve mission effectiveness.

### 👤 Defence Equipment and Security Modernization Programs – Latin America
Participation in opportunities associated with defence modernization, security enhancement, and capability-development initiatives involving government and institutional customers. These programs may include equipment supply, technology integration, training, and long-term support requirements.

### 👤 Defence Infrastructure and Training Facilities – Europe and Allied Markets
Assessment of projects involving the design, development, and modernization of advanced training environments, tactical simulation capabilities, live-fire facilities, and related defence infrastructure intended to support military and security personnel development.

### 👤 Integrated Support, Sustainment, and Technology Programs – Multiple Regions
Exploration of opportunities involving lifecycle support, maintenance, logistics, systems integration, autonomous technologies, artificial intelligence applications, and other next-generation solutions that complement broader defence modernization efforts. In addition to the above initiatives, Brightcom Defence continues to evaluate strategic collaborations in emerging areas including autonomous systems, advanced manufacturing, security technologies, maritime surveillance applications, and mission-critical technology solutions. The Company believes that the combination of defence modernization, autonomous systems, training infrastructure, and long-term support services provides a strong foundation for the development of a diversified and sustainable defence business. Brightcom Defence remains committed to building long-term capabilities through strategic partnerships, technological innovation, and disciplined execution in the global defence and security marketplace.
