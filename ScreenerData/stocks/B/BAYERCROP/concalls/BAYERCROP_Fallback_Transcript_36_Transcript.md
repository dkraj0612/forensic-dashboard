### 👤 Weather
Normal to excess rains across the country barring some areas of deficit. Reservoirs across the country hold adequate water for Rabi crop.

### 👤 Acreages
Early onset of sowing across crops with higher sowing recorded across major kharif crops. Continuous and excess rains led to damage & re-sowing and loss of sprays in some areas/crops.

### 👤 Commodity Price
Commodity price of key kharif crops held good; early announcement of MSPs may have helped as well.

### 👤 Sales
Growth in CP on account of early placement, out manoeuvring competition with better supplies & collections. Growth in Seeds powered by Rice, Corn & Millets; increase in these mitigated lower Cotton sales

### 👤 Liquidation
Agility shown by Grower Engagement & Marketing teams in shifting all farmer connect to online/digital platforms ensured continued farmer outreach. 26% growth in Q2 Bayer Liquidation led by superior Bayer CP demand, good monsoon & early sowing.

### 👤 Rabi Outlook
Good water availability led by good monsoon augurs well for key Rabi crops. Only watch- out is delayed monsoon withdrawal has led to continuous rains in some parts, this will impact some acres/sprays or lead to delayed rabi sowings.
