### 👤 BUSINESS
Global Business of the Bank stood at Rs. 9,00,825 crore as on December 31, 2019 against Rs. 8,87,931 crore as on December 31, 2018. Domestic Business stood at Rs. 7,86,192 crore as on December 31, 2019 against Rs. 725,264 crore in December, 2018, signifying Y-o-Y increase of 8.40%.
