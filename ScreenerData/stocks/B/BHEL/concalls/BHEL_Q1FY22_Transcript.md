BHEL supplied over 85,000 cylinders of medical oxygen from its Bhopal and Haridwar manufacturing plants by diverting industrial oxygen from production activities and setting up infrastructure as well as logistics for filling oxygen cylinders from the then existing 200/300 cylinders per day to reach almost 4,000 cylinders per day.
