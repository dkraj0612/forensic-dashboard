Operating Profit for Sep 19 increased to INR 5,336 crore registering Y-o-Y and Q-o-Q growth of 23.2% and 24.8% respectively. Non-interest income increased by 40.7% led by trading gains. IT integration is in progress with roadmap of system integration in 2020. Net Interest Margin (NIM) increased to 2.81% in Q2 FY 20 from 2.62% during last quarter led by an increase in domestic margins to 2.95% in Q2FY20 from 2.73% in the previous quarter.
