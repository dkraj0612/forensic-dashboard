> India Glycols Limited announced that the National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT) has sanctioned its Scheme of Arrangement, enabling the demerger of its Bio Pharma and Spirits & Biofuel undertakings into two new entities: Ennature Bio Pharma Limited and IGL Spirits Limited, respectively.
