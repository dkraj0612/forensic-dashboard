This is to inform you that the Scheme of Amalgamation of J. B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals 
Limited ("Transferor Company") with Torrent Pharmaceuticals Limited ("Transferee Company") 
and their respective shareholders, under the provisions of Sections 230 to 232 and other applicable 
provisions of the Companies Act, 2013 ("Scheme"), read with the rules framed thereunder, has 
been sanctioned by the Hon'ble National Company Law Tribunal ("Hon'ble NCLT") vide its order 
dated July 6, 2026 ("Order"). 
 
A copy of the Order is available on the website of the Hon'ble NCLT. 
 
The Scheme shall become effective upon filing of the certified copy of the Order with the Registrar 
of Companies. The Company will intimate the Stock Exchange of the effective date in due course, 
upon the Scheme becoming effective.
