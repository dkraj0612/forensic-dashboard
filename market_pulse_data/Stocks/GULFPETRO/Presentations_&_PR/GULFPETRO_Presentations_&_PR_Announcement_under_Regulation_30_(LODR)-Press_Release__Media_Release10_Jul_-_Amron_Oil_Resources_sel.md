GP Petroleums’ Joint Venture Amron Oil Resources selected by Indian Oil 
Corporation Limited (IOCL) as Operating Partner for Pipavav Bitumen Cell 
 
Mumbai, July 10, 2026: GP Petroleums Limited (GPPL), a publicly listed petroleum products 
company, has achieved a significant milestone through its joint venture company, Amron Oil 
Resources Private Limited. 
Amron Oil Resources is a 50:50 joint venture between GP Petroleums Limited (GPPL) and West 
Coast Oils LLP. Established in 2004, the company specializes in the manufacturing and supply of 
high-performance bitumen products that support India's rapidly expanding road and 
infrastructure sector. Its product portfolio includes Bitumen Emulsions, Polymer Modified 
Bitumen (PMB) and Crumb Rubber Modified Bitumen (CRMB), solutions engineered to enhance 
road durability, performance and sustainability. 
During the year, Amron Oil Resources was selected by Indian Oil Corporation Limited (IOCL) as 
the operating partner for its Bitumen Cell at Pipavav, Gujarat. The dispatch of bulk bitumen from 
the facility was flagged off on June 4, 2026, by Shri John Prasad K., Country Head (Industrial 
Business), Marketing Head Office, IndianOil, in the presence of Shri V. M. Gade, CGM (IB), Gujarat 
State Office, Shri Abhishek Tripathi, DIBH, ADO, and Shri Aman Kumar, DIBH, RDO, along with 
officials from IndianOil, GP Petroleums Limited and Amron Oil Resources. The facility was 
inaugurated under IndianOil's SPRINT 2026 Mission Excellence initiative. 
Amron’s selection as the operating partner reflects the company’s proven technical expertise, 
operational capabilities, and long-standing industry experience in providing specialty bitumen 
solutions.  
For GPPL, this achievement underscores its commitment to building value-accretive businesses 
that address the nation's critical infrastructure requirements. Over the years, GPPL has 
strategically expanded beyond traditional petroleum products into specialised, high-growth 
segments that support India's infrastructure development. The partnership with Indian Oil 
Corporation Limited (IOCL) further reinforces this strategic direction and strengthens GPPL's 
position within the infrastructure and industrial ecosystem. 
The Pipavav Bitumen Cell is expected to play a vital role in ensuring the efficient supply of specialty 
bitumen products across Gujarat and neighbouring regions, supporting the development of 
stronger, longer-lasting roads and transportation infrastructure. Leveraging Pipavav's strategic 
port location, the facility is well-positioned to serve large-scale infrastructure projects while 
enhancing logistics efficiency and expanding market reach. 
Commenting on the development, a spokesperson for GP Petroleums Limited (GPPL) said, "The 
selection of Amron Oil Resources as Indian Oil Corporation Limited's (IOCL) operating partner for 
the Pipavav Bitumen Cell marks a significant milestone for GP Petroleums. It reflects the strength of 
our partnerships, our technical expertise, and our commitment to contributing meaningfully to 
India's infrastructure growth story. We look forward to supporting IOCL in delivering high-quality 
bitumen solutions that will help build the roads of tomorrow."

About GP Petroleums Limited 
GP Petroleums Limited is an ISO-certified public limited company engaged in the formulation, 
manufacturing and marketing of Industrial Lubricants, Automotive Lubricants, Process Oils, 
Transformer Oils, Greases and other specialty products under its trusted "IPOL" brand. 
Founded in 1973, IPOL has established itself as a leading lubricant brand with over five decades 
of industry experience. The Company operates a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility at Vasai, 
near Mumbai, with a base oil storage capacity of 15,000 KL and an annual blending capacity of 
80,000 KL. 
Its products cater to a wide range of industrial and automotive applications and conform to 
various national and international specifications, including API, JASO and ACEA. GP Petroleums 
has established a strong presence in Industrial Lubricants, Rubber Process Oils and Automotive 
Lubricants, with exports to more than 12 countries. The Company also enjoys an exclusive 
partnership with Repsol, the global Spanish multi-energy company, for the manufacturing and
