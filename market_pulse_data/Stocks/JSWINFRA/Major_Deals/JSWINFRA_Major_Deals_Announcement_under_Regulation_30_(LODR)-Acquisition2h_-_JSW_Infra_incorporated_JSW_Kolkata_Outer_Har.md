### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> JSW Infrastructure Limited has incorporated JSW Kolkata Outer Harbour Container Terminal Private Limited as a wholly-owned subsidiary, acting as a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) to execute the integrated development and operation of an outer container terminal and berths at Netaji Subhash Dock, Kolkata. This action formalizes progress on a significant Design Build Finance Operate Transfer (DBFOT) project, strategically expanding JSWIL's port infrastructure portfolio. While the initial financial outlay for incorporation is a nominal Rs. 1 lakh, this move signals a material long-term growth initiative with no immediate red flags evident in this disclosure.
