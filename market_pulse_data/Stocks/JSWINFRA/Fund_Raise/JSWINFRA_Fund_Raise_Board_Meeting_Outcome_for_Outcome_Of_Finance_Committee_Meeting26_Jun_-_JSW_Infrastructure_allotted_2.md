Exchange Board of India (Issue of Capital and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2018, as

In continuation of our earlier letters dated June 22, 2026 and June 25, 2026 on the captioned subject, we 
wish to inform you that, in respect of the Offer, which opened on June 22, 2026 and closed on June 25, 
2026, the Finance Committee at its meeting held today, i.e., June 26, 2026, approved the issue and 
allotment of 23,00,00,000 Equity Shares pursuant to Fresh Issue and transfer of 3,32,52,427 Equity Shares 
pursuant to an Offer for Sale by the Promoter Selling Shareholder to qualified institutional buyers at the 
Offer Price of ₹ 285 per Equity Share, which includes a discount of ₹ 5.35 per Equity Share (1.84% discount 
to the floor price, as determined in terms of the SEBI ICDR Regulations) aggregating to ₹ 75,02,69,41,695 
(Rupees Seven Thousand Five Hundred and Two Crore Sixty Nine Lakh Forty One Thousand Six Hundred 
and Ninety Five Only), pursuant to the Offer which includes Fresh Issue aggregating to ₹ 6555,00,00,000 
(Rupees Six Thousand Five Hundred and Fifty Five Crore Only)  and Offer for Sale by the Promoter Selling 
Shareholder aggregating to ₹ 947,69,41,695 (Rupees Nine Hundred and Forty Seven Crore Sixty Nine Lakh 
Forty One Thousand Six Hundred and Ninety Five Only). 
 
Pursuant to the allotment of Equity Shares in the Offer, the paid-up equity share capital of the Company 
stands increased from ₹ 4,20,00,03,134, comprising 2,10,00,01,567 Equity Shares of ₹ 2 each to ₹ 
4,66,00,03,134 comprising 2,33,00,01,567 Equity Shares of ₹ 2 each.  
 
Further, find attached herewith the list of allottees who have been allotted more than 5% of the Equity 
Shares in the Offer (after aggregating allotments made pursuant to Fresh Issue and Offer for Sale), 
enclosed as Annexure 1. The shareholding pattern of the Company, before and after the Offer, will be 
submitted along with the listing application in the format specified in Regulation 31 of the SEBI Listing 
Regulations.
