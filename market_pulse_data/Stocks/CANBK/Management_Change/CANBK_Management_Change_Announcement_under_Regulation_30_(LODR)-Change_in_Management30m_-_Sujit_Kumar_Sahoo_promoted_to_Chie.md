BRIEF PROFILE 
 
 
Shri Sujit Kumar Sahoo, Chief General Manager, has 29 plus years of experience in Banking. 
He has a Master of Business Administration and Diploma in Treasury, Investment and Risk 
Management. He is also a Certified Associate of Indian Institute of Bankers. He worked from 
branch level to head office level in 8 different states and across rural to metro. He has 
administrative experience of heading Ranchi Circle Office and three Regional Offices at 
Madurai, Chennai and Sambalpur. At present, he is heading Compliance & RBS Wing, Head 
Office Bengaluru.
