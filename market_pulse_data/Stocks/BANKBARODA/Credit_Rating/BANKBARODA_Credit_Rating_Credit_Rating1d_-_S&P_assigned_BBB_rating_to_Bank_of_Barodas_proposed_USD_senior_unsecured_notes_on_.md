Bank of Baroda's Proposed Senior Unsecured Notes 
Rated 'BBB' 
June 22, 2026
SINGAPORE (S&P Global Ratings) June 22, 2026--S&P Global Ratings today assigned its 'BBB' 
long-term issue rating to the U.S. dollar-denominated senior unsecured notes that the GIFT City 
branch of Bank of Baroda Ltd. proposes to issue. The bank will draw down the issuance from its 
US$4 billion medium-term note program.
We equalize the rating on the proposed notes with the issuer credit rating on Bank of Baroda 
(BBB/Stable/A-2). This reflects our expectation that the notes shall always rank equally with all 
the bank's other senior unsecured obligations. The notes will constitute direct, unconditional, 
unsecured, and unsubordinated obligations of the India-based bank.
The rating on the proposed notes is subject to our review of the final terms and conditions.
