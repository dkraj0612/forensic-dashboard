Sub: Intimation under Regulation 30 of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (“SEBI Listing Regulations”) 
- Change in Senior Management of the Company 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of SEBI Listing Regulations, we would like 
to share an update on the changes in Senior Management Personnel of the Company, as 
follows:  
1. Ms. Shanta Gupta, Functional Head – Human Resources & Administration and a member 
of the Senior Management Team of the Company will retire from the services of the 
Company with effect from the close of business hours on June 30, 2026. 
 
2. Dr. Nitin Dharmadhikari, Chief Operating Officer and a member of the Senior 
Management Team of the Company, shall be retiring from the services of the Company 
with effect from the close of business hours on June 30, 2026.  
 
3. Mr. Prasanna Deshmukh, has been appointed as Functional Head – Human Resources & 
Administration and will be designated as Senior Management Personnel with effect from 
July 01, 2026.  
 
Details required under Regulation 30 read with Para A of Schedule III (Part A) of SEBI 
Listing Regulations, along with the additional details required pursuant to SEBI Circular No. 
SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated November 11, 2024, are enclosed herewith as 
Annexure - A. 
 
You are requested to take the above information on record.
