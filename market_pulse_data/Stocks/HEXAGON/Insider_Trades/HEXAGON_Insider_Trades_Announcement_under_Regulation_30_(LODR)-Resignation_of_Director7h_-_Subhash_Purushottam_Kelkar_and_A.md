### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Hexagon Nutrition Limited announced the resignation of two directors, including Executive Director Mr. Subhash Purushottam Kelkar and Non-Executive Director Mr. Aditya Kelkar, effective July 6, 2026. While stated as a planned transition with non-compete agreements in place, this simultaneous departure post-IPO warrants attention as a potential yellow flag regarding the long-term commitment of early key stakeholders.
