The Unaudited Financial results for the quarter ended on June 30, 2026 of the Company have been reviewed by the Audit Committee and 
approved by the Board of Directors at meeting held on 23-07-2026. The Statutory Auditors have conducted a "Limited Review" of these 
results in terms of Regulation 33 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015. 
The company has officially marked a significant financial turnaround, reporting a net profit for the current quarter following a prolonged 
streak of consecutive quarterly losses. This turnaround highlights the successful execution of the company's operational restructuring 
plans and operational improvements. Like optimising product mix, the management successfully shifted focus toward high-margin paper 
variants, reducing dependency on low-yield commodities. While focusing quality upgrades across product lines, the company also focused 
on superior price realization, which allowed the company to command higher market premiums and defend margins against industry 
infiation. 
The Company operates in only one segment i.e. paper and therefore as per Ind AS 108 segment reporting is not required. 
Results for the quarter ended on June 30, 2026 are in accordance with Indian Accounting Standards ("Ind AS") as prescribed under| 
section 133 of the Companies Act, 2013 read with relevant rules issued there under.
