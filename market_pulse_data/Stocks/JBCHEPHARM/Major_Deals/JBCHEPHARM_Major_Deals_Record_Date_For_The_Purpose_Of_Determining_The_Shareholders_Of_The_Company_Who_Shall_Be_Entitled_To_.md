This is further to our earlier intimations dated 6th July, 2026 and 7th July, 2026 in connection with Scheme of 
Amalgamation of J. B. Chemicals & Pharmaceuticals Limited (“Transferor Company” or “Company” or 
“JB Chemicals”) with Torrent Pharmaceuticals Limited (“Transferee Company” or “Torrent Pharma”) and their 
respective shareholders under the provisions of Sections 230 to 232 and other applicable provisions of the 
Companies Act, 2013 (“Scheme”) read with the rules framed thereunder sanctioned by the Hon’ble National 
Company Law Tribunal, Ahmedabad Bench (‘Hon’ble NCLT’) vide its order dated 6th July, 2026 (“Order”).  
 
In terms of the Scheme, the Company has fixed Friday, 17th July, 2026, as the Record Date for the purpose of 
determining the shareholders of the Company who shall be entitled to receive fully paid-up equity shares of 
Torrent Pharma as per the share exchange ratio set out in the Scheme i.e. 51 fully paid-up equity shares of 
Torrent Pharma having face value of Rs. 5 each for every 100 fully paid-up equity shares of Re. 1 each in 
JB Chemicals.
