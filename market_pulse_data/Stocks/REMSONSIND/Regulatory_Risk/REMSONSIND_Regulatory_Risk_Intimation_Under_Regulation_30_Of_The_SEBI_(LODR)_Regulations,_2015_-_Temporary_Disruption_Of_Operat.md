Intimation under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015 – Temporary disruption of operations due to flooding 
 
Pursuant to the provisions of Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and 
Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, we wish to inform you that the operations at the Company's 
manufacturing facilities situated at A2/3 and 4, Diamond, Somnath Road, Kachigam, Near SBI Bank, Daman 
– 396210, A3/4, Somnath Industrial Estate, Somnath, Daman, Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu, 
396210 and Survey No. 146, Pardi Karia Road, Pardi, District Valsad, Gujarat – 396121 have been disrupted 
due to flooding of the factory premises caused by incessant heavy rainfall and severe waterlogging in the 
region. 
 
As a precautionary measure and considering the prevailing conditions, operations at the aforesaid 
manufacturing facilities have been temporarily suspended with effect from 23rd July, 2026. 
 
The extent of the damage to the plant and machinery, inventory and other assets is presently being assessed. 
As access to certain areas of the manufacturing facilities remains restricted due to waterlogging, the financial 
impact, if any, cannot be ascertained at this stage. 
 
The assets at the said manufacturing facilities are adequately covered under the Company's insurance 
policies. The Company has initiated all necessary measures to safeguard its assets, assess the damage and 
restore operations at the earliest. Further updates, including the impact on operations, if any, will be 
communicated as and when material information becomes available. 
 
This is for your information and record.
