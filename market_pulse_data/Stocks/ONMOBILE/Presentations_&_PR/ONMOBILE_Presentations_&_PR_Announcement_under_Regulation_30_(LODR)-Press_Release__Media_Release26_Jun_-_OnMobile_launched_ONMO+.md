Annexure II 
 
ONMO+ Smart Console Launches on Flipkart, Bringing High-Performance Premium Gaming 
to India at Low Cost  
 
Bengaluru, 26 June 2026 
 
OnMobile Global Limited announced the launch of ONMO+ on Flipkart today, marking its entry 
into India's console gaming market with a first-of-its-kind Smart Console experience. ONMO+ 
brings premium gaming to people’s screens, including TVs, laptops, tablets, and 
smartphones, making it omnipresent and eliminating the need for a gaming PC or traditional 
consoles. 
 
By leveraging Flipkart's 
nationwide 
reach, 
ONMO+ 
aims 
to make high-
performance console gaming accessible across the country. At a time when the market is 
expanding rapidly, the cost and complexity of traditional gaming hardware continue to rise, 
taking it out of reach for many. ONMO+ addresses this gap with a right-out-of-the-
box offering that includes a Smart Console with cross-platform access, 100s of ready-to-play 
premium games, and a Pro Controller. The system is designed for everyday players but built 
for pro gamers who can play their compatible AAA games on our custom-built gaming GPU 
servers, in partnership with AMD and AsRock Rack, deployed across the northern and southern 
parts of the country. 
 
"Gaming is one of the fastest-growing forms of entertainment in the world, yet premium 
gaming experiences often remain confined to a niche audience," said François-Charles 
Sirois, Executive Chairman & CEO, OnMobile. "With ONMO+, we set out to change that with a 
complete, ready-to-play gaming solution that removes the barriers associated with 
traditional console gaming. Aspiring players discover highly rated games right from the 
comfort of their existing screens, and hard-core enthusiasts are offered a powerful Smart 
Console ready to run the latest resource-intensive games. This launch also marks an 
important milestone for OnMobile as we expand beyond our B2B roots and bring our 
technology directly to consumers through Flipkart, whose strong track record of introducing 
innovative products and categories makes it an ideal partner for this launch." 
  
Sujith Agashe, Vice President, Electronics, Flipkart, said, “At Flipkart, we are constantly expanding 
the range of choices for consumers across the country, with a sharpened focus on giving them 
access to the latest technology and experiences. We are happy to partner with ONMO+ and 
allow consumers to discover and enjoy a console-quality gaming experience on the screens 
they already own." 
 
The launch comes as India's gaming industry continues to grow rapidly, with over 500 million 
gamers across mobile, TV, and PC platforms. As internet access expands, connected devices 
become more commonplace, and demand for digital entertainment rises, the market 
is opening to a new generation of gaming experiences.

About OnMobile 
OnMobile Global Limited (NSE: ONMOBILE | BSE: 532944) is a leader in mobile gaming, 
entertainment, and enterprise solutions headquartered in Bangalore, India, with operations 
across 41 countries. For over 25 years, the company has helped telecom operators to engage 
and monetise their subscriber base, reaching over 70 million monthly active subscribers 
worldwide.  
 
The ONMO+ Smart Console is its latest product, available directly to consumers via retailers 
and e-commerce platforms, as well as distributed across OnMobile’s operator ecosystem 
globally. The Smart Console is offered with a pro gaming controller and an instant access card
