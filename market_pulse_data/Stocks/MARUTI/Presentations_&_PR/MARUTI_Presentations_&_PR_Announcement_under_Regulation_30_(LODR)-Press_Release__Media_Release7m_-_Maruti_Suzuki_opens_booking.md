A New Era of Supremacy and Excitement with The New Brezza: Bookings Now Open 
New Delhi, 14 July, 2026: Maruti Suzuki India Limited (MSIL), India's leading passenger vehicle 
manufacturer, commences bookings for The New Brezza. Since its introduction in 2016, Brezza, India’s 
Highest Selling Compact SUV*, has been an ideal SUV for Indian customers. In 2026, The New Brezza will 
infuse new energy in the SUV segment and unlock a new dimension of Supremacy and Excitement by 
offering New Age Features and a lot more.   
Speaking on the announcement, Mr. Partho Banerjee, Senior Executive Officer, Marketing & Sales, 
Maruti Suzuki India Limited, said, "Brezza's extraordinary journey since its introduction in 2016 has made 
it India’s Highest Selling Compact SUV. In 10 Years, Brezza has won the trust of over 1.4 million customers 
which is a testament of its product supremacy and segment domination. In 2026, The New Brezza will 
represent a perfect synthesis of customer’s love and unlock a new dimension of excitement. We invite 
customers to book the New Brezza today and be among the first to experience it." 
Customers can book The New 2026 Brezza at their nearest Maruti Suzuki ARENA showroom or eBook by 
clicking on www.marutisuzuki.com/arena/brezza with a booking amount of INR 11 000.
