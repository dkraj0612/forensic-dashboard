Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 
2015, we hereby inform you that 3i Infotech (Thailand) Limited, a wholly owned step down subsidiary of 
the Company, has received a contract on July 9, 2026, from Krung Thai Bank Public Company Limited, 
Thailand. 
The order pertains to talent deployment with expertise into Full Stack Development, Backend 
Development (Java), Frontend Development (Angular), DevOps Engineering, Quality Assurance / Test 
Automation Engineering, Business Analysis and Solution Architecture, to support the customer’s 
application development, enhancement, testing and digital transformation initiatives. 
The agreement is for a period of 15 months from June 24, 2026 to September 20, 2027. The total value 
of the contract is THB 10,542,056.07  (approximately Rs. 3 crore), exclusive of applicable taxes. 
The requisite details as per SEBI Master Circular HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated 
January 30, 2026, are enclosed as Annexure A. The Company is making this disclosure on a voluntary 
basis as the amount involved is below the materiality threshold limits as per the aforesaid Regulation. 
The above information will also be made available on the website of the Company. 
You are requested to take the same on record.
