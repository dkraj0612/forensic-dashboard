Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 and other applicable provisions of the Securities and Exchange Board of India 
(Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, we hereby inform the Stock 
Exchanges that the Company is planning to take annual maintenance shutdown with effect from 19.06.2026 
to 02.07.2026 (estimated period of 14 days) at the factory of its 100% Subsidiary namely Vintage Coffee 
Private Limited located at Rachur Village,Veldanda Mandal, Nagarkurnool District, Telangana. 
 
 
The operations of the Company's other 100% subsidiary, Delecto Foods Private Limited shall continue 
uninterrupted during the aforesaid maintenance period. 
 
 
The Company is undertaking annual maintenance as part of its normal operational activities, with the 
objective of enhancing efficiency and ensuring the long-term sustainability of its operations.
