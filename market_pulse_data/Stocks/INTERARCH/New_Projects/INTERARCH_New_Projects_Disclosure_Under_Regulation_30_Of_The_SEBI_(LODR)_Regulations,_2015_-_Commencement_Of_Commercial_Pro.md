Subject: Disclosure under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure

Manufacturing Facility and Capacity Addition 
Dear Sir/Madam, 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Para B of Part A of Schedule III of the SEBI (Listing Obligations 
and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 ("SEBI Listing Regulations"), we wish to inform you 
that the Company has commenced commercial production of Phase-1 at its newly established 
manufacturing facility located at Kheda, Gujarat with effect from 9th July’2026 
The commencement of commercial production marks a significant milestone in the Company's 
expansion plans. The commissioning of the facility has resulted in an enhancement of the Company's 
manufacturing capacity. 
Consequent to the above capacity addition, the Company's total installed manufacturing capacity 
has increased from 201,000 MT to 221,000 MT with effect from 9th July’2026 
The details as required under Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations read with the SEBI Master 
Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated 30thJanuary, 2026 (“SEBI Circular”), 
are provided in Annexure A enclosed herewith.  
The above expansion is expected to strengthen the Company's manufacturing capabilities, improve 
operational efficiencies, enhance its ability to meet growing customer demand and support its long-
term business growth. 
This disclosure is also being made available on the website of the Company.
