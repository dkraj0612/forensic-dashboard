To, 
The Managing Director 
V-Mart Retail Limited 
Date: 10th June, 2026 
Subject: Resignation from the Position of Chief Operating Officer and Senior Management 
Personnel 
Dear Lalit Ji & Respected Board Members, 
I would like to formally submit my resignation from my current role. 
This has been an extremely difficult decision for me given the deep association, trust, and 
support I have received from the organization over the years. I remain sincerely grateful for the 
opportunities, guidance, and confidence reposed in me throughout my journey. 
At this stage of my career, I have decided to pursue a new opportunity aligned with a broader 
mandate and future growth aspirations. The decision has been made after considerable 
thought and reflection. 
I remain fully committed to ensuring a smooth and seamless transition and will extend all 
possible support in handing over responsibilities during this period. 
I confirm that the reason stated above is the sole reason for my resignation and that there are 
no other material reasons for my resignation. 
I would like to once again sincerely thank you, the Board, and the entire leadership team for the 
trust, support, and learning throughout my association with the organization. I will always 
remain deeply grateful for the opportunities provided to me. 
Warm regards, 
Vineet Jain
