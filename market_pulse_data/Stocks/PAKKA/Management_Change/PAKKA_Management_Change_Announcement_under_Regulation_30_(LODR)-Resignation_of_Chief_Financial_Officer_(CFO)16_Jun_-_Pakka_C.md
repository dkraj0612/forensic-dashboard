Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015 ("Listing Regulations"), read with Para A of Part A of Schedule III 
thereto, we hereby inform that Mrs. Neetika Suryawanshi, Chief Financial Officer (Key 
Managerial Personnel) of the Company, has tendered her resignation from the position of 
Chief Financial Officer (KMP) of the Company. Her resignation has been accepted by Mr. 
Ved Krishna, Managing Director (KMP) of the Company, and she shall be relieved from 
her duties upon closure of business hours on June 30, 2026.

rendered by Mrs. Neetika Suryawanshi during her tenure as Chief Financial Officer of the 
Company.
