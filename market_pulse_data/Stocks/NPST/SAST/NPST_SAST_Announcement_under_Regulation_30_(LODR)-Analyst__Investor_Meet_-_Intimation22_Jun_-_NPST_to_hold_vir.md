ABOUT
Network People Services Technologies Limited (NPST) is a leading payment technology 
company powering the digital banking and payments ecosystem. NPST develops and manages 
mission-critical payment infrastructure that enables banks, fintechs, and enterprises to process 
digital transactions securely and at scale.
Guided by its vision to drive digital transformation across the financial value chain, NPST 
operates through three strategic business verticals: Technology Service Provider (TSP), 
Payments Platform-as-a-Service (PPaaS), and Regulatory Technology (RegTech). Founded in 
2013, the Company delivers technology solutions which helps institutions modernize 
infrastructure, accelerate innovation, improve transaction efficiency, enhance customer 
experiences, and strengthen operational resilience.
NPST’s product portfolio includes UPI and IMPS switches, CBDC switch, Bharat Connect (BBPS), 
UPI 123Pay, Omnichannel Super App, Bank-in-a-Box, Evok 3.0, merchant orchestration, and AI-
powered Risk Intelligence Decisioning Platform (RIDP). Its products cater to a diverse customer 
base comprising banks, fintechs, government institutions, merchants, and enterprises.
FY26 marked a significant year in NPST's strategic evolution and growth journey. Over the last 
four years, the Company has delivered a revenue CAGR of 81%, EBITDA CAGR of 103%, and 
profit CAGR of 128%, underscoring the scalability of its business model, the strength of its 
technology platforms, and its growing role in India's rapidly expanding digital payments 
ecosystem.
