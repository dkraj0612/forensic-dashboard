MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING 
This Memorandum of Understanding is entered on 03™ July, 2026 at Patna, Bihar 
Between 
The Government of Bihar 
And 
Housing and Urban Development Corporation Limited 
. This MoU is made to facilitate HUDCO for planning, development and financing of the 
proposed Greenfield Satellite Cities in Bihar in a time bound manner as per the rules and 
regulations of the State Government. 
. HUDCO shall provide term loans to the extent of Rs 1,00,000 (One Lakh) Crore, over a 
period of 5 years, for various Urban infrastructure projects including land acquisition. 
. The loan shall be availed in tranches by the statutory authority specifically designated by 
the State Government. 
. HUDCO shall provide funds on flexible terms and conditions, including a moratorium period 
with a flexible repayment schedule up to 25 years, with the option of prepayment. 
. The repayment of the loan shall be made from escrowing the revenue generated from the 
project or by ringfencing other sources of revenues coming from identified receivables/ 
state budget so as to ensure timely payments to HUDCO. 
. Separate operational agreements with specified scope of work and terms and conditions, 
shall be executed based on mutually agreed terms between the Parties, keeping in view 
of the requirements of the projects. 
. This MoU is executed to put on record the intent of Parties to undertake the activities as 
stated. This MoU shall remain valid for 3 years from the date of execution subject to annual 
review.
