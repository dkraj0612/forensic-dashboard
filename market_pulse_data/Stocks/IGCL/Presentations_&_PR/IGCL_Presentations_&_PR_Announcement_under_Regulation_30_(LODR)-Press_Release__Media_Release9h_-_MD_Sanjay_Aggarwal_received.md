PRESS RELEASE 
 
Indogulf Cropsciences' Managing Director Shri Sanjay Aggarwal receives the 17th Visionary 
Leadership Award 2026 
 
New Delhi, 9th July 2026: Indogulf Cropsciences Limited is pleased to announce that its Managing 
Director, Shri Sanjay Aggarwal, has been conferred with the prestigious "17th Visionary 
Leadership Award 2026" by the Agriculture Leadership Awards Committee. 
 
The award recognizes Shri Aggarwal's visionary leadership and significant contribution towards 
advancing sustainable crop protection solutions, driving innovation in agriculture, and empowering 
farmers through technology-led and quality-focused initiatives. 
 
The award was presented by Shri Rajnath Singh, Union Minister of Defence, during the 
Agriculture Leadership Conclave 2026 held on 8th July 2026 in New Delhi. 
 
Commenting on this recognition, Shri Sanjay Aggarwal, Managing Director, Indogulf Cropsciences 
Limited, said: 
 
"I am deeply honoured to receive this recognition. This award reflects the dedication and collective 
efforts of the entire Indogulf Cropsciences team towards supporting Indian agriculture through 
innovation, quality, and sustainable farming solutions. We remain committed to creating long-term 
value for farmers and contributing to the growth and resilience of the agricultural ecosystem, which is 
the backbone of our nation." 
 
The Agriculture Leadership Awards were instituted in 2008 to recognize individuals and 
organizations that have made outstanding contributions towards the development of Indian 
agriculture, rural prosperity, and sustainable growth. 
 
About Indogulf Cropsciences Limited 
Founded in 1993 and headquartered in New Delhi, Indogulf Cropsciences Ltd is engaged in the 
manufacturing and distribution of crop protection products, plant nutrients and biologicals. The 
Company has presence across 34+ countries and offers a diversified portfolio of 300+ products 
supported by 990+ registrations. Indogulf operates manufacturing facilities across Haryana and 
Jammu & Kashmir and remains focused on innovation, quality and sustainable agricultural solutions.
