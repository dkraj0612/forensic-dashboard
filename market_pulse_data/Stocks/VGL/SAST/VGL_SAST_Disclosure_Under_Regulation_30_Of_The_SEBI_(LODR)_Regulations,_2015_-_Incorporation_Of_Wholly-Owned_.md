We refer to our disclosure dated May 27, 2026, pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI 
(Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (11 SEBI Listing 
Regulations"), related to proposed incorporation of wholly-owned subsidiary (WoS).

granted by the Registrar of Companies, the Company's wholly-owned subsidiary, Varvee Energy 
Private Limited, has been incorporated on July 07, 2026.
