Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Pursuant to provisions of Regulation 30 and other applicable regulations, if any, of the SEBI (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (“SEBI Listing Regulations, 2015”), we 
would like to inform you that the Company has acquired an additional 37,00,000 equity shares of ABREL 
Century Energy Limited (‘ABRELCEL’), a Special Purpose Vehicle (‘SPV') by way of right issue. 
 
We further wish to inform that ABRELCEL has completed the requisite corporate action for credit of 
37,00,000 equity shares to the Company’s Demat Account. As per Communication received from 
ABRELCEL and the said shares were credited to the Company’s Demat Account Today, 23rd July 2026. 
 
The said transaction is required to maintain minimum twenty-six percentage (26%) of the equity shares 
of the Power Producer to qualify as a Captive User of the Power Producer as per the provisions of the 
Electricity Act, 2003 read with Electricity Rules, 2005. 
 
The details of the above acquisition (as required to be furnished pursuant to Regulation 30  read with 
Para A of Part A of Schedule III of SEBI Listing Regulations and SEBI Circular No.

as Annexure -I. 
 
This is for your information and records.
