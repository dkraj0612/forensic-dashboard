### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Century Enka Ltd. has executed a Power Purchase Agreement with ABREL Century Energy Limited (an SPV) for a 9.9 MW wind-solar hybrid plant, securing a captive and renewable power source for its Pune facility. The primary material impact and a red flag is the significant one-year delay in the project's scheduled commissioning, now pushed to June 2027, impacting the timeline for benefits realization. While this is noted as a related party transaction, it is declared to be at arm's length.
