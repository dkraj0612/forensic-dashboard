Vadodara, July 21, 2026: Viviana Power Tech Limited (“the Company”) (NSE: VIVIANA), a 
specialist in power transmission, distribution and industrial EPC, today announced the successful 
commissioning of its 400 kV Double-Circuit (D/C) LILO transmission line serving a 323.4 MW wind 
park. The line was energised on July 10, 2026, completing a technically demanding scope within 
a compressed execution window - a milestone that underscores the speed, agility and engineering 
depth of the Company’s project teams working under significant pressure.

PROJECT MILESTONE  
The newly commissioned line evacuates power from a 323.4 MW wind park onto the high-voltage 
grid, forming a critical link in the region’s renewable-energy transmission network. Delivering a 400 
kV double-circuit LILO configuration on an accelerated timeline required precise planning, close 
coordination with grid authorities and disciplined on-site execution - all achieved while maintaining 
the Company’s stringent safety standards. 
 
KEY ENGINEERING CHALLENGES OVERCOME  
Despite the tight timeline, the Company’s teams successfully navigated a series of high-risk site 
conditions: 
▪ High-risk power-line crossings:  Safely executed three major high-voltage intersections - a 
765 kV under-crossing, a ±500 kV HVDC under-crossing and a 400 kV overhead crossing - 
deploying a 100 MT crane to erect two 97 MT extension towers. 
▪ Live-line (hot-line) stringing:  Completed a critical 200-metre stringing section (AP-07 to AP-
08) while adjacent circuits remained live, avoiding a total grid shutdown and minimising 
disruption to the network.

ABOUT VIVIANA POWER TECH LIMITED 
Viviana Power Tech Ltd. is an EPC company engaged in power transmission, distribution, and 
industrial electrical projects across India. The company provides comprehensive solutions 
including the supply, erection, testing, and commissioning of transmission lines, development of 
EHV substations, underground cabling, and modernization of existing power systems. 
It has successfully executed projects of 400/220/132/66/33 KV levels for government utilities, 
private entities, and renewable energy developers. Backed by strong engineering capabilities and 
quality-driven operations, Viviana Power Tech operates through two verticals — supply of high-
quality materials and equipment, and service excellence through technical expertise. 
For FY26 (Consolidated), the company reported revenue from operations of ₹531 crore, EBITDA 
of ₹76 crore, and Net Profit of ₹53 crore.
