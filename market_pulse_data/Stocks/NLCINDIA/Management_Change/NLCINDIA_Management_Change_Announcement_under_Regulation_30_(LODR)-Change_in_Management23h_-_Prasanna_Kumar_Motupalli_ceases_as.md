In terms of Regulation 30 of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing Obligations 
and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, we   write   to   inform   that  
Shri Prasanna Kumar Motupalli, Chairman and Managing Director of the Company has 
ceased to hold the office of Director of the Company upon attaining the age of 
superannuation on 30.06.2026. 
 
            It is also informed that Ministry of Coal, the Administrative Ministry has entrusted the  
Additional Charge of the post of Chairman and Managing Director (CMD) to Shri Sanoj 
Kumar Jha, Additional Secretary, MoC and Nominee Director, NLC India Limited, for an 
initial period of (03) three months w.e.f. 01.07.2026 or till the appointment of a 
regular incumbent to the post or until further orders, whichever is the earliest.
