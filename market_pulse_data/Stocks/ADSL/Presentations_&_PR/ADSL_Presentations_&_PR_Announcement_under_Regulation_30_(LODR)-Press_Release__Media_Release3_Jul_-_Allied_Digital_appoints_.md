Allied Digital Services Ltd. appoints Arun Pathak as CEO 
- Cloud and Infrastructure Services (CIS) for India and 
Middle East 
                                       
 
 
Mumbai, July 3, 2026 — Allied Digital Services Limited (BSE: 532875 | NSE: ADSL), a leading global 
provider of IT services and digital transformation solutions, has announced the appointment of Mr. 
Arun Pathak as Chief Executive Officer – Cloud and Infrastructure Services (CIS) for the India and 
Middle East regions. 
In this role, Mr. Pathak will lead the growth, strategy, and operations of Allied Digital's CIS business 
across the region, with a focus on expanding managed services, strengthening enterprise customer 
relationships, and accelerating business growth. 
Mr. Pathak brings over three decades of leadership experience across managed services, cloud, 
cybersecurity, enterprise networking, and digital infrastructure. He has a proven track record of 
scaling businesses, leading large teams, and delivering complex digital transformation programs for 
leading enterprises across India and the SAARC region. 
Prior to joining Allied Digital, he spent nearly two decades at NTT DATA India, most recently as 
Executive Head – Cloud & Cybersecurity, where he led business growth, go-to-market strategy, and 
the expansion of cloud, cybersecurity, and managed services capabilities. Earlier, he also led the 
company's Managed Network & Collaboration Services business and successfully managed several of 
its largest strategic customer relationships.

His appointment further strengthens Allied Digital's leadership team as the Company continues to 
enhance its capabilities across cloud, infrastructure, cybersecurity, AI-enabled operations, and 
managed services, supporting its next phase of growth. 
Commenting on the appointment, Mr. Nitin Shah, CMD (Chairman & Managing Director), ADSL, 
said “We are delighted to welcome Arun Pathak to Allied Digital. With the industry witnessing 
unprecedented growth and transformation, Allied Digital is strategically reshuffling its top 
management, bringing together experienced leaders and dynamic young minds to thrive in an AI-driven 
world. His extensive experience across managed services, cloud, and cybersecurity, combined with a 
proven track record of building businesses and leading high-performing teams, makes him ideally 
positioned to lead our Cloud and Infrastructure Services business across India and the Middle East. We 
look forward to his leadership as we continue to strengthen our market position and deliver long-term 
growth." 
Mr. Arun Pathak, CEO, Cloud and Infrastructure Services (India and Middle East), ADSL, said: 
"I am delighted to join Allied Digital at this exciting stage of its growth. The Company has built a strong 
reputation as a trusted partner across managed services, cloud, cybersecurity, and digital 
transformation. 
As AI reshapes enterprise technology, customers are seeking partners who can deliver intelligent, 
scalable, and outcome-driven solutions. Allied Digital is well positioned to meet these evolving needs 
through its strong capabilities, global delivery platform, and talented teams. I look forward to working 
with our customers and colleagues across India and the Middle East to strengthen client relationships, 
expand our market presence, and drive sustainable growth."
