Further to our earlier intimation dated June 23, 2026 whereby the Company had informed the stock 
Exchange regarding the proposed acquisition of equity shares of Penta Automation System Private 
Limited (Penta), pursuant to Regulation 30 read with SEBI (Listing Obligation and Disclosure 
Requirement) Regulations, 2015, we wish to inform you that the acquisition transaction has been 
successfully completed on 3rd July, 2026 i.e. Closing Date in accordance with the terms and conditions of 
the Share Purchase Agreement(s). 
 
Consequent of completion of the transaction, the Company has acquired 80% of the equity share capital 
of Penta and accordingly the Penta has now become a subsidiary of Batliboi Limited. 
 
Pats Robotics Private Limited (PAtS), a group company of Penta, has been acquired by Penta as its 
wholly –owned subsidiary as on the closing date i.e. 3rd July, 2026. Consequently, PAtS has become an  
indirect subsidiary of Batliboi Limited. 
 
The details required under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (LODR) Regulations 2015,  were already disclosed 
to the Stock Exchanges vide our earlier intimation dated 23rd June 2026. This disclosure is being made to 
inform the Stock Exchanges regarding the completion of the acquisition. 
Kindly take the above information on record.
