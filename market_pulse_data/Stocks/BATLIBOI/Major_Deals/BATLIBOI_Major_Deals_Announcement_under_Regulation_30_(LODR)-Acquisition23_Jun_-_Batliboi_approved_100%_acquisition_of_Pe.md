### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Batliboi Limited is strategically acquiring 100% of Penta Automation Systems Private Limited, including its subsidiary PAtS Robotics, for approximately INR 19.8 crores to significantly expand its presence in industrial automation and robotics. This cash-based acquisition, structured with 80% upfront and 20% deferred over five years to ensure management continuity, diversifies Batliboi's portfolio and aims for long-term value creation. The target group demonstrated strong revenue growth in FY26, despite some prior year volatility in its smaller subsidiary, and the transaction is confirmed to be at arm's length.

Dear Sir/ Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 
2015 (“SEBI Listing Regulations”), read with Para A of Part A of Schedule III thereof and the SEBI Master 
Circular for compliance with the provisions of the SEBI Listing Regulations by listed entities, we wish to 
inform you that the Board of Directors of Batliboi Limited (“the Company”), at its meeting held today, 
i.e., June 23 2026, has approved the proposal to acquire 100% (one hundred percent) of the paid-up 
equity share capital of Penta Automation Systems Private Limited (“Penta”) from its existing 
shareholders i.e Mr. Dharmesh Mistry, Ms. Avani Mistry and Manders Industries B.V. (Netherlands), by 
way of a secondary acquisition of shares through a mix of upfront acquisition of 80% and balance 
acquisition of 20% over 5 years in a manner elaborated in Annexure A through definitive Share Purchase 
Agreement(s), Shareholders’ Agreement, and other ancillary agreements to be entered into with the 
shareholders of Penta. Mr. Dharmesh Mistry and Mrs. Avani Mistry will continue to be employed by 
Penta for the said period of 5 years. 
 
Pats Robotics Private Limited (“PAtS”), a group company of Penta, is proposed to be acquired by Penta 
as its wholly-owned subsidiary prior to Closing; accordingly, upon completion of the proposed 
acquisition, PAtS will become an indirect wholly-owned subsidiary of the Company upon acquisition of 
Penta. 
 
The Board Meeting commenced at 12:00 p.m. and concluded at 12:45 p.m. 
 
The details of the proposed acquisition, as required under Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations 
read with Para A of Part A of Schedule III thereof, are set out in “Annexure A” to this letter.
