Subject: Change in Senior Management of the Company 
 
Dear Sir/ Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of the Securities and Exchange Board 
of India (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, and 
amendments thereof, this is to inform you that following changes have taken place in the 
Senior Management of the Company: 
 
1. Shri Vinod Kumar Paswan, SAG/IRSE has joined RITES on 17.07.2026 on 
deputation basis as Group General Manager (Civil), Regional Head / Eastern 
Region Project Office / Kolkata w.e.f. 17.07.2026 and has accordingly been 
included in the Senior Management of the Company. 
 
2. Shri Alok Bansal, GGM (Civil) / Former RH/ERPO has been transferred from 
ERPO to PU Lucknow. Consequently, he has ceased to be the part of Senior 
Management of the Company w.e.f. 17.07.2026. 
 
In this regard, relevant disclosures pursuant to SEBI Circular are placed at Annexure-A. 
 
Kindly take this information on record and acknowledge the same. 
 
Thanking You,
