Dear Sirs/Madam, 
Subject: Update – Purchase Orders from Vale, Brazil 
Scrip Code: Thejo – EQ 
 
We wish to inform you that pursuant to a three-year supply contract secured from Vale, Brazil, 
for THOR-R and THOR-M Mill Liners, and following the strong performance demonstrated 
during plant trials, the Company has received the first tranche of orders from Vale, Brazil, an 
international entity.  These orders cover the supply of the initial two complete sets under the 
contract and are scheduled for supply by October, 2026. 
 
The combined value of this initial tranche is USD 0.89 million (approximately ₹8.46 crore). 
 
The purchase order will not fall under related party transaction, and the promoter/promoter 
group/group companies do not have any interest in the entity awarding the purchase order. 
 
As this is a purchase order received in the normal course of business and not covered under 
Regulation 30 (r.w. Schedule III) of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015, this may be treated as an update for the information of stakeholders.
