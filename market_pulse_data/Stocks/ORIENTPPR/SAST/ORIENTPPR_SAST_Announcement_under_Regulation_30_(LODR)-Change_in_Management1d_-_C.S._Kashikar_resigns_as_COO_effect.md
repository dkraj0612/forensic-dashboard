Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Schedule III, Part A, Para A (7) of the SEBI Listing Regulations, we

Management Personnel of the Company as per Regulation 16(1)(d) of SEBI Listing Regulations) with 
effect from 30th June, 2026.
