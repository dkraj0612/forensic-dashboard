Sub: Intimation pursuant to Regulation 30 of SEBI (LODR) Regulation, 2015 
 
Dear Sir/ Madam, 
 
The operations of one of the Company's plastic processing Units located at Vasona 
were stopped today (23rd July 2026) morning due to flooding inside the factory area 
on account of heavy rains in the Silvassa area in the Union Territory of Dadra & Nagar 
Haveli and Daman & Diu since yesterday. The Company has taken adequate 
measures to ensure safety of the equipment, material and personnel. 
 
The Company is currently assessing the impact of the damages, if any and the effect 
on the operations. Subject to the improvement in weather, we expect that the situation 
may start normalizing in the next few days gradually. 
 
The Company will inform the Stock Exchanges once the operation of the said Unit is 
resumed.

letter.
