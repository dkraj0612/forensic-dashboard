Detailed Rationale
CRISIL has reaffirmed its 'CRISIL BB/Stable/CRISIL A4+' ratings on the bank facilities of Gujarat Raffia Industries
Limited (GRIL).
 
The ratings continue to reflect the extensive experience of the promoter in manufacturing high density polyethylene
(HDPE)/polypropylene (PP) products, and GRIL's moderate financial risk profile. These strengths are partly offset by
the modest scale of operations, large working capital requirement, and the exposure to volatility in raw
material
prices.
Key Rating Drivers & Detailed Description
Strengths
* Extensive experience of the promoter
Benefits from the promoter's experience of three decades, his strong understanding of local market dynamics,
and
healthy relations with suppliers and customers should continue to support the business.
* Moderate financial risk profile
Networth was Rs 16.5 crore and gearing low at 0.55 time as on March 31, 2019. Debt protection metrics are
above-
average with interest coverage and net cash accrual to total debt ratios of over 4 times and 0.20 time, respectively, in
fiscal 2019.
Weaknesses
* Modest scale of operations
Revenue was average at Rs 48 crore in fiscal 2019 with near to full utilisation of capacity. Scale may remain
small
over the medium term and any increase therein remains contingent on enhanced capacities.
* Large working capital requirement
The working capital cycle is likely to remain stretched over the medium term. Gross current assets were more
than
200 days as on March 31, 2019, driven by high inventory and debtors.
* Exposure to volatility in raw material prices
Since cost of procuring the major raw material (polymer) accounts for 75-80% of total production cost, even a
slight
variation in rates may drastically impact profitability. Further, intense competition and the need to maintain a
large
inventory exposes the business to any adverse fluctuation in raw material price.
Liquidity: Adequate
The company is expected to generate annual accruals of Rs. 2 cr against which it does not have any term
debt
obligations. The bank limit utilization is moderate below 50% of sanctioned working capital limit. While operations are
working capital intensive, modest topline means that the incremental working capital requirements are
adequately
funded from internal accruals.
