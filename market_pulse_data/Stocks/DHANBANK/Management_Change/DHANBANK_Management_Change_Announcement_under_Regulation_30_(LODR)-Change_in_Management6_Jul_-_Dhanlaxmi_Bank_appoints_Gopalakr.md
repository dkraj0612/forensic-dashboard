Brief Profile 
Mr. Gopalakrishnan V.N is a seasoned banker with more than 
40 years of experience across various financial institutions 
such as ESAF Small Finance Bank, Federal Bank Limited, 
EXIM Bank. Mr. Gopalakrishnan V.N was the Deputy Vice 
President in the Vigilance department of ESAF Small Finance 
Bank, where he was in the Vigilance department for 6 years 
and has handled investigations, Preventive Vigilance and 
Training. He was the AGM - Operations in EXIM Bank, 
Djibouti, East Africa. He had extensive exposure to Forex & 
Trade Finance, Credit, Branch Banking, Vigilance, and Risk 
Management during his tenure in Federal Bank Ltd. Mr. 
Gopalakrishnan V.N has wide experience in varied areas of 
banking including core banking operations, vigilance, fraud 
prevention & compliance, risk management, credit, forex & 
trade finance, governance, and ESG across various financial 
institutions. 
Mr. Gopalakrishnan V.N holds an MBA and a degree of 
Bachelor in Law. He is also a Certified Associate of Indian 
Institute of Bankers (CAIIB) and also holds a Certification in 
Small Finance Bank Operations.
