RATING ACTION / WITHDRAWAL 
Brickwork Ratings has withdrawn the rating of Basel III Tier II Bonds of Rs. 150 Crores (present 
outstanding nil) of Dhanlaxmi Bank Ltd (DBL) on redemption in full at its maturity date of          
29 Mar 2025. The issuer (DBL) has confirmed the due redemption in full of the aforesaid bonds 
including interest at its maturity. The debenture trustee confirms timely payment of principal / 
interest and has no dues outstanding. Withdrawal of Rating is in compliance with BWR Rating 
Withdrawal Policy. 
KEY COVENANTS OF THE INSTRUMENT/FACILITY RATED:  
Basel III Bonds of this nature are susceptible to loss absorbency features as per the terms of the 
issue and RBI’s extant guidelines. The key distinguishing feature of these bonds is the existence 
of the Point of Non-Viability (PONV) trigger and the occurrence of such a PONV trigger event 
may at the option of the RBI be temporarily or permanently written - off, resulting in permanent 
loss of the principal to the investor and a default on the instrument to the investor. Basel III Tier II 
bonds are redeemable after 7 years from the deemed date of allotment with coupon servicing at 
annual intervals. The bonds are subject to the extant guidelines of the RBI applicable to Tier II 
Basel III-compliant bonds issued by banks.

COMPANY PROFILE 
Incorporated in 1927 at Thrissur, Kerala, Dhanlaxmi Bank (the bank)’s major presence is in states 
of Kerala (58%), followed by Tamil Nadu (14%), 7% Maharashtra, 5% in Karnataka and 
remaining in other states. It has over 560 Customer Touch Points including 261 Branches, 282 
ATMs and 17 BCs across, Metro Branches – 58, Urban Branches – 71, Semi-Urban Branches 
–112 and Rural Branches – 20 as on 31 Mar 2025. The Bank has its presence primarily in the 
southern region. The bank is led by Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer Shri. Ajith 
Kumar K.K who has over 36 years of experience in the banking industry.
