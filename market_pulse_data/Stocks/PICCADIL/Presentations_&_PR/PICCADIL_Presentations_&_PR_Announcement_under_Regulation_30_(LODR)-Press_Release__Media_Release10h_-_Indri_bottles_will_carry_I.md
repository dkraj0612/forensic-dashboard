About Piccadily Agro Industries Limited (PAIL) 
 
Piccadily Agro Industries Limited (PAIL) is a publicly listed company with state-of-the-art 
manufacturing facilities in Indri, Haryana and Chhattisgarh, equipped with advanced technology 
for the production of Malt Spirit, Extra Neutral Alcohol (ENA) and Ethanol. 
 
The company has emerged as a prominent player in the premium spirits segment, with award-
winning brands such as Indri Single Malt, Camikara, Cashmir, and Whistler gaining strong 
recognition across domestic and international markets. 
 
PAIL has further strengthened its leadership position in the Indian single malt category with 
Indri Single Malt becoming the fastest-growing single malt whisky brands globally. Driven by a 
strong focus on innovation, premiumization and strategic capacity expansion, the company 
continues to build a future-ready business while creating long-term value for its stakeholders.
