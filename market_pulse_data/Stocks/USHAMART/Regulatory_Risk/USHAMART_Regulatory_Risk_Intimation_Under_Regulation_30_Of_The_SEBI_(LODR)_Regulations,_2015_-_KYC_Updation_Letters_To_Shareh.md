Dear Shareholder, 
 
We draw your kind attention that Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) vide its Master Circular No.HO/38/13/(4)2026-
MIRSD-POD/I/4298/2026 dated February 06, 2026, has mandated all holders of physical securities to furnish their KYC (viz., PAN, 
postal address with pin code, mobile number, bank account details and specimen signature). 
 
Upon reviewing our records, we note that you have not yet furnished the mandatory documents/ details against your holdings. 
 
Please note that you will be eligible to lodge grievances, submit service requests with the RTA, and receive any payments, 
including dividends, in respect of such folios only through electronic mode after completing the above-mentioned KYC 
compliance requirements. 
 
For the purpose of updation of KYC details in your folio, kindly submit the following forms along with the supporting documents: 
 
i. Form  ISR-1 duly filled in along with self-attested supporting documents as stated therein. 
ii. Form ISR-2 duly filled in for banker’s attestation of signature along with Original cancelled cheque with your name(s) printed               
thereon or self-attested copy of bank passbook/statement. 
 
Further, you are also requested, in your own interest, to provide ‘choice of nomination’ with respect to your folio(s). Relevant 
forms for providing ‘choice of nomination’ are mentioned below.  
 
i. Form SH-13 for updation of Nomination 
ii. Form SH-14 for cancellation of Nomination or change in existing Nomination 
iii. Form ISR-3 for Opting out of Nomination  
 
The above said forms can be downloaded from the website of the Company at: https://ushamartin.com/investor-relations/investor-
information/others#Physical_Shareholders or from the website of the RTA at https://ris.kfintech.com/clientservices/isc/isrforms.aspx
