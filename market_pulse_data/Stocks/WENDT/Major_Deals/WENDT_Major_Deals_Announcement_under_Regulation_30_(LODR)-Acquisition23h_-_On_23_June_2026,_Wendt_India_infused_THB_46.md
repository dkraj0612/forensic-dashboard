Sub: Disclosure under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2015 – Capital infusion in Wholly owned 
subsidiary 
 
We wish to inform you that the Company has on 23rd June 2026 infused further capital into its 
wholly owned subsidiary in Thailand – M/s. Wendt Grinding Technologies Limited (WGTL).  
 
WGTL was incorporated in the year 2005 with a registered equity capital of 103,000,000 Baht 
(THB). The Company’s paid-up equity capital as on 23rd June 2026 was THB 25,750,000. While 
the registered capital comprised of 10,300,000 equity shares of THB 10 each, the paid up 
capital was partly paid at THB 2.50 Baht per share. The Board of the subsidiary has called for 
the remaining capital to be paid up in one or more tranches. Accordingly, in line with the local 
applicable laws, the Company has today paid up the capital in the WGTL to the extent of THB 
4.50 per share aggregating to THB 46,350,000 (approximately INR 13.29 crores). The balance 
THB 3.00 per share will be infused by the Company in due course.  
 
The details as required under Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of SEBI (Listing Obligations 
and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 read with and SEBI master Circular dated 30th 
January 2026, are enclosed as Annexure.
