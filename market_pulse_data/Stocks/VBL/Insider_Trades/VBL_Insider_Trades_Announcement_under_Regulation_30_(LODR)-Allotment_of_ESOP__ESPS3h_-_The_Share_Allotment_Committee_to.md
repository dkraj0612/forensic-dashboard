This is to inform you that the Share Allotment Committee of the Board of Directors of the 
Company today (started at 3:30 P.M. and concluded at 3:45 P.M.) approved the allotment of 
1,68,750 Equity Shares of face value of Rs. 2/- each of the Company to the eligible employees 
upon exercise of stock options vested under Employees Stock Option Scheme 2016 (“ESOP 
2016”).

338,22,96,144 Equity Shares to 338,24,64,894 Equity Shares having face value of Rs. 2/- 
each.

as “Annexure - I”.
