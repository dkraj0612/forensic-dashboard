 Dear Sir / Madam,  
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Part A of Schedule III of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and 
Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (“SEBI Listing Regulations”), we hereby inform 
you that Anik Industries Limited (the “Company”) alongwith its material subsidiary i.e., Revera 
Milk and Foods Private Limited has acquired a 50% stake and has become a partner in Best 
Season Developers LLP w.e.f., 17th June, 2026. 
 
Following the acquisition, Company alongwith its material subsidiary i.e., Revera Milk and 
Foods Private Limited holds 50% ownership interest in the aforesaid LLP. The meeting 
commenced at 7:00 pm IST and concluded at 7:30 pm IST. 
 
Further details as per SEBI Circular No. HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated
