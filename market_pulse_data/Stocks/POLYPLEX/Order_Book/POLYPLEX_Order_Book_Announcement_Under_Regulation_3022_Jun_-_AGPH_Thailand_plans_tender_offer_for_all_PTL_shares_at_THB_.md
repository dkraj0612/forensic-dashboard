We wish to inform you that Polyplex (Thailand) Public Company Limited, Thailand (PTL), a 
step-down subsidiary of the Company listed on Stock Exchange of Thailand, has today i.e. 
June 22, 2026, received a copy of the announcement submitted by AGPH (Thailand) Ltd., 
Thailand (Tender Offeror), to the office of the Securities and Exchange Commission and the 
Stock Exchange of Thailand, intimating its intention to make a tender offer to acquire all of 
the ordinary shares of PTL.

enclosure.

AGP Holdco Limited, Dubai UAE, which holds 76,21,390 (24.28%) equity shares of the face 
value of Rs. 10/- each of the Company.
