Intimation regarding Fire Incident  
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read along with Para-B, Part-A of Schedule III of the SEBI Listing 
Regulations, it is hereby informed that on Thursday, July 2, 2026, at around 9:30 PM, fire broke out at 
Company’s Factory Unit- No. 1 situated at Plot No. 47, 100 to 103, 103/A, GVVM. Odhav,                  
Ahmedabad - 382415, Gujarat, India. A portion of packing material store area impacted wherein there 
are few raw materials, packing material along with one FFE machine were lying. 
 
There have been no injuries or casualties and no material financial loss to the Company. Fire was 
controlled within time. We are currently assessing the full extent of the damage and its potential impact 
on production. We will keep the Exchange informed on any further material developments, if any. 
 
We would like to state that the assets at the affected plant is adequately insured, and we are in the 
process of filing insurance claims to cover the damage and losses.
