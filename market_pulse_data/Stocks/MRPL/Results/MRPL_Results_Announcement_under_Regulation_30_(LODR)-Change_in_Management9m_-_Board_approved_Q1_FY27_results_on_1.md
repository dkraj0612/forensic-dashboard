### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Mangalore Refinery and Petrochemicals Limited (MRPL) reported a strong financial turnaround for Q1 FY2027, achieving significant year-over-year revenue growth and a net profit of ₹945.68 crore, largely boosted by an exceptional income of ₹471.76 crore from petroleum product price revisions and the adoption of a lower corporate tax rate. The company also improved its debt-to-equity ratio and reduced total borrowings. A material red flag, however, is the ongoing absence of the requisite number of Independent Directors, resulting in a governance deficiency where the Board currently performs Audit Committee functions.
