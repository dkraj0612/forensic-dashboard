Business Update Release 
Silver Touch Technologies Wins AI-Native DPR Appraisal Platform Order from RITES Limited, a 
Navratna Government Enterprise 
Company to build India's first self-hosted, multi-model AI platform for infrastructure project appraisal 
Establishes Silver Touch's leadership in AI for national infrastructure governance 
Ahmedabad, 07th July, 2026: Silver Touch Technologies Limited, a leading digital transformation and IT services 
company, has been awarded a significant order by RITES Limited (A Government of India Navratna Enterprise under 
the Ministry of Railways) for the Design, Development, and Implementation of PARAKH — an AI-Based Detailed 
Project Report (DPR) Appraisal & Intelligence Platform. 
"Silver Touch becomes one of the first Indian IT companies to deploy a fully self-hosted, multi-model AI platform for 
a Government of India Navratna enterprise — marking its transition from an IT services company to an AI-native 
platform company." 
"With PARAKH, Silver Touch now owns a referenceable, production-grade AI platform capability in India's ₹111 lakh 
crore infrastructure sector — a domain moat that deepens with every DPR the platform processes." 
"Silver Touch to power RITES' AI brain for appraising India's largest infrastructure projects — railways, highways, 
bridges, ports, airports, metro, and tunnels." 
Building India's First AI-Native Infrastructure Appraisal Platform 
Under this engagement, Silver Touch will architect and deploy a purpose-built AI platform — not a conventional 
software application with AI features added as an overlay, but a ground-up AI-native system designed to transform 
how India's largest infrastructure consultancy appraises Detailed Project Reports. The platform incorporates: 
● Three Self-Hosted Large Language Models (Llama 3.1 70B, Mistral 7B, Qwen 2.5 7B) deployed on NVIDIA A100 
GPU infrastructure within RITES' secure data centre — with zero external data egress 
● 500+ Codified Engineering Validation Rules drawn from IRC, RDSO, IS, and MORTH standards — delivering 
deterministic, auditable checks that complement AI intelligence 
● Hybrid Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) combining semantic vector search with keyword search across 
1,000+ historical DPRs for grounded, citation-backed appraisal 
● Four-Layer Hallucination Prevention Framework ensuring every AI-generated insight is citation-mandatory, 
source-verified, confidence-scored, and human-approved 
● Document Intelligence Pipeline processing DPRs of 500 to 5,000 pages with OCR, table extraction, and 
engineering entity recognition 
● GIS Integration with PM GatiShakti National Master Plan, BIM/IFC interoperability, and geospatial analytics 
● Multi-Sector Knowledge Graph linking engineering parameters, standards, and validation rules across eight 
infrastructure domains 
● Enterprise-Grade Security with Keycloak IAM, AES-256 encryption, mTLS service mesh, and OWASP Top 10 + 
LLM-specific security compliance

AI Leadership, Not AI Integration: Unlike conventional IT projects where AI is an add-on feature, PARAKH is an AI-
native platform where artificial intelligence is the core value proposition. The deployment of three self-hosted open-
source LLMs with a purpose-built inference pipeline positions Silver Touch among the very few Indian IT companies to 
deliver a fully self-hosted, multi-model AI platform for a Government of India Navratna enterprise. This is not a pilot 
or a proof-of-concept — it is a production-grade, mission-critical AI system. 
Domain Moat in AI for Infrastructure: The 500+ codified engineering rules, sector-specific ontologies, and historical 
DPR knowledge base constitute proprietary domain intelligence that deepens with every DPR processed. This creates 
a compounding competitive advantage — the more DPRs the platform appraises, the more accurate its benchmarking, 
anomaly detection, and validation become. 
Commenting on the order, Mr. Minesh V Doshi, Executive Director of Silver Touch Technologies Limited, said: 
"We are honoured to partner with RITES Limited in building what we believe is India's most advanced AI-native 
infrastructure intelligence platform. PARAKH is not just a technology project — it is a testament to Silver Touch's 
capability to engineer genuine, production-grade AI systems for mission-critical national applications. The platform 
deploys self-hosted Large Language Models, a 500-rule engineering validation engine, and a four-layer hallucination 
prevention system — all within RITES' secure infrastructure with zero external data dependency. We believe this 
establishes Silver Touch as a pioneer in AI for infrastructure governance, and we look forward to extending this 
capability across India's infrastructure ecosystem." 
The order has been secured through the Government e-Marketplace (GeM) portal following a competitive Quality and 
Cost-Based Selection (QCBS) evaluation, underscoring the Company's technical and commercial competitiveness.
