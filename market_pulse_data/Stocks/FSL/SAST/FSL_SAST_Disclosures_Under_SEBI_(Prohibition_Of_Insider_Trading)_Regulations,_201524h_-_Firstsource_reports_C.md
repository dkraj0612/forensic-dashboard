### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Firstsource Solutions Ltd. reported that its Chief Financial Officer, Mr. Dinesh Jain, inadvertently violated insider trading regulations by executing a contra trade (selling and buying the same shares) during a trading window closure. The company has issued a cautionary notice and prohibited the CFO from trading company shares for six months, noting it was a first-time procedural non-compliance not material to the company's equity capital. However, a senior executive's violation of such fundamental trading rules raises a red flag regarding the robustness of internal compliance and oversight.
