Proceedings of the 23rd Annual General Meeting of the Bank 
 
1. The 23rd Annual General Meeting (AGM) of the Shareholders of Bank of Maharashtra 
(the Bank) was held on Tuesday, 30th June 2026 at 11.00 a.m. through Video 
Conference / Other Audio-Visual Means in accordance with the Notice dated 6th June 
2026 and in compliance with applicable MCA/ SEBI / GOI Guidelines.  
 
2. The meeting was attended by 102 Members including 01 Authorised Representative 
of Government of India.  
 
3. The Representatives of Statutory Central Auditors and Secretarial Auditors of Bank 
also attended the meeting. 
 
4. Pursuant to Regulation 59 of Bank of Maharashtra (Shares and Meetings) 
Regulations, 2004, in the absence of Non-Executive Chairman, Shri Nidhu Saxena, 
Managing Director & CEO, chaired the meeting and welcomed the Shareholders of 
the Bank present at the meeting.  
 
5. After confirmation of requisite quorum by Shri Vishal Sethia, Company Secretary, the 
Chairman called the Meeting in order in accordance with Regulation 58 of the Bank 
of Maharashtra (Shares and Meetings) Regulations, 2004. 
 
6. The Chairman informed the Shareholders that in accordance with the circulars 
issued by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs and SEBI, the Annual General Meeting of 
Bank is being conducted through Video Conference / Other Audio-Visual Means. 
 
7. The Chairman then introduced the Directors of the Bank and the Authorized 
Representative of Government of India present in the meeting to the Shareholders 
and welcomed them to the meeting. 
 
8. The Chairman informed that in compliance with the extant guidelines, the Bank has 
sent copy of AGM Notice and Annual Report to shareholders through email and also 
published the Notice of this Annual General Meeting in newspapers. 
 
9. Company Secretary, informed that the Notice dated 6th June, 2026 convening this 
AGM and the link to the Annual Report was e-mailed to the shareholders of the Bank 
whose email-id were registered with the Bank / depositories. 
 
10. Company Secretary further informed that notice regarding the Annual General 
Meeting intimating the mailing of notice and the e-voting instructions was published in 
newspapers i.e. Financial Express (English daily), Loksatta (Marathi daily) and 
Jansatta (Hindi daily) on 6th June 2026. The Notice of this Meeting and Annual 
Report 2025-26 has been hosted on the website of the Bank, NSE and BSE.

11. Thereafter, the Chairman with the consent of the shareholders attending the Meeting, 
notice convening the 23rd Annual General Meeting together with the Explanatory 
Statement, the Audited Financial Statements for the year ended 31st March 2026 
along with the Directors’ Report and Auditors Report was taken as read. 
 
12. The Chairman then highlighted the performance and achievements of the Bank for 
Financial year 2025-26, along with key initiatives and way forward, to the 
Shareholders. 
 
13. The Company Secretary informed the Shareholders that as per SEBI (LODR) 
Regulations, 2015 and Companies (Management and Administration) Rules, 2014 as 
amended, remote e-voting facility was made available to the Shareholders of Bank. 
 
He further informed that the Bank has appointed M/s. S.N. Ananthasubramanian & 
Co., Company Secretaries as Scrutinizer for the remote e-voting and e-voting to be 
conducted at the AGM.  
 
14. The Chairman then took up the agenda items one by one.  
 
Agenda Item No.1 
 
To discuss, approve and adopt the Balance Sheet of the Bank as on 31st March, 
2026, and the Profit and Loss Account for the year ended on that date, the report of 
the Board of Directors on the working and activities of the Bank for the period 
covered by the accounts and the Auditor’s report on the Balance sheet and 
accounts. 
 
Agenda Item No.2 
 
To declare dividend on the Equity Shares of the Bank for the FY 2025-26  
Board in its meeting dated 20th April, 2026 has recommended for dividend of Rs. 
1.20/- per share for the FY 2025-26. This is in addition to the interim dividend of Rs. 
1.00/- per equity share declared on 13th January, 2026 and paid during the financial 
year. 
 
Agenda Item No.3 
 
To approve the appointment of Shri Sushanta Kumar Mohanty as Executive Director 
 
Agenda Item No.4 
 
To consider raising of Equity Capital aggregate upto Rs.7,500/- crore through various 
modes as mentioned in the Notice.
