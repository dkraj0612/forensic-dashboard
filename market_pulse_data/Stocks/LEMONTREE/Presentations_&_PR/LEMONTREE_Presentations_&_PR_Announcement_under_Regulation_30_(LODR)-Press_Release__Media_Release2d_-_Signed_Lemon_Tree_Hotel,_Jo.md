LEMON TREE HOTELS EXPANDS NORTHEAST FOOTPRINT 
WITH THE SIGNING OF LEMON TREE HOTEL, JORHAT  
Marks the brand’s entry into Assam’s cultural and tea capital as the group deepens 
its commitment to Northeast India 
New Delhi, 14th July, 2026: Lemon Tree Hotels Limited, one of India’s leading 
hospitality companies, today announced the signing of Lemon Tree Hotel, Jorhat 
in Assam. The property will be managed by Carnation Hotels Private Limited, a wholly 
owned subsidiary of Lemon Tree Hotels Limited. 
This signing marks the group’s entry into Jorhat, Assam’s cultural capital, and 
expands its footprint in the state to seven properties which includes one operational 
hotel and six under development.  
Widely recognised as the 'Tea Capital of the World', Jorhat is home to over 135 tea 
gardens, including renowned estates such as Cinnamora and Gatoonga, as well as 
the prestigious Tocklai Tea Research Institute. The city also serves as the primary 
gateway to Majuli, the world's largest river island and a vibrant centre of Vaishnavite 
culture. Together, its rich heritage, thriving tea industry and growing tourism appeal 
have positioned Jorhat as one of Northeast India's emerging travel destinations. 
The upcoming hotel will feature 70 well-appointed rooms, a restaurant, banquet hall, 
meeting room, swimming pool, spa, and fitness centre. Designed as a full-service 
offering, the hotel will cater to a diverse mix of business travellers, tea industry 
professionals, leisure guests and visitors exploring the region's rich cultural and 
natural attractions. 
Commenting on the signing, Mr. Vishvapreet Singh Cheema, President, Lemon 
Tree Hotels Ltd., said, “We are delighted to bring the Lemon Tree brand to Jorhat, 
a city that beautifully combines Assam's rich cultural heritage with its economic 
significance. Our expansion strategy is focused on strengthening our presence across 
high-growth regional markets where demand for quality branded hospitality is 
increasing. This signing reinforces our commitment to Northeast India while enabling 
us to serve a growing mix of business and leisure travellers with the signature comfort 
and warm hospitality that Lemon Tree Hotels is known for.” 
The hotel enjoys convenient connectivity to major transport hubs. Jorhat Airport is 
approximately 6 km away, while Jorhat Railway Station is around 2.5 km from the 
property. The hotel is easily accessible by road from key destinations across Assam 
and the Northeast.
