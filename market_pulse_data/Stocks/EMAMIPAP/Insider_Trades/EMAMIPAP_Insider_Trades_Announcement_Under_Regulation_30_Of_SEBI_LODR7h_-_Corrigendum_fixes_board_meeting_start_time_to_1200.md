Meeting was mentioned as 12:30 p.m. instead of 12:00 noon in the aforesaid submission.

It may also be noted that the commencement time of the Board Meeting was correctly mentioned as 
12:00 noon in the XBRL filing submitted by the Company on 22nd July, 2026, pertaining to the outcome 
of the Board Meeting.
