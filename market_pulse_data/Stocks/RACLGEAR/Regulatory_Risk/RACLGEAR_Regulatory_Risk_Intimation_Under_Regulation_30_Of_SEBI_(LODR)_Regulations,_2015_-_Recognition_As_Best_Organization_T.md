Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, 
we are pleased to inform you that RACL Geartech Limited has been recognized as one of the “Best 
Organizations to Work 2026” by ET Edge, The Times Group. The recognition was conferred upon a select 
group of 40 organizations across India, and RACL Geartech Limited is proud to be among the recipients of this 
prestigious honour. 
 
The award was presented during a private award ceremony by Mr. Anil Swarup, former Secretary to the 
Government of India and a distinguished public policy expert. The recognition acknowledges the Company's 
commitment towards fostering a people-centric work culture, employee engagement, leadership 
development, workplace excellence, and sustainable organizational growth. 
 
This achievement reflects the collective efforts of our employees and leadership team in creating an 
environment that promotes innovation, collaboration, inclusivity, and continuous learning. 
 
The above information is being submitted for the information of the stakeholders of the Company. Kindly take 
the same on record. Photographs from the award ceremony are enclosed herewith for reference.
