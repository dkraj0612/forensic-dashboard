### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> **Executive Summary:**  Apollo Hospitals Enterprise Limited (AHEL) has secured overwhelming approval from its equity shareholders, secured, and unsecured creditors for a comprehensive composite scheme of arrangement dated June 24, 2026. This strategic restructuring involves demerging an identified business unit from AHEL into a newly formed, separately listed entity named Apollo Healthco Limited, along with the amalgamation of two other companies. The material impact is a fundamental alteration of AHEL's corporate structure and asset base, creating a new publicly traded entity for shareholders, which received broad stakeholder endorsement despite a specific "upside share arrangement" detailed within the scheme.
