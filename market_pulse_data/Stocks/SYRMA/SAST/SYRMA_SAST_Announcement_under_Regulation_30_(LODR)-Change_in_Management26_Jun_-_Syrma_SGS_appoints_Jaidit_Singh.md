Dear Sir/ Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, 
we hereby inform you that the Board of Directors of the Company at its meeting held today i.e. June 26, 2026 
on recommendation of the Nomination and Remuneration Committee has  appointed  Mr. Jaidit Singh Brar, 
as the Chief Executive Officer of the Company. Mr. Satendra Singh, Chief Executive Officer, of the Company is 
moving to a new role and designated as Senor Operating Director. 
 
Mr. Jaidit Singh Brar, is an MBA-IIM Calcutta and brings with him over 25 years of distinguished experience 
in industry/consultancy. Prior to joining Syrma SGS, he served as senior partner at McKinsey & Company.
