PRESS RELEASE 
Karnataka

Sankaranarayanan as its New Executive Director 
Leading private sector lender - Karnataka Bank, is pleased to announce the joining of 
Mrs. Biji Sreekrishnavilas Sankaranarayanan as the new Executive Director of the 
Bank. The appointment is effective from 15th July 2026. 
Mrs. Biji S S is a Strategic Banking Leader with 31 years of end-to-end experience in 
South Indian bank across corporate banking, retail & branch banking, liabilities, 
secured assets, transformation and digital modernization. 
Mrs. Biji S S has a proven track-record in steering nationwide banking networks as 
independent profit centres, scaling corporate banking verticals, strengthening risk 
frameworks, improving asset quality, enhancing customer acquisition models and 
architecting digital/ operational turnaround journeys.
