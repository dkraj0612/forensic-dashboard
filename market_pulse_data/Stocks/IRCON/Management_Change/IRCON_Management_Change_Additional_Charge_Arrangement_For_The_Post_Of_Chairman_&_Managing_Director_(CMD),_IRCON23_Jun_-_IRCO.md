### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> IRCON International Ltd. announced an interim leadership change, as the current CMD, Shri Hari Mohan Gupta, will superannuate on June 30, 2026. Shri Saleem Ahmad, currently CMD of Rail Vikas Nigam Ltd. (RVNL), will assume additional charge for up to one year, ensuring continuity but creating a dual-hatted role across two major PSUs. This interim appointment suggests a delay in finding a permanent leader, which could potentially impact IRCON's dedicated strategic focus due to the temporary nature and divided attention of the CMD.
