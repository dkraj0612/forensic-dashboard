Credit Ratings 
 
Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
In terms of Regulation 30, 51,55 read with Schedule III of the Securities and 
Exchange Board of India (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015 (SEBI Listing Regulations), we wish to inform you that 
CARE Ratings Limited, a Credit Rating Agency, has upgraded its ratings from 
“CARE A; Stable” to “CARE A+; Positive” for the Non-Convertible Bonds 
issued by the Company amounting to upto Rs. 1500 crores only. 
 
Further, CARE Ratings Limited has upgraded its ratings from “CARE A; 
Stable” to “CARE A+; Positive” for the Long Term Bank Facilities availed/to 
be availed by the Company and has upgraded its rating from “CARE A1” to 
“CARE A1+” for the Short Term bank facilities availed by the Company.  
 
The copy of the letter issued by Care Ratings Limited is available on the 
website of the Company.
