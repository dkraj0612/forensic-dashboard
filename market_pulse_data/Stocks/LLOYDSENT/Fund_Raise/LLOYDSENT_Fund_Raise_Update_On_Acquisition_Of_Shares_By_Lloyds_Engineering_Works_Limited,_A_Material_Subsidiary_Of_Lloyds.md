Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
With reference to our earlier intimation dated 12th December, 2025, we had informed you that, Lloyds 
Engineering Works Limited, a material subsidiary of Lloyds Enterprises Limited (“the Company”), had 
incorporated Lloyds Advance Defence Systems Limited (“LADSL”) as its Wholly Owned Subsidiary in 
India on 11th December, 2025, thereby making LADSL a step-down subsidiary of the Company. 
 
Further, we wish to inform you that, LADSL at its Extraordinary General Meeting held on 24th June, 
2026, approved a private placement of equity shares to its holding company and two other investors 
by way of a special resolution. Pursuant thereto, the Board of Directors of LADSL, at its meeting held 
on 29th June, 2026, approved the allotment of such equity shares. Consequent to this allotment, the 
Lloyds Engineering Works Limited stake has been diluted by 15%, changing the status of LADSL from a 
wholly-owned subsidiary to a subsidiary of Lloyds Engineering Works Limited with an 85% stake. 
 
The disclosure as required under Regulation 30 of Securities and Exchange Board of India (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 read with recent Securities and Exchange 
Board of India Circulars is enclosed as Annexure I. 
 
The above information is also being made available on Company’s website at www.lloydsenterprises.in 
 
We request you to kindly take a note of the above.
