Subject: Intimation under Regulation 30 of SEBI (Listing Obligations and 
Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 -Change in Senior Management  
 
Dear Sir/Ma’am, 
 
Pursuant to the provisions of Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations & 
Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015 (“SEBI Listing Regulations”) we wish to 
inform you that the Board of Directors today on the recommendations of the 
Nomination and Remuneration Committee approved the below changes in the 
Senior Management Personnel (“SMP”) of the Company: 
1) Mr. Jijesh Gopalan currently Regional Director, Sales will move to the new role 
of Head-Service designated as Vice President-Service with effect from 15th July, 
2026 and will report to the Managing Director of Whirlpool of India Limited. His 
brief profile pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI Listing Regulations read with 
Master Circular having reference no. SEBI/HO/CFD/PoD2/CIR/P/0155 dated 11th 
November, 2024, is given below: 
 
Mr. Jijesh Gopalan has more than 21 years of experience in Sales and 
Commercial Management. He holds a Masters Degree in Business 
Administration and a Bachelors Degree in Engineering. He joined Whirlpool 
in 2016. During his decadal tenure at Whirlpool, he has held various roles 
across sales in different categories and has been instrumental in increasing 
the market share of the regions he worked in as Regional Head. Prior to 
joining Whirlpool, Mr. Gopalan has also worked with PepsiCo.
