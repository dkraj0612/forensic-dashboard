AABL Unveils KULTUR, a Contemporary HARD SELTZER for India's New Generation 
 
India, June 26, 2026: Associated Alcohols & Breweries Limited (AABL) (BSE: 507526 | NSE: 
ASALCBR), the flagship alco-beverage company of the Associated Kedia Group, has launched 
KULTUR Hard Seltzer in Madhya Pradesh, marking its entry into India's fast-growing hard 
seltzer and ready-to-drink (RTD) beverage segment. The launch aligns with the company's 
premiumisation strategy and focus on meeting the evolving preferences of Gen Z and millennial 
consumers. Driven by rising demand for convenient, flavourful, and lighter alcoholic beverages, 
India's hard seltzer market is expected to grow at over 10% CAGR, while the broader RTD 
segment is projected to expand at nearly 18% CAGR in the coming years. 
 
Madhya Pradesh has emerged as a promising market for premium alcoholic beverages due to 
increasing urbanisation, higher disposable incomes, expanding nightlife, and a growing base of 
young, aspirational consumers in cities such as Indore, Bhopal, Gwalior, and Jabalpur. KULTUR 
Hard Seltzer is available in sleek 330 ml cans across five flavours— Cranberry (bold and tangy 
with a crisp finish), Watermelon Mint (light, juicy, and refreshingly smooth), Mai Tai (tropical 
and vibrant with fruity citrus notes), Lemon (crisp, zesty, and easy-drinking), and Juniper Triple 
Sec (a more elevated citrus-botanical ﬂavour profile) - offering a refreshing and contemporary 
drinking experience designed for social occasions. AABL stated that the launch reflects its 
commitment to innovation and building a diversified premium portfolio. The company plans to 
distribute KULTUR through premium retail outlets and HoReCa channels, including hotels, 
restaurants, cafés, bars, and lounges, to enhance product visibility and encourage consumer 
trials. With its modern branding, bold flavours, and convenient format, KULTUR aims to 
establish itself as a distinctive lifestyle beverage for India's new generation of consumers. 
 
Management Comments on the launch; 
"Kultur is more than just a beverage; it represents a new generation of drinking culture in India. 
Today's consumers are increasingly seeking modern, experience-led brands that align with their 
evolving lifestyles and preferences. With Kultur, we are introducing a vibrant hard seltzer that 
brings together refreshing ﬂavours, contemporary design, and a distinctive lifestyle appeal. 
Madhya Pradesh is an important market for us, driven by a growing base of young, aspirational 
consumers and increasing demand for premium and innovative beverage choices. We believe 
the state offers strong potential for emerging categories such as hard seltzers, making it an 
ideal market for Kultur's debut. This launch reﬂects our commitment to innovation and our 
continued focus on building a diversified, premium portfolio that resonates with the preferences 
of new-age consumers across India."

About Associated Alcohols & Breweries Limited 
 
Established in 1989, Associated Alcohols and Breweries Ltd. (AABL) operates a cutting-edge 
integrated alcoholic beverage manufacturing facility near Indore, Madhya Pradesh. AABL's 
operations encompass the manufacturing and supply of Extra Neutral Alcohol (ENA),  
The company is involved in the sales of Indian Made Foreign Liquor (IMFL), Indian Made Indian 
Liquor (IMIL), and Extra Neutral Alcohol (ENA). Led by an experienced and capable leadership 
team and guided by an accomplished Board of Directors, AABL is committed to being a socially 
responsible corporate citizen with a strong emphasis on corporate governance and internal 
controls. The company is publicly traded, listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE: 507526) 
and the National Stock Exchange (NSE: ASALCBR) in India.
