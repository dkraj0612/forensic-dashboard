Subject: Disclosure under Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2015 – Disruption of Operations due to Work Stoppage 
 
Dear Sir/Madam, 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 read with Schedule III of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2015 2015, as amended (“SEBI LODR Regulations”) and the applicable 
SEBI Master Circular, we wish to inform you that certain workmen of one department of the Company's 
Dhamni Weaving Division have, with effect from 29 June 2026, abstained from reporting for duty in 
connection with the ongoing wage settlement process. 
 
The Company has, throughout the wage settlement process, engaged in continuous discussions with the 
workmen with a view to arriving at an amicable resolution. A mutually negotiated understanding was 
reached and a draft settlement was circulated for execution. However, certain workmen subsequently 
assembled at the factory gate but did not enter the factory premises or report for duty.  
 
The matter is presently under conciliation before the Assistant Labour Officer. Additionally, the 
Company has initiated appropriate legal and administrative proceedings before the competent 
authorities and is in continuous coordination with the labour authorities, local police authorities and 
other concerned authorities to maintain peace and facilitate restoration of normalcy.
