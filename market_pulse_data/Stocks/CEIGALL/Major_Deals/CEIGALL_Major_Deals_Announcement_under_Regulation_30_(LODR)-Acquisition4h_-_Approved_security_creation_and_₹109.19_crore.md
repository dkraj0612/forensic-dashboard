### 🤖 AI Intelligence Summary
> Ceigall India Limited has approved substantial financial commitments for its wholly-owned subsidiary, Velgaon Power Transmission Limited, a project-specific vehicle for establishing a new 400/220 kV substation. The company plans to infuse ₹109.19 crore into the subsidiary via equity and loans, while also creating security for a separate rupee term loan facility. A key red flag is the significant capital outlay for a greenfield project through a subsidiary with no operational history or turnover, increasing Ceigall India Limited's financial exposure to a nascent venture in the power transmission sector.
