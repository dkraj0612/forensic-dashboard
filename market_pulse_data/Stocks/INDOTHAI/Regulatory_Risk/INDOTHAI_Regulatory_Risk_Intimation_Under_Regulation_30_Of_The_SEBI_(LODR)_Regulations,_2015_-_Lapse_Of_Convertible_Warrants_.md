Allotment Committee through its power entrusted by the Board of Directors, at its meeting held today, 14th July, 2026, 
approved the forfeiture of the initial 25% of the amount received on the said warrants and the Warrants have accordingly 
lapsed, and stand cancelled pursuant to Regulation 169(3) of Chapter V of the SEBI (Issue of Capital and Disclosure 
Requirements) Regulations, 2018.
