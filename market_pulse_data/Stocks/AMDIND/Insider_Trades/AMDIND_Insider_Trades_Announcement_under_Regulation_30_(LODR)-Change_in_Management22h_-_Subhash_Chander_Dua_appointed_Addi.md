> AMD Industries Limited announced the appointment of Mr. Subhash Chander Dua as a Non-Executive Independent Director for a one-year term, effective July 7, 2026, bringing over 40 years of experience in the Accounts and Packaging industry to the board. This appointment aims to enhance board expertise and independence. A significant red flag is present in the regulatory filing, where the detailed "Term of Appointment" section mistakenly identifies "Ms. Laxmi Devi Aere" as the appointee instead of Mr. Subhash Chander Dua, indicating a material error and lack of diligence in the company's disclosure.
