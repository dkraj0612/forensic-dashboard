Brief profile (in case of appointment):  
Mr. Ashish brings with him over 25 years of extensive experience spanning P&L 
management, business transformation, sales, category development, operations, go-to-
market strategy, supply chain and omnichannel development across Indian and international 
markets. Prior to joining Havells, he served as Executive Vice President and Chief Operating 
Officer – North and East Regions at Diageo India, where he led large and diverse businesses, 
driving growth, margin expansion, innovation and commercial transformation.  
 
His professional journey also includes leadership roles at Reckitt, Marico, PepsiCo India and 
ITC Agrotech where he built considerable experience across personal care, healthcare, 
household products, foods and beverages. 
 
Mr. Ashish holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Rural Marketing from Narsee Monjee Institute 
of Management Studies, Mumbai, and a Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) degree from 
Osmania University, Hyderabad.
