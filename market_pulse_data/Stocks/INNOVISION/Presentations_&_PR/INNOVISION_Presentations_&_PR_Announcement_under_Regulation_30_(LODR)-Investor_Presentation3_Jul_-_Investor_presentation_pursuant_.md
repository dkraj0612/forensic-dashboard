the BSE and NSE and revenue growing to ₹981 Cr, while EBITDA and 
PAT increased to ₹55 Cr and ₹36 Cr, respectively.

Drone Manufacturing and aerial intelligence platform.

3
Backed by strong government relationships and over 14,000 deployed

defence, infrastructure and aerial intelligence.

security services company into a diversified infrastructure services platform with

enabled solutions.

serving enterprises, government bodies and infrastructure operators through 
its execution-driven platform.
