Sub:  Intimation and update regarding completion of acquisition of entire shareholding in Hintastica 
Private Limited (“HPL”) by Hindware Home Innovation Limited (“Company”) 
Ref:  Disclosure dated 3rd June, 2026 and 4th June, 2026 pursuant to Regulation 30(2) of SEBI (Listing 
Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as amended from time to time 
 
With reference to the captioned subject and our earlier disclosure dated 3rd June, 2026 and 4th June, 2026, pursuant 
to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) Regulations, 2015, as amended 
(“SEBI LODR Regulations”), we are hereby informing the exchanges that Hindware Home Innovation Limited 
(“Company”), has acquired 5,48,787 equity shares of Hintastica Private Limited (“HPL”) held by Atlantic 
Société Française de Développement Thermique (“Atlantic”) on 30 June, 2026, for an aggregate purchase 
consideration of INR 2,78,83,867.47/-. 
Consequent to the aforesaid acquisition, HPL has ceased to be a joint venture of the Company and Atlantic and 
has become a wholly owned subsidiary of the Company. 
Further, the existing joint venture arrangements between the Company and Atlantic in relation to HPL, including 
the shareholders’ agreement and all ancillary agreements entered into in connection therewith, have been 
terminated and no longer have any force or effect. 
The Company intends to continue and expand the water heater business through HPL, under the “hindware” 
brand. 
The disclosures required under SEBI LODR Regulations, in line with SEBI Master Circular No. 
HO/49/14/14(7)2025-CFD-POD2/I/3762/2026 dated 30th January, 2026 and other applicable circulars, have 
already been duly submitted as Annexure 1, Annexure 2 and Annexure 3 to our disclosure dated 3rd June, 2026. 
This disclosure shall also be available on the Company’s website at www.hindwarehomes.com.  
We request you to kindly take the above on record and disseminate the same on your website. 
 
This is for your information and records.
