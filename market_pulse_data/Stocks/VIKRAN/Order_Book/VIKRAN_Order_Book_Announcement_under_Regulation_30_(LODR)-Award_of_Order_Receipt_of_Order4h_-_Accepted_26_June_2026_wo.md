3517.98 Crores for solar plant development of 969 MW 
 
Pursuant to Regulation 30 of the SEBI (Listing Obligations and Disclosure Requirements) 
Regulations, 2015 ("SEBI LODR Regulations"), we wish to inform you that Vikran Engineering 
Limited ("the Company") has accepted a Work Order dated 26th June 2026 from NOPL Solar 
Projects Private Limited ("NOPL") for Designing, Engineering, Procurement, Supply, 
Erection, Testing and Commissioning on Turnkey EPC Basis (including Supply of Solar PV 
Modules and Inverters) for development of a 969 MW AC Solar Power Project at multiple 
locations in the State of Maharashtra, aggregating to INR 3517.98 Crores (Rupees Three 
Thousand Five Hundred Seventeen point Ninety Eight Crores Only), inclusive of GST. (Refer 
Annexure I) 
The Company had earlier accepted a Work Order from Onix Renewable Limited on 23rd  
December 2025 for execution of the EPC works for the 600 MW project for Rs. INR 2035.26 
Crores. Against the said earlier arrangement, work to the extent of around Rs. 388.67 Crores 
had been tentatively executed and the balance order value of around Rs. 1,893.26 Crores 
stands mutually cancelled pursuant to the revised contractual arrangement.  
Pursuant to the acquisition of 100% equity stake in NOPL by the Company, the project 
execution structure has been realigned and the EPC works for the project will now be executed 
directly under the Work Order awarded by developer (NOPL). Accordingly, the earlier Work 
Order issued by Onix Renewable Limited has been mutually cancelled and replaced by the 
direct Work Order from NOPL for the same project vide letter dated 23rd June 2026 from Onix 
Renewable Limited to NOPL and Company. This change is solely on account of the 
restructuring of contractual arrangements following the acquisition and does not arise due to 
any dispute, default or performance-related issue. (Refer Annexure II) 
The aforesaid change is not expected to have any material adverse impact on the Company's 
operations, financial position or business prospects as the Company continues to execute the 
EPC works for the project under the revised contractual arrangement with NOPL.
