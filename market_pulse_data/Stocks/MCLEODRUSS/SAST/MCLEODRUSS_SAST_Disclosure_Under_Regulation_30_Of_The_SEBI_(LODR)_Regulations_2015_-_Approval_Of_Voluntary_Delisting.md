Regulations 2015 (SEBI LODR, 2015) – Approval of Voluntary Delisting of Equity Shares from 
the Calcutta Stock Exchange 
 
In terms of Regulation 30 of SEBI LODR, 2015 and in continuation of our letter dated 14th August, 2025 
regarding outcome of Board Meeting wherein decision of Board of Directors were taken to file the application 
for delisting of Equity Shares of the Company from The Calcutta Stock Exchange (‘CSE’), we wish to inform 
that CSE has, vide its Notice Ref. No. CSE/LD/DL/18156/2026 dated 16th July, 2026, approved the 
application of voluntary delisting of the equity shares of the Company. 
Accordingly, the equity shares of the Company bearing CSE Scrip Code 23930 / 10023930 stand delisted 
from CSE with effect from 17th July, 2026. 
The equity shares of the Company shall continue to remain listed and traded on BSE Limited (‘BSE’) and 
National Stock Exchange of India Limited (‘NSE’).The aforesaid delisting from CSE shall have no impact 
on the shareholders of the Company whose shares continue to be traded on BSE and NSE. 
A copy of the aforesaid Notice available on the official website of CSE is enclosed herewith for your 
reference. 
This disclosure is also being hosted on Company's Website: www.mcleodrussel.com .
