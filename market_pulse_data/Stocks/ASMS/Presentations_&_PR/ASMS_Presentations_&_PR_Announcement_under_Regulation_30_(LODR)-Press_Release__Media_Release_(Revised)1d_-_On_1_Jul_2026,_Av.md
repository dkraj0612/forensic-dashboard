ANNEXURE-I 
Intellecap and Avio Smart Market Stack Join Hands to Digitally 
Empower Farmer Producer Organisations and Strengthen 
Rural Value Chains 
● Partnership to drive digital transformation, financial inclusion, export readiness and market 
access for Farmer Producer Organisations and Producer Enterprises 
National: Intellecap Advisory Services Private Limited and Avio Smart Market Stack Limited  
(formerly Bartronics) have entered into a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to strengthen 
Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs), Farmer Producer Companies (FPCs), and Producer 
Enterprises (PEs) through technology-led interventions that enhance digital capabilities, improve 
financial inclusion, expand market access, and support export readiness across India's rural value 
chains. 
The collaboration brings together Intellecap's expertise in strengthening rural enterprises and 
producer institutions with Avio Stack’s SMART platform, a unified digital ecosystem designed to 
support farmer organisations through integrated technology, financial services, and market 
enablement solutions, supporting inclusive growth, investment readiness, and scalable agricultural 
transformation across India, Asia, and Africa. 
The partnership aims to accelerate the digital transformation of FPOs/FPCs and PEs by enabling 
operational digitisation, improving access to financial services, creating stronger domestic and 
export market linkages, and supporting the adoption of technologies that enhance traceability, 
productivity, quality management, and supply chain efficiency. 
Under the collaboration, Intellecap will facilitate the identification and onboarding of Farmer 
Producer Organisations and Producer Enterprises onto the AVIO SMART platform while supporting 
the implementation of programmes focused on digital adoption, operational efficiency, financial 
inclusion, and enhanced market access. 
Avio Stack will provide end-to-end digital capabilities through the AVIO SMART platform, enabling 
farmer onboarding, procurement management, inventory tracking, transaction recording, and 
operational monitoring. The company will also provide technical support, user training, 
troubleshooting, and capacity-building initiatives to ensure effective adoption of the platforms for 
procurement, inventory management and market intelligence. Additionally, ASMS will facilitate 
access to banks, financial institutions, fintech partners, and digital lending ecosystems while 
supporting procurement, buyer-seller connectivity, domestic and export market linkages, and 
initiatives focused on branding, packaging, traceability, quality compliance, and certification for 
international markets.

Commenting on the collaboration, Dr Raja Krishna Murthy M, Head – Agri Tech Business, Avio 
Smart Market Stack Limited, said: "Farmer Producer Organisations play a critical role in creating 
sustainable livelihoods for smallholder farmers, building a stronger and more resilient agricultural 
ecosystem. Through our collaboration with Intellecap, we aim to equip FPOs/FPCs and Producer 
Enterprises with the digital infrastructure, financial access, and market connectivity they need to 
operate more efficiently and unlock new growth opportunities. The AVIO SMART platform has been 
designed to simplify operations, improve transparency, and connect producer organisations with 
domestic and global markets. Together, we are committed to enabling technology-driven rural 
transformation that creates long-term value for farmers and strengthens India's agricultural value 
chains." 
The collaboration establishes a framework through which both organisations will jointly support the 
digital, financial, and market capabilities of FPOs/FPCs and Producer Enterprises. While the 
partnership is non-exclusive, it reflects a shared commitment to leveraging technology to build more 
resilient rural enterprises and create sustainable economic opportunities for farming communities 
across India.
