ANNEXURE-I 
Avio Smart Market Stack and StandardWings Partner to Build AI-
Powered Digital Infrastructure for Rural India 
Hyderabad/Nashik, 01st July, 2026: Avio Smart Market Stack Limited (ASMS), a 
technology company building a unified digital operating system for rural India, has signed a 
Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with StandardWings Technologies Pvt. Ltd. to jointly 
explore the development of next-generation digital platforms aimed at transforming India's 
agricultural and rural ecosystem. 
The collaboration brings together ASMS' extensive rural network spanning over 5,000 
villages and access to nearly 4 crore farmers and rural residents, with StandardWings' 
expertise in Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Internet of Things (IoT), 
enterprise software, and digital product development. 
Under the proposed collaboration, the two companies will explore the development of 
scalable digital infrastructure to support sustainable agriculture, climate initiatives, rural 
commerce, financial inclusion, and technology-driven farmer services. 
The collaboration will focus on a wide range of technology initiatives, including AI-powered 
crop advisory systems, yield prediction models, pest detection, weather-based decision 
support systems, digital agri-platforms, IoT-enabled precision farming solutions, smart agri-
store technology, digital marketplace integration, supply chain traceability platforms, ESG 
assessments, digital Measurement, Reporting and Verification (dMRV) systems for carbon 
projects, sensor-based livestock anomaly detection, AI-powered applications, and farmer-
centric digital solutions. 
The companies also intend to jointly develop innovative agri-tech solutions, pilot 
programmes, and proprietary platforms tailored to India's evolving rural landscape, 
supporting the adoption of regenerative farming practices and climate-focused initiatives 
through digital technologies. 
StandardWings Technologies Pvt. Ltd. has successfully delivered 300+ digital transformation 
projects 
for 
150+ 
clients 
across 
agriculture, 
government, 
healthcare, 
logistics, 
manufacturing, and enterprise sectors, backed by over a decade of experience. Its 
agriculture-focused assignments include digital auditing and traceability solutions for Cotton 
Connect, AI-powered reporting and analytics for managing data from over 200,000 farmers, 
farmer evaluation and survey platforms, sustainability monitoring, and end-to-end traceability 
systems for the wine industry. The company also serves organisations such as the 
Maharashtra Government, National Health Mission, and municipal corporations, delivering 
AI, IoT, mobile, web, and cloud-based solutions that improve operational efficiency, 
transparency, and data-driven decision-making.  
Commenting on the collaboration, Dr. Raja Krishna Murthy M, Head – Agri Tech 
Business, Avio Smart Market Stack Limited, said: "India's agricultural sector is 
undergoing a rapid digital transformation, and technology will play a pivotal role in making 
farming more productive, sustainable and resilient. By combining ASMS' deep rural reach 
with StandardWings' technology capabilities, we aim to build scalable digital solutions that 
empower farmers with better advisory services, improved market access, climate resilience 
and data-driven decision-making."

The Memorandum of Understanding reflects the intent of both organisations to explore

About Avio Smart Market Stack Limited (ASMS) 
Avio Smart Market Stack Limited (formerly Bartronics India Limited) is building one of India's largest unified rural operating 
systems by integrating financial inclusion, agriculture, rural commerce, climate solutions, and healthcare to empower farmers 
and underserved communities digitally. The company has a presence across six countries, is listed on the National Stock 
Exchange and Bombay Stock Exchange, and has access to more than 5,000 villages with a network reaching nearly 4 crore 
farmers and rural residents. 
About StandardWings Technologies Pvt. Ltd. 
StandardWings Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a Nashik-based technology company specialising in Artificial Intelligence (AI), 
Machine Learning (ML), Internet of Things (IoT), enterprise software, web and mobile application development, and digital 
transformation solutions. Founded in 2014, the company develops end-to-end technology solutions for enterprises across 
multiple industries, with a focus on building scalable and innovative digital platforms.
