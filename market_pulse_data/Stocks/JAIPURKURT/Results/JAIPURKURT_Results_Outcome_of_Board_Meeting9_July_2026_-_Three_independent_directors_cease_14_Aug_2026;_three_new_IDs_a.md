c) Based upon the recommendation of Nomination and Remuneration Committee and subject to the approval of 
shareholders, Mrs. Megha Khandelwal (DIN: 11689335) be and is hereby appointed as an Independent Director

3. Re-Appointment of Managing Director & Whole Time Director of the Company: 
 
a) Based upon the recommendation of Nomination and Remuneration Committee and subject to the approval of 
shareholders, Mr. Anuj Mundhra (DIN: 05202504) be and is hereby re-appointed as a Managing Director of the 
Company for the period of five years from 15th August, 2026 to 14th August, 2031.
