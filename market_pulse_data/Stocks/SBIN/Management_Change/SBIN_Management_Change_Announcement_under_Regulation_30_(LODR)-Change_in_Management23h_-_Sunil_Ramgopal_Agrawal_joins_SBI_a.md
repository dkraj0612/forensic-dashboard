Profile of Sunil Ramgopal Agrawal 
 
Shri Sunil Ramgopal Agrawal brings over 27 years of experience in financial services 
and strategy. He has been the Chief Financial Officer of Life Insurance Corporation of 
India (LIC) and was responsible for the LIC's finance function. He played a pivotal role 
in strengthening the finance function, driving strategic initiatives, managing Board 
Regulatory interactions and supporting the organization’s growth and transformation 
agenda. 
 
Throughout his career, he has built deep expertise across financial planning, capital 
management, financial reporting, investor relations, governance, and regulatory 
engagement. He brings significant experience in working with Boards, regulators, 
investors, and other key stakeholders. 
 
Shri Agrawal is a Chartered Accountant and B.E. (Computer Science).
