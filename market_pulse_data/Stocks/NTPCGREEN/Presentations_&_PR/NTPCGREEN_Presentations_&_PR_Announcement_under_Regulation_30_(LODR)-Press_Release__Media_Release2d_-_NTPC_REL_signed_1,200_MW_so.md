PRESS RELEASE 
 
NTPC Renewable Energy Limited Signs 1,200 MW Solar Power 
Purchase Agreement with PTC India Limited 
 
New Delhi, 3 July 2026: NTPC Renewable Energy Limited (NTPC REL), a wholly owned 
subsidiary of NTPC Green Energy Limited (NGEL), has signed a Power Purchase Agreement 
(PPA) with PTC India Limited for the sale of 1,200 MW of solar power under a bilateral 
arrangement. 
The PPA was exchanged in the august presence of Dr. Manoj Kumar Jhawar, MD & CEO (PTC) and Dr. 
J. S. Chandok, CEO (ONGPL) and other senior officials of NGEL/NTPC REL and PTC India Limited. 
 
The agreement marks a significant milestone in strengthening collaboration between the 
two organizations to promote clean and sustainable energy in India.
