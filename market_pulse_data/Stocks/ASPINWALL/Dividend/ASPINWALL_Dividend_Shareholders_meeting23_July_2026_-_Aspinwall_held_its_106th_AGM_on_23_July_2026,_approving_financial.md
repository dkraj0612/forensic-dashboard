ORDINARY BUSINESS: 
 
Item No.1: - Adoption of Financial Statements 
To receive, consider and adopt the financial statements of the Company for the year ended

To declare a first and final dividend on the equity shares of the Company for the financial

To appoint a director in place of Mr.Chemprol Raja Raja Varma (DIN: 00031924), who
