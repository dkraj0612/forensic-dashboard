Subject: Resignation from the position of Chief Financial Officer of SBFC Finance Limited 
Dear Sir(s)/Madam, 
I joined SBFC in October 2018 drawn by the opportunity to be part of an entrepreneurial journey, working 
alongside esteemed professionals, investors and a Board united by a long term, strategic vision of building 
SBFC into an institution. 
As Co-founder, it was a professional challenge, to scale a recently acquired business and transform it into 
an institution of enduring value. We began with a team that combined the right skill, confidence, grit, 
patience, mutual respect, and strategic thinking. Together, we steered the organization through 
unforeseen shifts in the economy and financial services landscape, staying focused on opportunity & 
execution while remaining disciplined about risk. 
Life is millions of combinations of probabilities that are interdependent and hard to predict. The best people 
improve the probability of success through strategic thinking, smart choices and hard work. 
After more than seven years with SBFC, I have decided to pursue an entrepreneurial career in 
manufacturing business. I have been blessed to have got extensive experiences in FMCG & Engineering 
business before joining SBFC and I think the next decade in India will be consumer and manufacturing led. 
I therefore formally tender my resignation from the position of Chief Financial Officer, with effect from the 
close of business hours of 15th July 2026. There are no other material reasons for my resignation other 
than those stated hereinabove. 
I am proud of what SBFC has achieved and remain confident it will reach many more milestones in the 
years ahead. I sincerely thank the Board of Directors, the management team, my colleagues, and all 
stakeholders for their guidance, trust, and support throughout my tenure. It has been a privilege to build 
this journey with you. 
I request the Board and its committees to kindly take this resignation on record and initiate the necessary 
statutory compliances as appropriate. 
Yours Sincerely, 
Narayan Barasia 
Chief Financial Officer 
SBFC Finance Limited
